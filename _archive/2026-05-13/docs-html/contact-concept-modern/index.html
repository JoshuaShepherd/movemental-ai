Answers to the
common questions

If any of this is still unclear, just say so in your message — plain English is welcome and we promise not to over-jargon our reply.

How quickly will I hear back?
Within one business day (Mon–Fri, 9a–5p Central). Most replies land within a few hours. If your message arrives on a weekend, expect Monday morning.
Do I need to have our AI strategy figured out first?
No. Most organizations we talk with are in the “we know we need to think about this” stage. A walkthrough is designed to meet you there.
Is the first call free?
Yes. The first 20–30 minute conversation is on us. We use it to understand your context and tell you honestly whether we’re the right partner.
What happens to my information?
It stays with the Movemental team. We don’t sell lists, we don’t add you to a drip, and we don’t pass your details to third parties. You can ask us to delete everything at any time.
Can you speak at our event?
Often yes. Select “Speaking” above and include the date, format, audience, and topic, and we’ll reply with availability and fit.
