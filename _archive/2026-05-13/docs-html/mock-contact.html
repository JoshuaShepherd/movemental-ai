Start a conversation

A first conversation is thirty minutes.

We will tell you in that time whether the AI Stewardship Sequence is a fit for your organization, and whether the timing is. Real people, real email, no chat widget, no automated qualification.

The fields below shape the first reply. None are decisive on their own. Plain language helps more than the right word.

What happens next

A real reply within two business days.

Written by Brad, Alan, or Joshua. Sometimes more than one of us. We will name what we think the work is and what we are not sure about, in plain language.

If we are a fit

A thirty-minute first call.

We will not pitch the engagement. We will ask what is actually on your desk and tell you what we think the next move is, even if the next move is not us.

If we are not a fit

A short, honest answer.

We say so. We will usually point you toward a trusted voice or resource that will fit better. Saying we are not the right team is a signal of taking your work seriously.
