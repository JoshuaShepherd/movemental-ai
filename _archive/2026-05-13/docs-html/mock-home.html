The diagnosis

AI amplifies what is already true for you.

AI does not start from zero—it works with whatever is already there. When the work is fragmented, AI increases output without forcing coherence: integrated work compounds credibly; fragmented work produces slop, faster. The early work is human—judgment, alignment, coherence—before integration can mean anything technical.

Until that foundation exists, nothing else holds at scale. Organizational wisdom first; organizational intelligence second—then systems can serve something real.
