# Book Vision Development Wizard

**Date**: January 2026  
**Purpose**: Comprehensive interview guide to help authors develop a clear vision for their book before making structural decisions  
**Use**: Complete this wizard/interview before selecting a book structure template

---

## How to Use This Wizard

This wizard is designed as a structured interview that helps you clarify your book vision. Work through each section thoughtfully, writing detailed responses. The more specific and detailed your answers, the better the structural template selection will be.

**Process**:
1. Read each question carefully
2. Write detailed responses (aim for 2-5 sentences per question)
3. Be honest about constraints, limitations, and uncertainties
4. Review your answers for consistency
5. Use your completed vision to evaluate structural templates

## Next Steps

1. **Review your completed vision**: Read through all your answers
2. **Identify patterns**: Look for consistent themes, priorities, and approaches
3. **Use the rubric**: Take your vision to the Book Structure Template Evaluation Rubric
4. **Evaluate templates**: Score each template against your vision
5. **Select top 2-3 templates**: Focus on highest-scoring templates
6. **Refine vision if needed**: If no template scores well, refine your vision

## Vision Elements Summary

After completing this wizard, you should have clarity on:

✅ **Core Message**: What you're saying and why it matters  
✅ **Target Audience**: Who you're writing for and their needs  
✅ **Content Type**: What kind of content you're creating  
✅ **Desired Outcomes**: What transformation you want to create  
✅ **Voice & Style**: How you want to communicate  
✅ **Structure Needs**: What structural elements are required  
✅ **Resources Available**: What you have to work with  
✅ **Constraints**: What limitations you face  
✅ **Scope & Scale**: How big and broad this book is  
✅ **Unique Characteristics**: What makes this book distinctive
