### Revenue Structure Overview

#### Launch Phase Pricing (Founders Cohort)
**Entry Investment**: $1,000 flat fee for complete platform setup
- **Includes**: Full React/Next.js platform with AI integration
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks from contract to launch
- **Support**: 90 days of implementation support and optimization
- **Target**: First 20-25 creators building proof of concept

**Ongoing Partnership**: 10% of platform-generated revenue
- **Revenue Sharing**: Creator retains 90% vs. 15% on traditional platforms
- **Transparency**: Monthly reporting with detailed analytics
- **Alignment**: Movemental succeeds only when creators succeed
- **Sustainability**: Recurring revenue model scales with network growth

#### Scale Phase Pricing (Network Expansion)
**Platform Development**: Tiered pricing based on customization needs
- **Standard Platform**: $1,000 (template-based deployment)
- **Enhanced Platform**: $2,500 (moderate customization)
- **Premium Platform**: $5,000 (extensive customization and features)
- **Enterprise Platform**: $10,000+ (full custom development)

**Revenue Sharing Model**: 10% across all tiers
- **Minimum Threshold**: No revenue sharing below $100/month platform earnings
- **Scaling Benefits**: Higher volume creators receive enhanced support and features
- **Network Incentives**: Cross-promotion opportunities reduce effective revenue sharing
