### **The "Invitation-Only" Protection**

**Why This Works:**
- No public applications = no public rejections
- No pricing transparency until invitation = no anchor price problems
- Network vouching = pre-qualification through relationships
- Scarcity creates demand rather than resentment

**When Someone Asks Directly:**
"We're currently growing through invitation and referral within our existing movemental leader network. I'll keep you in mind as we expand, but the best way in is usually through connection with one of our existing members who can speak to your work."

### **The "Quality Drift" Prevention**

**Natural Quality Controls:**
- Existing members have reputation stake in referrals
- Network effects mean poor fits become obvious quickly
- Revenue share model means we want successful, engaged members
- Small cohort size maintains intimacy and accountability

**If Quality Starts to Drift:**
- Slow down invitation velocity
- Focus on member success and engagement
- Use existing high-quality members to recommend better fits
- Remember: 100 great members > 500 mediocre members
