# Movemental Qualification Gate Strategy
## Managing Exclusivity Without Burning Bridges

**Date**: September 25, 2025  
**Purpose**: Strategic framework for managing platform access while preserving relationships and market opportunities  
**Status**: Strategic Analysis & Recommendations

## üéØ **The Core Challenge**

You've identified the fundamental tension in our positioning:

**The Dilemma**: We want to be exclusive enough to maintain movemental credibility and network effects, but not so exclusive that we:
- Damage relationships with quality leaders who don't make the cut
- Burn potential $10K-$100K leads who could pay traditional rates
- Create confusion or offense among good people who don't understand the distinction
- Limit our growth potential artificially

**The Stakes**: This decision shapes whether we become a sustainable business serving 1,000+ leaders or an exclusive club serving 100 elites.

## üß≠ **Big Picture Vision: The Three-Tier Strategy**

### **Tier 1: Movemental Collective (Invitation Only)**
- **Who**: True movemental leaders (90+ points on our rubric)
- **Pricing**: $1,000 + 10% revenue share
- **Access**: Invitation/referral only through existing members
- **Platform**: movemental.ai
- **Value Prop**: "The HBR of movemental publishing" - exclusive network, shared success

### **Tier 2: TrailGuide Professional (Open Application)**
- **Who**: Quality ministry/nonprofit leaders (70-89 points)
- **Pricing**: $10,000-$25,000 one-time + $500/month maintenance
- **Access**: Application process with consultation
- **Platform**: trailguide.ai or similar
- **Value Prop**: Premium digital publishing for mission-driven leaders

### **Tier 3: TrailGuide Standard (Public Market)**
- **Who**: Anyone willing to pay market rates
- **Pricing**: $50,000-$100,000 (competitive with market)
- **Access**: Standard business development process
- **Platform**: Main TrailGuide offering
- **Value Prop**: Custom digital publishing solutions

## üö™ **The Qualification Gate Framework**

### **How We Decide: The 90-Point Threshold**

**Movemental Qualification Criteria:**
1. **Movement Alignment** (25 points): Clear missional/incarnational theology
2. **Network Position** (20 points): Recognized influence in movemental circles
3. **Content Depth** (20 points): Published work demonstrating theological depth
4. **Audience Quality** (15 points): Engaged community, not just large numbers
5. **Platform Readiness** (10 points): Capacity to maximize digital publishing
6. **Revenue Potential** (10 points): Ability to generate platform earnings

**The 90+ Rule**: Only leaders scoring 90+ on our rubric qualify for Movemental Collective pricing and access.

### **How We Communicate It**

**For Movemental Qualified Leaders:**
> "We're building an exclusive collective of movemental thought leaders who are pioneering the future of incarnational publishing. Based on your influence in [specific movement area] and your work on [specific content], we'd love to invite you to join our founding cohort at our launch pricing of $1,000 + 10% revenue share."

**For High-Quality Non-Movemental Leaders:**
> "We love your work in [their area] and would be honored to build you a premium digital publishing platform. While our movemental collective is specifically focused on incarnational movement leaders, we offer comprehensive digital publishing solutions through TrailGuide Professional starting at $10,000. This includes everything the collective gets except the movemental network effects."

**For General Inquiries:**
> "TrailGuide specializes in digital publishing platforms for mission-driven leaders. Our full-service solutions start at $50,000 and include custom development, content strategy, and ongoing optimization. We also have a limited movemental leader collective for qualified movement catalysts."

## ü§ù **Relationship Management Strategy**

### **The Lance Ford Scenario**

**If Lance Ford Applied and Didn't Qualify:**

**Step 1 - Honor & Redirect:**
"Lance, we have tremendous respect for your work in [specific area]. Our movemental collective is specifically focused on leaders working in incarnational/missional movement multiplication - think Alan Hirsch, Brad Brisco level - and has very limited spots. However, we'd love to explore building you a premium platform through TrailGuide Professional, which includes all the same technology and AI capabilities, starting at $10,000."

**Step 2 - Relationship Preservation:**
"Whether or not we work together on a platform, we'd love to stay connected and potentially collaborate on content or speaking opportunities. The movemental space is small and we're all advancing the same kingdom mission."

**Step 3 - Future Door:**
"If your work evolves more toward the incarnational movement space, we'd love to revisit the collective opportunity in the future."

### **The "Regular Pastor" Scenario**

**For Leaders Who Don't Understand the Movement Distinction:**

"Thanks for your interest in Movemental! We specialize in a very specific niche - incarnational movement leaders who are pioneering new models of church and mission. Think of us as focused on the same space as Alan Hirsch's work in 'The Forgotten Ways' or Brad Brisco's church multiplication networks. 

If that resonates with your calling and context, we'd love to explore further. If not, we'd recommend [other digital publishing solutions] that might be a better fit for your ministry focus."

### **The "Burning Good Leads" Problem**

**Solution: The Upgrade Path**

Every "no" to Movemental becomes a "yes" opportunity for TrailGuide:

1. **Capture the Lead**: "While Movemental isn't the right fit, we'd love to build you an amazing platform through TrailGuide."

2. **Premium Positioning**: "This actually gives you access to some features that Movemental doesn't include, like [specific premium features]."

3. **Revenue Optimization**: Convert a potential $1,000 client into a $10,000-$50,000 client.

## üí° **Strategic Recommendations**

### **Recommendation 1: Launch with Dual Brand Architecture**

**Immediate Action:**
- Launch Movemental as exclusive collective for 90+ point leaders
- Simultaneously launch TrailGuide Professional for 70-89 point leaders
- Keep TrailGuide Enterprise for full market-rate clients

**Benefits:**
- No burned relationships - everyone has a path forward
- Revenue optimization - capture value at every level
- Clear positioning - each tier serves distinct needs

### **Recommendation 2: The "Soft Launch" Approach**

**Phase 1 (Next 6 months):**
- Invite only the top 25 movemental leaders personally
- Build through referrals within existing networks (MLC, 100Movements)
- No public qualification process - all invitation based

**Phase 2 (6-12 months):**
- Introduce application process with clear qualification criteria
- Launch TrailGuide Professional as the "public" option
- Position Movemental as "by invitation only"

**Benefits:**
- Reduces early rejection scenarios
- Builds credibility through exclusive early adopters
- Creates natural scarcity and demand

### **Recommendation 3: The "Assessment Consultation" Model**

**Process:**
1. **Initial Consultation**: Free 30-minute assessment call for all inquiries
2. **Platform Recommendation**: Based on assessment, recommend appropriate tier
3. **Value Alignment**: Help them understand why the recommendation serves their goals
4. **Multiple Pathways**: Always provide options, never just rejection

**Sample Flow:**
- Inquiry comes in ‚Üí Assessment call ‚Üí "Based on your goals and context, I think TrailGuide Professional would be perfect for you because [specific reasons]"
- OR: "You're exactly the kind of movemental leader our collective is designed for..."

## üéØ **Bottom Line Recommendation**

**Launch with clear three-tier architecture that gives everyone a pathway forward:**

1. **Movemental Collective**: Exclusive, invitation-only, revolutionary pricing
2. **TrailGuide Professional**: Premium option for quality leaders who don't fit movemental criteria  
3. **TrailGuide Enterprise**: Market-rate option for anyone willing to pay

**This approach:**
- Preserves the exclusivity and network effects you want for Movemental
- Captures revenue from leaders who don't qualify but could pay more
- Maintains relationships by always offering appropriate alternatives
- Creates natural upgrade/referral pathways between tiers
- Positions qualification as "strategic fit" rather than "good enough/not good enough"

**The key insight**: Instead of one qualification gate that says "yes/no," create multiple pathways that say "here's where you belong" - everyone gets served, everyone has dignity, everyone has appropriate value exchange.

---

*The goal isn't to exclude people from value - it's to ensure everyone gets the right value in the right context at the right price point.*
