### **Primary Requirements**

- **AI Integration**: Sophisticated conversation tracking, context management, and personalization for Custom GPT interactions

### **Technical Architecture Principles**
- **Supabase-First Design**: Leveraging PostgreSQL with Row Level Security, real-time subscriptions, and edge functions
- **Performance at Scale**: Optimized for high-volume AI interactions and global distribution
- **Data Security**: Ministry-grade security with role-based access and comprehensive audit trails
- **Analytics Ready**: Built-in analytics capabilities for both business intelligence and ministry impact measurement
- **Integration Friendly**: API-first design supporting third-party integrations and custom applications
- **Future-Proof**: Extensible architecture supporting innovation and feature development
