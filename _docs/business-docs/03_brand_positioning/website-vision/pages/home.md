# Home / Landing Experience

## Purpose
Deliver Movemental’s core narrative, immediately communicate the revolutionary economics, and route qualified visitors toward deeper story, platform proof, and referral-based engagement.

## Primary Audience
- Movement leaders referred by Alan/Brad/partners
- Potential organizational partners (Forge, Wesleyan, Tampa Underground)
- Influential observers/investors validating Movemental’s story

## Primary Outcomes
1. Visitors grasp Movemental’s “Own Your Platform” value prop within 5 seconds.
2. Visitors self-select into “Explore the Network” vs. “Learn the Story” flows.
3. Hero-level social proof (Dave Ferguson launch) and economic calculator convert curiosity into intent.

## Component Map
1. **Animated Hero**
   - Elements: Network mesh background, headline/subhead, primary/secondary CTAs, Dave Ferguson quote/video micro-thumbnail.
   - Data: hero copy, CTA URLs, testimonial snippet.
2. **Me / Us / Now Scrollytelling**
   - Sticky timeline with Alan Hirsch video, Movemental platform reel, convergence infographic.
   - Triggered animations and pull quotes.
3. **Five Whys Value Grid**
   - Five cards with metrics (revenue, reach, AI voice integrity, content capture, infrastructure).
   - Expand/collapse for mobile.
4. **Vision/Mission Capsule**
   - Two-column block with SSOT statements + core values badges.
5. **Economic Liberation Calculator**
   - Inputs: current yearly revenue + pricing model.
   - Outputs: revenue kept with Movemental vs. publisher/custom dev.
6. **Movemental Media Lab Preview**
   - NotebookLM demo video, podcast snippet, CTA to Media Lab page.
7. **Platform Capability Theatre**
   - Carousel/tabs for Content OS, AI Copilot, LMS, Commerce, Analytics.
   - Contains UI imagery, bullet benefits, “See Platform” deep link.
8. **Credibility & Transparency Strip**
   - Stats: 28x–500x amplification, 95%+ voice integrity.
   - Link to AI Principles & Transparency page.
9. **Governance Snapshot**
   - Infographic referencing Movemental Control Plane, provisioning steps, 10% covenant.
10. **Network Showcase Teaser**
    - 6-card grid (Alan, Rob/Brian, Soul of Movement, Forge America, Dave Ferguson, Bree Mills).
    - CTA to `/network`.
11. **Movemental Gate CTA**
    - Summary of rubric, button to referral form.
12. **Invitation Footer**
    - Contact CTA, TrailGuide badge, Powered-by statement, Trust Center link.

## Integrations & Data Requirements
- CMS entries for hero copy, testimonials, video IDs.
- Calculator logic referencing valuation assumptions.
- Media Lab embed (NotebookLM outputs).
- Network card data (name, portrait URL, tagline, CTA).
- Gate CTA linking to secure referrals form.

## Copy / Tone Notes
- Voice: authoritative, invitational, visionary.
- Avoid generic SaaS language; lean into “movement infrastructure” vocabulary.
- Use scarcity cues (“invitation-only, referral-driven”) without overt exclusivity language.
