# Invitation & Contact Page (`/invitation`)

## Purpose
Provide clear pathways for qualified leaders, partners, and media to initiate conversations, while reinforcing Movemental’s referral-first, relationship-driven onboarding model.

## Audience
- Leaders referred by network members.
- Organizations exploring bundled deployments.
- Investors/media requesting briefings.

## Outcomes
1. Visitors understand the referral-first process and expectations.
2. Provide channel-specific contact (leaders, orgs, research, media).
3. Surface readiness checklist before submission.

## Component Map
1. **Intro Copy**
   - “Movemental grows through trusted relationships. Here’s how to connect.”
2. **Referral Checklist**
   - Bullet list aligning to Gate pillars (mission alignment, content cadence, audience stewardship, etc.).
3. **Contact Cards**
   - Leader referral (form link referencing Movemental Gate).
   - Organization/denomination inquiry (bundle consultation).
   - Research/Media contact (press kit download + email).
4. **Process Timeline**
   - Steps: Referral submission → Rubric review → Discovery call → Platform design lab → Launch.
5. **Expectation Setting**
   - Mention $1K + 10% covenant, provisioning timeline, content readiness requirements.
6. **FAQ**
   - “Can I apply without referral?”, “How long does evaluation take?”, “Do you work outside North America?”, etc.
7. **TrailGuide Context**
   - Link for leaders/orgs not yet Movemental-ready to explore TrailGuide services/aitrailmap assessment.

## Data Needs
- Links to referral forms, Calendly/booking flows.
- Email aliases for media/research.
- TrailGuide/aitrailmap CTA links.

## Tone
- Warm, relational, clear about standards.
- Encourage readiness and patience (e.g., “We evaluate cohorts quarterly.”)

## Open Questions
- Confirm SLAs for responses.
- Decide if real-time scheduling should be available or only after rubric review.
