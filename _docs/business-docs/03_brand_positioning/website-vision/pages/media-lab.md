# Movemental Media Lab Page (`/media-lab`)

## Purpose
Showcase the NotebookLM-powered workflow that turns Movemental documentation into AI-hosted podcasts, visual decks, and clip libraries—positioning Movemental as a media accelerator for every leader.

## Audience
- Leaders wanting to repurpose sermons/books into multi-format media.
- Partners evaluating Movemental’s AI production capabilities.
- Prospects comparing Movemental to agencies/consultants.

## Outcomes
1. Visitors understand how a single “doc bundle” powers podcast + deck outputs.
2. Page captures interest in Media Lab sessions (booking form).
3. Provides examples from Dave Ferguson, Soul of Movement, Forge America, Daniel Yang.

## Component Map
1. **Hero Banner**
   - Headline: “Turn your Movemental archive into multi-format media in days.”
   - CTA: `Book a Media Lab Session`.
2. **Workflow Diagram**
   - Steps: Gather docs → NotebookLM ingestion → AI podcast script → AI-hosted audio → Visual deck → Clip kit.
3. **Output Gallery**
   - Embedded audio player (sample AI podcast), video deck preview, downloadable clip kit.
4. **Use Cases**
   - Cards: Dave Ferguson launch brief, Soul of Movement anthology, Send Institute research summary.
5. **Session Packages**
   - Table describing Starter (single doc), Expansion (series), Org bundle (Forge/denomination).
6. **FAQ**
   - Questions about AI voice, editing, turnaround time, pricing (bundled in 10% covenant).
7. **CTA Footer**
   - `Talk with a Media Producer`, `See AI Principles`.

## Data Needs
- NotebookLM outputs (audio/video/deck).
- Copy describing process + deliverables.
- Testimonials from initial participants.

## Tone
- Creative, inspiring, concrete about deliverables.
- Reinforce voice-preservation and human editorial oversight.
