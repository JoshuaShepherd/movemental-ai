# Platform Page (`/platform`)

## Purpose
Demonstrate Movemental’s end-to-end product capabilities—Content OS, AI copilot, LMS, commerce, analytics, governance—and differentiate it from $50K custom dev or extractive publishers.

## Audience
- Prospective leaders/orgs evaluating build vs. Movemental.
- Investors assessing technical depth.
- Existing members discovering features they haven’t activated.

## Outcomes
1. Visitors understand each module and its benefits within minutes.
2. They can self-assess readiness to migrate or expand usage.
3. Page links to demos (Media Lab, Customization Studio) and governance docs.

## Component Map
1. **Hero Banner**
   - Headline: “A complete content OS for movement leaders.”
   - Subhead referencing $1K + 10% model.
   - CTA: `Book a Platform Walkthrough`.
2. **Capability Tabs**
   - Tabs: Content OS, AI Copilot, Learning & Assessments, Commerce & Revenue, Analytics & Insights, Federation & Connectedness.
   - Each tab: screenshot/gif, feature bullets, example workflows.
3. **AI Copilot Deep Dive**
   - Illustrates voice-preserving AI, theological integrity checks, transparency badges.
   - Includes sample prompts and QA flow.
4. **Learning & Assessment Section**
   - Explains LMS, cohort journeys, assessments (APEST, mDNA).
   - Highlights Forge America, Multipliers Journey deployments.
5. **Commerce & Revenue**
   - Shopify integration, Stripe Connect payouts, 10% rev-share automation.
   - Calculator showing payouts vs. publisher deals.
6. **Customization Studio**
   - Video/gif of session artwork upload, theming tokens, layout variations.
7. **Governance & Security**
   - Infographic of Movemental Control Plane, RLS isolation, feature keys, audit logs.
   - Link to Trust Center.
8. **Media Lab & Automation**
   - NotebookLM workflow demonstration, CTA to `/media-lab`.
9. **Integration Map**
   - Diagram showing Supabase MCP, Vercel, Stripe, CMS, analytics warehouse.
10. **CTA Footer**
    - `Talk with a Movemental Architect` + `See Pricing Model` + `Read AI Principles`.
