# Story Page (`/story`)

## Purpose
Deliver the full Me / Us / Now narrative with multimedia depth, reinforcing Movemental’s theological roots, platform promise, and historical timing.

## Audience
- Referred leaders who want fuller context before engaging.
- Media/investor stakeholders validating Movemental’s legitimacy.
- Existing network members seeking shareable narrative content.

## Outcomes
1. Visitors spend >3 minutes consuming the Alan / Movemental / Convergence story.
2. Users access embedded videos/podcasts for deeper resonance.
3. Story page drives secondary CTAs (Media Lab, Network, Gate).

## Component Map
1. **Hero Banner**
   - Split layout with Alan Hirsch portrait, pull quote, “Watch the Movemental Story” CTA.
2. **Chapter Navigation**
   - Sticky side nav or top tabs (Me, Us, Now, Future).
3. **Chapter 1 – Me (Alan Hirsch)**
   - Timeline of key milestones (Apartheid era, Forge founding, global influence).
   - Video embed (Alan describing the moment).
   - Downloadable PDF excerpt or audio clip.
4. **Chapter 2 – Us (Movemental)**
   - Explanation of platform pillars (Platform Ownership, Network Effects, Voice-Preserving AI).
   - Infographics illustrating economic shift.
5. **Chapter 3 – Now (Convergence)**
   - Visual showing AI revolution, credibility crisis, platform ownership movement.
   - Testimonial quotes from Dave Ferguson, Rob Wegner, Deb Hirsch.
6. **Future Vision Timeline**
   - Milestones (10/50/100/500/1,000 leaders) with expected outcomes and valuations.
7. **Movemental Principles Carousel**
   - Highlights from AI commitments, governance, and scenius values.
8. **Media Shelf**
   - Embedded podcast episode (NotebookLM output) + downloadable deck.
9. **CTA Zone**
   - Dual CTAs: `Explore the Network` + `See the Movemental Gate`.

## Data / Content Needs
- Copy from Movemental Strategic Narrative + Valuation Narrative.

- Testimonials (Dave, Forge, Soul of Movement).
