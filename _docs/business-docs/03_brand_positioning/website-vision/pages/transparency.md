# Transparency & Trust Center (`/transparency`)

## Purpose
Provide operational visibility into Movementalâ€™s AI usage, data governance, uptime, security, and platform stewardship commitments.

## Audience
- Movemental leaders and their audiences.
- Organizational partners and denominational compliance teams.
- Investors/media assessing risk mitigation.

## Outcomes
1. Publish real-time or regularly updated trust metrics (uptime, incident history, QA compliance).
2. Explain data handling (Supabase MCP, RLS, audit logs, export policies).
3. Link to AI Principles, governance documentation, and contact routes for security/ethics issues.

## Component Map
1. **Trust Metrics Banner**
   - KPIs: 30-day uptime %, average response time, QA compliance (voice/theology), transparency badge adoption rate.
2. **Security & Infrastructure Section**
   - Overview of Movemental Control Plane, hosting stack, encryption, backups, incident response process.
3. **Data Ownership & Portability**
   - Explanation of leader-owned content/data, export tooling, watermarking, audit trails.
4. **Transparency Badge Analytics**
   - Charts showing usage by badge level across the network.
   - Visitor-facing explanation of what each badge means.
5. **Governance & Legal**
   - Links to Platform Ownership Covenant, Managed Service Agreement highlights, AI ethics policy.
6. **Incident & Update Log**
   - Timeline listing key releases, security updates, any incidents/resolutions.
7. **Contact & Reporting**
   - Buttons for security disclosures, AI ethics concerns, data export requests.
8. **Related Links**
   - `/ai-principles`, `/platform`, documentation downloads.

## Data / Content Needs
- Monitoring data (Vercel, Supabase, logging pipelines).
- QA metrics from internal dashboards.
- Legal summaries of covenant/agreements.
- Incident log entries (curated for public view).

## Tone
- Transparent, factual, calm.
- Use accessible language while retaining technical accuracy.

## Open Questions
- Determine refresh cadence (real-time vs. weekly snapshots).
- Confirm public visibility of specific incidents or only aggregate metrics.
