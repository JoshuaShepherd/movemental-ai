## üìà **Success Metrics for Initial Conversations**

### **Immediate Indicators**

**Strong Interest Signals**:
- Asks for technical details about implementation
- Wants to speak with Alan or Brad directly
- Inquires about timeline for their own platform
- Asks about network requirements or qualification process
- Requests comprehensive documentation

**Medium Interest Signals**:
- Asks about pricing and timeline
- Wants to see more case studies
- Asks to be updated on network progress
- Shares with colleagues or spouse
- Schedules follow-up conversation

**Low Interest Signals**:
- Polite but non-committal responses
- Focus on potential problems rather than opportunities
- Asks to "think about it" without next steps
- Questions the network model or community aspect
- Compares unfavorably to current simple solutions

### **Conversion Timeline Expectations**

**High-Interest Leaders**: Decision within 2-4 conversations over 2-3 weeks
**Medium-Interest Leaders**: Decision within 4-6 conversations over 2-3 months  
**Low-Interest Leaders**: May convert after seeing 6-12 months of network success

**The Patient Approach**: Focus on relationship building and value demonstration rather than pressure sales tactics. The right leaders will recognize the opportunity when they see the proof.
