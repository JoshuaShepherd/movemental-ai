# Movemental Leader Deep Profile Process
## Comprehensive Assessment Framework for Platform Candidates

*Date: September 24, 2025*  
*Systematic process for evaluating movemental leaders for platform inclusion*

---

## Overview

This process creates comprehensive leader profiles for potential Movemental platform candidates, combining internal knowledge, web-based research, network analysis, and strategic fit assessment. The framework ensures we identify not only individual leader potential but also their network connections and collaborative opportunities.

**Purpose**: Generate detailed intelligence profiles that inform:
- Platform invitation decisions
- Partnership development strategies  
- Network expansion opportunities
- Content collaboration potential
- Strategic alliance formation

**Output**: Complete leader profile with assessment scores, network mapping, and strategic recommendations.

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Foundation Research (Pre-Existing Knowledge)

#### Step 1.1: Core Identity Assessment
**Task**: Document everything we already know about the leader
**Process**:
1. Review existing mentions in Movemental documentation
2. Check prospect database for current scoring (if exists)
3. Document any previous interactions or connections
4. Note any partner/network references (MLC, 100Movements, etc.)
5. Identify theological/movement positioning from internal knowledge

**Output**: Baseline knowledge summary with gaps identified

#### Step 1.2: Initial Movement Alignment Check
**Task**: Assess basic fit with Movemental criteria
**Process**:
1. Theological alignment with missional/incarnational theology
2. Movement leadership vs. institutional leadership
3. Platform ownership mindset vs. traditional publishing approach
4. Network influence and collaboration potential
5. Revenue generation capability and business model fit

**Output**: Initial go/no-go assessment for full research

---

### Phase 2: Comprehensive Web Research

#### Step 2.1: Digital Presence Audit
**Task**: Map complete online footprint and platform presence
**Research Areas**:
1. **Official Websites**: Personal sites, organizational affiliations, bio pages
2. **Social Media Presence**: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram engagement
3. **Publishing Platforms**: Substack, Medium, personal blogs, newsletter archives
4. **Speaking Circuit**: Conference appearances, podcast interviews, webinar participation
5. **Academic Presence**: Seminary affiliations, research publications, academic profiles

**Process**:
- Use web search for "[Name] + movemental/movement/missional/church planting"
- Search "[Name] + books + published works + speaking"
- Check "[Name] + network + partnerships + organizations"
- Investigate "[Name] + revenue + business model + platform"

**Output**: Complete digital footprint map with engagement metrics

#### Step 2.2: Content & Thought Leadership Analysis
**Task**: Assess content quality, consistency, and theological depth
**Research Process**:
1. **Published Works**: Books, articles, white papers, research publications
2. **Speaking Content**: Conference talks, sermon series, workshop materials
3. **Digital Content**: Blog posts, newsletters, social media thought leadership
4. **Collaborative Works**: Co-authored materials, joint initiatives, partnerships
5. **Media Appearances**: Interviews, podcast appearances, media coverage

**Analysis Criteria**:
- Theological depth and movemental alignment
- Content quality and unique voice development
- Consistency of publishing and thought leadership
- Audience engagement and response quality
- Innovation in movemental thinking and practice

**Output**: Content assessment with quality scores and theological positioning

#### Step 2.3: Network & Influence Mapping
**Task**: Identify network connections and influence patterns
**Research Areas**:
1. **Direct Partnerships**: Organizations led, board memberships, advisory roles
2. **Collaboration History**: Co-authored works, joint conferences, shared initiatives  
3. **Endorsement Networks**: Who endorses them, whom they endorse
4. **Speaking Networks**: Conference circuits, shared stages, mutual appearances
5. **Social Media Connections**: Twitter follows, LinkedIn connections, interaction patterns

**Network Analysis Process**:
- Map connections to other movemental leaders in our database
- Identify potential referral sources and collaboration opportunities
- Document influence patterns and thought leadership positioning
- Assess network reach and multiplication potential

**Output**: Network influence map with strategic connection opportunities

---

### Phase 3: Strategic Assessment

#### Step 3.1: Movemental Leader Evaluation System Scoring
**Task**: Apply our standardized scoring framework
**Categories** (100-point scale):
1. **Movement Alignment & Theological Depth** (25 points)
2. **Audience Size & Engagement** (20 points)  
3. **Content Quality & Consistency** (20 points)
4. **Revenue Potential & Business Readiness** (15 points)
5. **Platform Ownership Mindset** (10 points)
6. **Network Value & Collaboration Potential** (10 points)

**Process**: Score each category based on research findings and document evidence supporting scores.

**Output**: Numerical assessment score with category breakdown

#### Step 3.2: Platform Fit Analysis
**Task**: Assess specific alignment with Movemental platform capabilities
**Assessment Areas**:
1. **Content Publishing Needs**: Blog, newsletter, course creation, resource libraries
2. **Community Building Potential**: Audience development, engagement, network effects
3. **Revenue Model Alignment**: Subscription, course sales, speaking bookings, consulting
4. **AI Integration Opportunities**: Content creation, audience analysis, automation
5. **Collaboration Readiness**: Cross-promotion, joint ventures, network participation

**Output**: Platform feature alignment analysis with implementation recommendations

#### Step 3.3: Strategic Value Assessment
**Task**: Evaluate broader strategic value beyond individual platform
**Considerations**:
1. **Flagship Potential**: Could they serve as case study/testimonial for platform?
2. **Network Access**: What additional leaders could they connect us to?
3. **Market Credibility**: How does their participation elevate platform reputation?
4. **Partnership Opportunities**: Speaking, content, strategic alliance potential
5. **Revenue Multiplier**: How might their success drive additional business?

**Output**: Strategic value analysis with partnership recommendations

---

### Phase 4: Network Expansion Intelligence

#### Step 4.1: Connected Leader Identification
**Task**: Map potential candidates in their network
**Research Process**:
1. **Direct Connections**: Co-authors, frequent collaborators, organizational partners
2. **Speaking Networks**: Shared conference circuits, mutual appearances
3. **Endorsement Networks**: People they endorse, people who endorse them
4. **Social Media Networks**: Active engagements, mentions, collaborations
5. **Organizational Affiliations**: Board members, advisory roles, network memberships

**Output**: Prioritized list of connected leaders with contact pathways

#### Step 4.2: Referral Strategy Development
**Task**: Create systematic approach for network expansion through this leader
**Strategy Elements**:
1. **Introduction Requests**: Specific asks for connections to high-value prospects
2. **Collaboration Opportunities**: Joint projects that naturally include other leaders
3. **Network Events**: Speaking opportunities that provide access to their networks
4. **Testimonial Leverage**: Using their success to attract similar-level leaders
5. **Partnership Development**: Strategic alliances that multiply network access

**Output**: Network expansion strategy with specific action steps

---

### Phase 5: Profile Compilation & Recommendations

#### Step 5.1: Comprehensive Profile Document
**Task**: Compile all research into structured leader profile
**Document Structure**:
1. **Executive Summary**: Key findings and strategic fit assessment
2. **Leader Overview**: Background, calling, movement positioning
3. **Platform Alignment**: Specific fit with Movemental capabilities and model
4. **Content & Thought Leadership**: Quality assessment and strategic positioning
5. **Network Analysis**: Connection mapping and influence assessment
6. **Revenue Potential**: Business model fit and growth projections
7. **Strategic Value**: Broader platform and business implications
8. **Connected Leaders**: Network expansion opportunities
9. **Implementation Recommendations**: Next steps and engagement strategy

**Output**: Complete leader profile (5-10 pages) with appendices

#### Step 5.2: Strategic Recommendations
**Task**: Provide specific action recommendations based on assessment
**Recommendation Categories**:
1. **Invitation Priority**: Immediate, high priority, moderate priority, or future consideration
2. **Engagement Strategy**: How to approach and position platform opportunity
3. **Platform Configuration**: Specific features and capabilities to emphasize
4. **Partnership Potential**: Additional collaboration opportunities beyond platform
5. **Network Development**: How to leverage their connections for expansion

**Output**: Strategic action plan with prioritized recommendations

## Implementation Guidelines

### Research Tools & Resources
- **Web Search**: Google, Bing with targeted search strings
- **Social Media**: Twitter/X advanced search, LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- **Academic**: Google Scholar, JSTOR, seminary databases
- **Publishing**: Amazon author pages, publisher websites, library catalogs
- **Speaking**: Conference websites, YouTube, podcast directories

### Quality Standards
- **Source Verification**: Multiple sources for key claims and assessments
- **Currency**: Prioritize recent content and current affiliations
- **Relevance**: Focus on movemental/missional content over general leadership
- **Evidence-Based**: Support all assessments with specific examples and documentation

### Documentation Requirements
- **Source Citations**: Track all research sources for verification
- **Evidence Files**: Screenshots, links, and documentation for key findings
- **Assessment Justification**: Clear rationale for all scoring and recommendations
- **Update Protocols**: Process for maintaining current information over time

---

## Process Optimization

### Efficiency Measures
- **Parallel Research**: Multiple web searches and platform checks simultaneously
- **Template Usage**: Standardized research templates and assessment frameworks
- **Research Depth**: Scale research intensity based on initial fit assessment
- **Automation Opportunities**: Tools and systems to streamline repetitive research

### Quality Assurance
- **Cross-Verification**: Multiple sources for key biographical and professional information
- **Assessment Calibration**: Compare scores across similar leaders for consistency
- **Strategic Review**: Final review by movemental leader for theological and strategic alignment
- **Update Cycles**: Regular refresh of leader profiles as circumstances change
