# Movemental's Core Values and Beliefs About AI
## Documenting Our Philosophy, Commitments, and Response to Artificial Intelligence

## Ranked List of Core Values and Beliefs About AI

### Ranking Criteria
Values/beliefs are ranked by:
1. **Strength**: How deeply held and non-negotiable
2. **Necessity**: How essential for Movemental's mission
3. **Resonance**: How well it aligns with our audience and the times

### Value Categories
- **Foundational Principles (#1-10)**: Core, non-negotiable commitments that shape all AI usage
- **Implementation Practices (#11-18)**: Practical principles for effective AI-human collaboration
- **Strategic Positioning (#19-23)**: Forward-looking commitments and competitive advantages
