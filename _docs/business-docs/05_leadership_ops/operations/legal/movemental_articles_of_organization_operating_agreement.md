### SECTION 3 - MANAGEMENT STRUCTURE

**3.1 Management Authority**:
The Company shall be managed by Josh Shepherd as Managing Member with full operational authority.

**3.2 Managing Member Powers**:
- Day-to-day business operations
- Hiring and personnel decisions
- Client acquisition and platform development
- Financial management and banking
- Contract negotiation and execution

**3.3 Major Decisions Requiring Member Approval**:
- Sale of Company or substantially all assets
- Merger or acquisition transactions
- Changes to ownership structure
- Annual distributions exceeding operating reserves
- Admission of new Members
- Amendment of this Operating Agreement

**3.4 Strategic Advisory Board**:
Equity Partners shall serve on Strategic Advisory Board providing:
- Market intelligence and network introductions
- Product development guidance
- Strategic partnership opportunities
- Movement leader referrals and validation

**4.1 Revenue Model**:
- **Platform Development Fees**: $1,000 per client platform
- **Revenue Sharing**: 10% of client platform earnings
- **Consulting Services**: Market rate professional services
- **Licensing Revenue**: Technology and methodology licensing

**4.2 Profit and Loss Allocation**:
Profits and losses shall be allocated according to membership percentages:
- Josh Shepherd: 70%
- Alan Hirsch: 10%  
- Brad Brisco: 10%
- Movement Leaders Collective: 10%

**4.3 Distribution Policy**:
- **Operating Reserves**: Maintain 6-month operating capital
- **Growth Investment**: 40% of profits reinvested in platform development
- **Member Distributions**: 60% of remaining profits distributed quarterly

**4.4 Capital Accounts**:
Each Member shall maintain a capital account reflecting:
- Initial capital contributions
- Additional contributions
- Allocated profits and losses
- Distributions received

### SECTION 6 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**6.1 Company IP**:
All intellectual property developed for or by the Company shall be owned by the Company, including:
- Platform technology and codebase
- AI agents and automation tools
- Movemental methodology and frameworks  
- Brand identity and marketing materials

**6.2 Pre-Existing IP**:
Members retain ownership of intellectual property contributed:
- Alan Hirsch: Existing books, content, and methodologies
- Brad Brisco: Existing content and institutional relationships
- Josh Shepherd: TrailGuide parent company assets

**6.3 License Arrangements**:
Strategic Partners grant Company non-exclusive license to use their names, likenesses, and testimonials for Company marketing and business development.

### SECTION 7 - RESTRICTIONS AND LIMITATIONS

**7.1 Non-Compete (Strategic Partners)**:
Strategic Partners agree not to:
- Develop competing digital publishing platforms for movemental leaders
- Directly solicit Company clients for alternative services
- Disclose confidential Company information or trade secrets

**7.2 Transfer Restrictions**:
Membership interests may not be transferred without:
- Written consent of Managing Member
- Right of first refusal by Company
- Compliance with securities laws

**7.3 Confidentiality**:
All Members shall maintain confidentiality of:
- Client information and business details
- Proprietary technology and methodologies
- Financial performance and strategic plans
- Network contacts and referral sources

### SECTION 8 - DISPUTE RESOLUTION

**8.1 Internal Resolution**:
Disputes shall first be addressed through:
- Direct negotiation between parties
- Mediation with neutral third party
- Strategic Advisory Board consultation

**8.2 Arbitration**:
Unresolved disputes shall be settled by binding arbitration under Delaware Arbitration Rules.

**8.3 Governing Law**:
This Agreement shall be governed by Delaware Limited Liability Company Act and Delaware law.

### SECTION 9 - AMENDMENT AND TERMINATION

**9.1 Amendment Requirements**:

- Formal documentation of changes
- Legal review and compliance verification

**9.2 Withdrawal of Members**:

- Completion of outstanding obligations
