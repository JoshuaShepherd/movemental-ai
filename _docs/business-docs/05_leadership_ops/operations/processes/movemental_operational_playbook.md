## Client Acquisition Operations

### Lead Generation Process

#### Inbound Lead Management
**Lead Sources & Tracking**
- Website contact forms and consultation requests
- Content downloads and resource access
- Webinar registrations and event participation
- Partnership referrals and network introductions

- **Tier 1**: Established movement leaders (15+ years, 5,000+ audience, current revenue)
- **Tier 2**: Emerging movement leaders (5-15 years, 1,000-5,000 audience, some revenue)
