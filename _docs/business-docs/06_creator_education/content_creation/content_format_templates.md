## Article Structures and Content Frameworks for Alan Hirsch's AI Agent

**Document Date**: September 28, 2025  
**Purpose**: Comprehensive content format templates and structural frameworks for consistent, high-quality content creation across all platforms and audiences  
**Classification**: Content Strategy & SEO Foundation

## üéØ **EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

This document provides systematic content format templates that ensure consistent quality, voice, and impact across all content types and platforms. Each template integrates Alan Hirsch's signature communication style, theological depth, and practical application focus while optimizing for specific audience needs and platform requirements.

**Core Framework**: Modular template system with adaptable components that maintain theological integrity and practical relevance across long-form articles, social media posts, video scripts, podcast outlines, and email communications. Each template includes specific sections for audience engagement, theological grounding, practical application, and call-to-action optimization.

**Key Innovation**: The template system uses a "scaffolding approach" where core theological and practical elements remain consistent while format-specific elements adapt to platform requirements and audience preferences, ensuring authentic Alan Hirsch voice regardless of content length or distribution channel.
