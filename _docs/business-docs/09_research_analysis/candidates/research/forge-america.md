# Forge America (Organization)

## Profile
National pioneer-training network birthed by Alan Hirsch and Michael Frost, equipping missional leaders through residencies, labs, and coaching cohorts across the US.

## Fit Assessment
Forge America is almost synonymous with Movemental’s DNA—if we solve budget structure and operations, onboarding them would instantly validate Movemental and deliver a pipeline of additional leaders.

## Detailed Research

### Overview
Forge America trains pioneer leaders through residencies, labs, and coaching contexts birthed from Alan Hirsch & Michael Frost’s missional movement work.

### Current Programs & Assets
- National residency network with contextualized cohorts
- Faculty producing curricula, webinars, and field guides
- Events and coaching intensives for church planters and movement catalysts

### Audience & Influence
- Active hubs in multiple US cities; hundreds of leaders annually
- Deep relational ties to Forge Canada, Forge Global, and partner denominations

### Movemental Resonance
- Legacy connection to Alan Hirsch provides credibility
- Organizational adoption creates pipeline of faculty/author platforms
- Movemental infrastructure can host resource marketplace, alumni directory, and AI mentor matching

### Risks & Considerations
- Nonprofit budget constraints; need phased pricing
- Governance coordination with global Forge entities
