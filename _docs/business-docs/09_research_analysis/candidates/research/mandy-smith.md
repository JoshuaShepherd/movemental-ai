# Mandy Smith

## Profile
Author of *The Vulnerable Pastor* and steward of Rumors of Beauty, Mandy equips pastors and creatives with embodied, artful leadership practices.

## Fit Assessment
She’s a natural Movemental storyteller whose essays and liturgies would shine on the platform, but she’ll need help building digital products and a steadier release cadence—making her a medium-fit candidate best approached through the Soul of Movement collective.
