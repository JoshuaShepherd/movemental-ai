**Core Evolution Factors**:
- Recognition that movemental leaders represent an underserved, high-value market

- Alignment with prophetic calling to serve movement multiplication

**Mission**: Empowering movemental leaders to multiply their impact through revolutionary digital publishing platforms that prioritize platform ownership, revenue retention, and movement collaboration over traditional extraction models.

- **Platform Ownership**: Every movemental leader deserves to own their digital presence
- **Movement First**: Technology serves movement multiplication, not vice versa
- **Shared Success**: 90% revenue retention aligns our success with client success
- **Network Effects**: Individual platforms strengthen the collective movemental ecosystem

These leaders don't need us to create their message—they need us to amplify the impact of the message they're already called to share.
