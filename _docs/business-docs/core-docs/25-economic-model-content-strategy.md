## Part 2: The Content Strategy

### The 80/20 Repurposing Model

**The Strategy**: 80% repurposing + 20% original content

**Why Repurposing Matters**:
- **You're already creating content**: Sermons, talks, books, teachings, videos, PDFs
- **Content is being wasted**: Most content is used once and then archived
- **AI enables efficiency**: Repurposing takes 70% less time than original creation
- **Multiplier effect**: One sermon becomes 2-3 articles, one talk becomes a course

### The Repurposing Workflow

#### 1. Content Capture

**What Gets Captured**:
- **Sermons**: Audio/video recordings, notes, outlines
- **Conference Talks**: Presentations, recordings, handouts
- **Books**: Full text (if rights retained), excerpts, key insights
- **Teachings**: Video/audio archives, course materials, PDFs
- **Conversations**: Coaching notes, email insights, recorded discussions
- **Blog Posts**: Existing articles, unpublished drafts, ideas

**How Capture Works**:
- Upload audio/video files (AI transcription)
- Paste existing text content
- Import from existing platforms
- Link to external resources
- Document ideas and insights

#### 2. AI-Powered Repurposing

**Sermon → Article Transformation**:
- AI transcribes audio to text
- Extracts key points and structure
- Generates article draft preserving your voice
- Suggests headlines and SEO optimization
- Creates multiple article variations (2-3 articles from one sermon)

**Talk → Course Conversion**:
- Breaks presentation into modules
- Creates course outline and structure
- Generates learning objectives
- Suggests assignments and assessments
- Transforms slides into course content

**Book → Article Series**:
- Extracts key chapters as standalone articles
- Creates article series with logical flow
- Adapts book content for web format
- Suggests SEO optimization and updates
- Links articles back to original book

**Video → Multiple Formats**:
- Transcribes video to text (article)
- Extracts key quotes (social media)
- Creates video highlights (short clips)
- Generates summary and takeaways (newsletter)

**PDF → Searchable Content**:
- Converts PDF to web format
- Makes content searchable and discoverable
- Suggests article adaptations
- Creates resource library entries

#### 3. Human Refinement

**The 70/30 Rule**:
- **AI handles 70%**: Structure, transcription, formatting, SEO suggestions, initial draft
- **Human provides 30%**: Voice refinement, theological accuracy, final decisions, creative direction

**Refinement Process**:
- Review AI-generated drafts
- Refine voice and style
- Ensure theological accuracy
- Add personal stories and examples
- Finalize headlines and structure
- Approve for publication

#### 4. Publication & Distribution

**Multi-Channel Publishing**:
- Platform articles (searchable, SEO-optimized)
- Newsletter distribution (email list)
- Social media snippets (quotes, highlights)
- Course modules (structured learning)
- Resource library (archived content)

**Network Integration**:
- Automatic cross-linking to related content
- Network-wide discovery and recommendations
- Collaborative content opportunities
- Scenius credibility through association
