# Post-Commitment Onboarding
## Structure, Process, and Workflow

**Purpose**: Clear organizational document outlining the post-commitment onboarding structure, process, and workflow for Movemental Authors. Focuses on what happens, who does what, when it happens, and what success looks like.

**Audience**: Internal operations team, onboarding coordinators, authors

**Status**: Foundational Document - MVP-Complete / Pre-Scale

---

## Overview: The Onboarding Structure

**Timeline**: 12 weeks (Weeks 1-12)

**Structure**: Phased approach with clear milestones, deliverables, and transition points

**Support Model**: Human coaching + AI automation + network integration

**Goal**: Transform committed author from contract signing to self-sufficient platform operator with sustainable publishing rhythm

---

## Pre-Start: Contract to Kickoff (3-5 Business Days)

### Author Responsibilities

**Within 24 Hours of Contract**:
- Sign platform agreement and payment authorization
- Complete initial questionnaire (10 minutes)
- Provide branding assets (logo, colors, fonts if available)
- Share existing content inventory (blog links, video/audio archives, PDFs)

**Within 3 Days**:
- Schedule Week 1 kickoff call (90 minutes)
- Gather 2-3 sample pieces of content (sermons, talks, articles they're proud of)
- Review platform preview (if available)

### Movemental Responsibilities

**Day 1 (Contract Signed)**:
- Create author account and access credentials
- Set up project workspace (Slack channel, dashboard access)
- Assign onboarding coordinator
- Queue platform deployment

**Days 2-3**:
- **Platform Deployment Team**: 
  - Deploy platform architecture (clone from template)
  - Configure basic branding (if assets provided)
  - Set up domain and DNS (if custom domain)
  - Configure payment processing
  - Run initial platform tests

**Days 4-5**:
- **Onboarding Coordinator**:
  - Review questionnaire responses
  - Review content inventory
  - Prepare personalized kickoff materials
  - Draft initial content strategy suggestions
  - Schedule Week 1 kickoff call

### Deliverables

- ✅ Platform deployed (basic version)
- ✅ Author dashboard access granted
- ✅ Questionnaire completed
- ✅ Week 1 kickoff call scheduled
- ✅ Initial content inventory received

## Week 1: Vision & Voice (Kickoff)

### Call Structure: 90 Minutes

**Segment 1: Discovery (45 minutes)**
- Opening: "Tell me your story" (15 min)
- Vision: "What movement are you advancing?" (15 min)
- Voice: "Walk me through a recent talk/sermon" (15 min)

**Segment 2: Platform Tour (15 minutes)**
- Dashboard walkthrough
- Key features demonstration
- AI tools preview
- Publishing workflow overview

**Segment 3: Strategy & Setup (30 minutes)**
- Content strategy: First 3 articles identified
- Editorial calendar: Next 8 weeks planned
- Accountability: Weekly check-ins scheduled
- Next steps: Week 1 assignments confirmed

### Post-Call Actions

**Movemental Team (Day 1-2)**:
- Complete AI voice capture (analyze provided content samples)
- Customize platform branding (if not done pre-call)
- Set up curriculum access
- Create personalized dashboard view
- Document voice/style guide
- Prepare Week 2 call agenda

**Author (Day 3-7)**:
- Draft first article (800 words on assigned topic)
- Complete profile setup (bio, photo, links)
- Watch Module 0 and 1 curriculum videos
- Set personal goals in dashboard
- Review and approve platform customization

### Deliverables (End of Week 1)

**From Movemental**:
- ✅ Voice/style guide documented
- ✅ AI agent trained on author's voice
- ✅ Platform fully customized
- ✅ Curriculum access granted
- ✅ Week 2 call scheduled

**From Author**:
- ✅ First article draft completed
- ✅ Profile setup complete
- ✅ Initial curriculum modules completed
- ✅ Platform customization approved

### Success Criteria

- Vision clearly articulated and documented
- Voice captured and AI trained
- Platform ready and customized
- First article draft complete
- Author understands publishing workflow
- Relationship established with onboarding coordinator

## Week 2: First Publication

### Call Structure: 60 Minutes

**Pre-Call (Movemental Team)**:
- AI analysis of draft article (voice consistency, SEO optimization)
- Detailed feedback prepared (dashboard comments)
- Headline variations generated
- SEO optimization suggestions prepared

**Call Activities**:
- **Article Workshop** (35 minutes): Review draft together, live editing, headline refinement, SEO optimization
- **Publishing Process** (10 minutes): Walk through publishing workflow, hit publish together
- **Promotion Strategy** (10 minutes): Email to existing list, social post creation
- **Next Steps** (5 minutes): Article 2 topic assigned, curriculum modules assigned

### Post-Call Actions

**Movemental Team (Day 1-2)**:
- Monitor first publication (analytics tracking begins)
- Network forum announcement (automated)
- SEO indexing initiated
- Prepare Week 3 call materials

**Author (Day 3-7)**:
- Review and implement feedback
- Publish first article
- Send migration email to existing audience
- Promote via social media
- Begin drafting Article 2

### Deliverables (End of Week 2)

**From Movemental**:
- ✅ First article published (co-published during call)
- ✅ SEO optimization complete
- ✅ Network announcement sent
- ✅ Analytics tracking active
- ✅ Week 3 call scheduled

**From Author**:
- ✅ First article published
- ✅ Migration email sent
- ✅ Social promotion complete
- ✅ Article 2 draft in progress

### Success Criteria

- First article published and promoted
- Publishing process confidence achieved
- Initial audience engagement (emails opened, social engagement)
- Article 2 draft in progress

**Week 5 Focus**: Content review, editorial calendar refinement, network engagement strategy

**Week 7 Focus**: Analytics deep dive, SEO performance review, audience growth analysis

### Author Responsibilities (Weeks 5-8)

**Ongoing**:

- Network engagement facilitation (introductions, collaboration opportunities)

**From Movemental**:

- ✅ Network engagement facilitated
- ✅ Week 9 call scheduled

**From Author**:

### Success Criteria (Week 8 Milestone)

## Roles & Responsibilities

- Facilitate network integration
- Monitor progress and address concerns
- Coordinate with technical team as needed

**Primary Role**: Technical setup and platform deployment

**Responsibilities**:
- Platform architecture deployment
- Branding customization
- Content import and migration
- Domain and DNS configuration

**Primary Role**: AI voice training and content support

**Responsibilities**:
- AI voice capture and training

## Key Workflows

### Platform Deployment Workflow

1. **Contract Signed** → Queue platform deployment
2. **Content Inventory Received** → Import existing content
3. **Branding Assets Received** → Apply branding customization
4. **Domain Information Received** → Configure domain and DNS
5. **Payment Authorization Received** → Configure payment processing
6. **Platform Tests Run** → Validate all features
7. **Platform Preview Sent** → Author review and approval
8. **Platform Live** → Author access granted

**Timeline**: 2-4 business days

### Voice Capture Workflow

1. **Content Samples Received** → Queue for analysis
2. **AI Analysis Run** → Identify voice markers, style patterns, theological positions
3. **Voice Guide Documented** → Create style guide document
4. **AI Agent Trained** → Configure AI with voice and theology
5. **Voice Guide Approved** → Author review and approval
6. **AI Tools Active** → Repurposing and content tools ready

**Timeline**: 1-2 business days

### Article Publishing Workflow

1. **Article Draft Submitted** → Author uploads draft to dashboard
2. **AI Analysis Run** → Voice consistency, SEO optimization, headline suggestions
3. **Feedback Provided** → Comments and suggestions added to draft
4. **Call Scheduled** → Article workshop call (Weeks 2-4)
5. **Live Editing** → Collaborative editing during call
6. **Publish Together** → Co-publish during call (Week 2)
7. **Post-Publication** → Network announcement, SEO indexing, analytics tracking

**Timeline**: 2-3 days from draft to publication (Weeks 2-4)

### Network Integration Workflow

1. **Week 4 Call** → Network introduction discussion
2. **Relevant Connections Identified** → Match author with relevant network members
3. **Introductions Sent** → Automated + personal introductions
4. **Initial Outreach** → Author connects with network members
5. **Collaboration Opportunities** → Identify cross-referencing and collaboration
6. **Ongoing Engagement** → Support network participation

**Timeline**: Weeks 4-12 (ongoing)

## Escalation & Support

### Issue Resolution

**Level 1: Standard Support**
- Platform questions
- Content guidance
- Basic troubleshooting
- **Handled By**: Onboarding coordinator

**Level 2: Technical Support**
- Platform bugs or errors
- Feature implementation issues
- Payment processing problems
- **Handled By**: Platform deployment team

**Level 3: Strategic Support**
- Complex strategy questions
- Network integration challenges
- Revenue optimization needs
- **Handled By**: Senior leadership / strategic advisors

### Critical Issues

**Definition**: Issues that prevent author from publishing or operating platform

**Process**:
1. Immediate notification to onboarding coordinator
2. Coordinator assesses and escalates if needed
3. Technical team response within 4 hours
4. Resolution tracking until resolved

## Summary: The Onboarding Structure

**Timeline**: 12 weeks intensive onboarding, transition to monthly support

**Structure**: Phased approach with clear milestones and deliverables

**Support Model**: Human coaching + AI automation + network integration

**Success Criteria**: Self-sufficient author with sustainable publishing rhythm, established authority, active network integration, monetization launched

**Key Principle**: Coach the cook, not the stove. Focus on movemental strategy and content, not technical training.
