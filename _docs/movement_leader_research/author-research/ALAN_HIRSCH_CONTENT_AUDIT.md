## Executive Summary

This audit provides a comprehensive inventory of Alan Hirsch's existing content assets across all formats and distribution channels. It catalogs 17+ published books, identifies content strengths and gaps, maps distribution channels, analyzes performance opportunities, and outlines repurposing strategies. The audit reveals a strong foundational content library with coherent frameworks (mDNA, APEST) but identifies opportunities for increased digital content frequency, multimedia formats, and updated foundational content for current contexts.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Complete content inventory by format and topic
- Content performance analysis and opportunities
- Content gap identification
- Repurposing roadmap
- Content architecture recommendations
