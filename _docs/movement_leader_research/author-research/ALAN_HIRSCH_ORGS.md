### 3. 5Q Collective (5Q Central)

**Website:** [https://5qcentral.com/](https://5qcentral.com/)

**Logo Location:** `public/images/orgs/5q-collective-logo.png`

**Relationship:** Founder, Resident Coach and Consultant

**Mission:**
To create training options where fivefold imagination and practice can be activated and developed throughout the Body of Christ.

**Vision:**
To reactivate the original intelligence and capacity of the church through the fivefold ministry (APEST).

**Theological Foundation - The APEST Model:**
Based on Ephesians 4:11-13, the five ministry gifts are:
- **Apostles** - Pioneering, breaking new ground, guarding the DNA
- **Prophets** - Discernment, justice, keeping the community true to covenant
- **Evangelists** - Recruitment, communication, storytelling
- **Shepherds** - Nurture, care, community development
- **Teachers** - Understanding, wisdom, biblical literacy

**Key Focus Areas:**
- Fivefold ministry assessment and development
- Complete ministry mix for churches
- Leadership team development
- Organizational intelligence

**Key Features:**
- APEST assessment tools
- Training programs for each of the five ministries
- Coaching and consulting services
- Resources and materials for fivefold activation

**Programs & Services:**
- APEST assessments for individuals and teams
- 5Q training intensives
- Church consulting and coaching
- Certification programs for 5Q practitioners
- Online courses and resources

**Impact:**
The 5Q Collective has helped thousands of churches and organizations understand and implement the fivefold ministry, leading to more balanced and effective ministry teams.
