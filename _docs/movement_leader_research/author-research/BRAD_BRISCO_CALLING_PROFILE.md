## Calling Statement

### Synthesized Calling

Brad Brisco is called to **bridge institutional church structures with grassroots missional movements**, equipping churches for multiplication and transformation through practical frameworks, sustainable models, and accessible resources. His vocation is not to critique the church but to catalyze transition from maintenance to mission, connecting the wisdom of institutional structures (NAMB/Send Network) with the energy of grassroots movements (Forge, missional communities). Through frameworks (missional ecclesiology, covocational planting, multiplication strategies), training (Forge America, Forge Kansas City, Sentralized Conference), and resources (books, curriculum, articles), he empowers others for missional transition and sustainable church planting. His calling integrates missional theology with practical implementation, bridges theory and practice, and centers on multiplication as the organizing principle for church health. He is a bridge-builder, multiplication catalyst, and equipper of leaders—called to connect what has been separated and activate what has been dormant.

### Core Calling Elements

**1. Bridge-Builder**
- Not choosing between institutional and grassroots but connecting them
- Language of "bridge," "connection," "integration"
- Focus on practical pathways between structures and movements

**2. Multiplication Catalyst**
- Not just addition but multiplication and movement
- Focus on sustainable, reproducible models
- Emphasis on equipping others to multiply

**3. Transition Facilitator**
- Helping existing churches shift toward mission
- Not just planting new churches but transforming existing ones
- Practical frameworks for sustainable change

**4. Practical Equipper**
- Through training, resources, curriculum, and coaching
- Empowers others to carry forward the calling
- Builds infrastructure for missional movement (Forge, NAMB/Send Network)

**5. Accessible Theologian**
- Makes missional theology accessible to practitioners
- Integrates theory and practice without oversimplifying
- Practical theology that serves everyday mission
