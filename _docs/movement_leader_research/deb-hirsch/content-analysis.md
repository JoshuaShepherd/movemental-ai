## Content Thematic Analysis

### Primary Themes

Based on published works, organizational roles, speaking engagements, and professional background, Deb Hirsch's content focuses on:

1. **Sexuality and Faith**
   - "Redeeming Sex" - Personal journey, bringing new conversations about sexuality into church context
   - 25+ years of professional experience in sexuality counseling
   - Focus on integrating difficult conversations about sexuality into Christian community
   - Emphasis on redeeming and reframing sexuality within faith contexts

2. **Missional Discipleship**
   - "Untamed: Reactivating a Missional Form of Discipleship" (co-authored with Alan Hirsch)
   - Co-founder of Forge Mission Training Network
   - Focus on authentic, untamed discipleship
   - Emphasis on missional form of discipleship

3. **Female Agency and Leadership**
   - "Activating Female Agency" - Topic at The Praxis Gathering
   - "The Presence of a Leader" - Topic at The Praxis Gathering
   - Emphasis on equal agency and authentic leadership within the church
   - Focus on female presence and leadership in Christian contexts

4. **Community Development and Social Work**
   - 25+ years of experience in social work and community development
   - Led churches in both Australia and Los Angeles
   - Lives in community with others
   - Emphasis on community-based ministry and development

5. **Authentic Leadership**
   - "The Presence of a Leader" - Speaking topic
   - Focus on authentic leadership within fragmented societies
   - Emphasis on leadership presence and authenticity
   - Integration of personal journey and professional experience

6. **Church Leadership and Ministry**
   - Led churches in Australia and Los Angeles
   - Leadership team member of Christian Associates (church planting movement)
   - Board member of Missio Alliance
   - Focus on missional church leadership

7. **Counseling and Pastoral Care**
   - 25+ years as trained counselor specializing in sexuality
   - Integration of counseling expertise with church leadership
   - Focus on bringing professional counseling insights into church contexts
