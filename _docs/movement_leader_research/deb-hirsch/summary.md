# Digital Reality Profile: Deb Hirsch

**Research Date**: January 4, 2026  
**Confidence Level**: High

## Executive Summary

Deb Hirsch is an Australian-born speaker, church leader, and writer with extensive experience in both Australia and Los Angeles. She is a co-founder of the Forge Mission Training Network, serves on the Forge America national team, and is a board member of Missio Alliance. With over 25 years of professional experience in social work, community development, and counseling (specializing in sexuality), Deb brings a unique combination of professional expertise and missional church leadership. She has authored "Redeeming Sex" (solo work) and co-authored "Untamed: Reactivating a Missional Form of Discipleship" with her husband, Alan Hirsch, both published through InterVarsity Press.

Deb's digital presence includes an InterVarsity Press author page, organizational affiliations (Missio Alliance profile, Forge, Christian Associates), Twitter presence (@debrahirsch), and speaking engagement references (The Praxis Gathering). Her content focuses on sexuality and faith integration, missional discipleship, female agency, authentic leadership, and community development. The content ecosystem shows strong thematic consistency around these areas, though opportunities exist for greater content interconnection, digital amplification of professional expertise, and content series development.
