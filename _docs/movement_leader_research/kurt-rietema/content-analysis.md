5. **Christian Community Development**
   - Street Psalms involvement (Christian community development and spiritual formation)
   - Church planting work (Pathway Community Church, Bible studies)
   - Focus on building faith communities in under-resourced areas
   - Emphasis on relationship-building and community formation

6. **Youth Empowerment & Leadership**
   - Youth-led initiatives (Snack Shack KC)
   - Youth social entrepreneurship programs
   - Focus on empowering young people to address social issues
   - Creating safe spaces for teens

7. **Global Development & Social Justice**
   - Master's degree in Global Development and Social Justice

   - Integration of global perspective with local engagement
   - Border support work demonstrates global-local connection
