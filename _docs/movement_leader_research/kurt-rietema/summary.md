## Executive Summary

Kurt Rietema is a dedicated community development leader and youth ministry innovator based in Kansas City, Kansas. As Senior Director of YF Neighborhood at Youthfront and Senior Director at Street Psalms, Rietema focuses on integrating practical theology with social entrepreneurship, community development in under-resourced neighborhoods, and immigration advocacy. He co-authored "To Mend the World: A New Vision for Youth Ministry" (2023) with Jason Lief, serves as an adjunct professor at MidAmerica Nazarene University and William Jewell College, and actively engages in community development work in the Argentine neighborhood of Kansas City.

Rietema's digital presence includes organizational platforms (Youthfront, Fuller Youth Institute, Street Psalms), academic affiliations, a published book, and community work documented in news articles. His content spans books, academic papers, organizational resources, and community development initiatives, with strong thematic consistency around youth ministry innovation, social entrepreneurship, community development, and justice work. Opportunities exist for greater content interconnection, digital amplification of community work, and personal brand establishment.
