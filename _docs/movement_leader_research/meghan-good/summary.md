## Executive Summary

Meghan Larissa Good is an author, theologian, and pastor with over sixteen years of pastoral experience, currently serving as Faculty of Record for Teaching Leadership in the Doctor of Ministry program at Anabaptist Mennonite Biblical Seminary. She holds degrees from Gordon College, Duke Divinity School, and Portland Seminary (where she received the Frederick Buechner Award for Excellence in Preaching). Good has published two books: "The Bible Unwrapped: Making Sense of Scripture Today" (focusing on biblical hermeneutics) and "Divine Gravity: Sparking a Movement to Recover a Better Christian Story" (focusing on movement-oriented Christian narrative recovery).

Good's digital presence includes a personal website (meghanlarissagood.com), academic affiliation with Anabaptist Mennonite Biblical Seminary, and published books. Her work focuses on biblical hermeneutics, contemporary preaching, recovering a better Christian story, and teaching leadership.
