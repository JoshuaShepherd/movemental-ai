# Content Analysis: Michael Frost

**Research Date**: January 4, 2026

## Content Thematic Analysis

### Primary Themes

1. **Missional Theology & Practice**
   - Missional-incarnational approaches to church and mission
   - Post-Christendom church and mission
   - Missional living frameworks (BELLS method)
   - Contextual ministry and cultural exegesis

2. **Cultural Exegesis & Contextual Ministry**
   - Understanding cultural context for ministry
   - Reading "signs" in communities (billboards, advertisements, graffiti, political signs)
   - Understanding demographic, diversity, voice, and values of places
   - Creating contextual ministry responses rather than generic religious services

3. **Missional Living & Daily Practices**
   - BELLS framework: Bless, Eat, Listen, Learn, Sent
   - Integrating evangelism into daily life
   - Practical habits for missional living
   - Natural and fulfilling evangelism practices

4. **Post-Christian Culture & Exile**
   - Living missionally in post-Christian society
   - Christians as exiles in contemporary culture
   - Reimagining Christian community as monastic missionary order
   - Trinitarian theology, covenantal expression, catholic orientation, missional intention

5. **Church Innovation & Transformation**
   - Shaping the future of church (The Shaping of Things to Come)
   - Road to missional transformation (The Road to Missional)
   - Missional church development
   - Church transition and renewal

6. **Discipleship & Faith Formation**
   - Learning Christ (as part of BELLS)
   - Commitment to learning about Jesus and His teachings
   - Listening to the Holy Spirit
   - Faith integration into daily life

- **Missiology**: Missional theology, missional-incarnational approaches

- **Cultural Theology**: Cultural exegesis, contextual ministry
