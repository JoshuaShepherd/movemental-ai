# Digital Reality Profile: Rob Wegner

**Research Date**: January 6, 2026  
**Confidence Level**: High

## Executive Summary

Rob Wegner is the founding leader and director of Kansas City Underground (KCU), a mission agency and decentralized network of microchurches committed to saturating Kansas City with the beauty, justice, and Good News of Jesus. He also serves as the Director of Microchurch Next for Exponential, focusing on the resurgence of microchurches in the West, and is a member of the Global Leadership Team of NewThing, a global network equipping movement-makers. Wegner co-authored "The Starfish and the Spirit: Unleashing the Leadership Potential of Churches and Organizations" with Lance Ford and Alan Hirsch, exploring decentralized leadership models.

Wegner's digital presence includes an active podcast (KC Underground Podcast), published articles on Exponential, training programs (including "Find Your Place"), conference speaking, and organizational websites. The content ecosystem shows strong thematic consistency around microchurch movements, disciple-making, decentralized leadership, and empowering ordinary people, with active content creation across multiple platforms. Opportunities exist for greater content interconnection, repurposing across forms, and unified personal content hub.
