# Digital Reality Profile: Rowland Smith

**Research Date**: January 2025  
**Confidence Level**: Medium-High

## Executive Summary

Rowland Smith (Dr. L. Rowland Smith) is a pastor, educator, and author who serves as the National Director of Forge America Mission Training Network and holds adjunct faculty positions at Fuller Theological Seminary, Denver Seminary, and Grand Canyon University. He is the author of "Red Skies: 10 Essential Conversations for our Future as the Church," which focuses on essential conversations for church leaders about the future of the church.

Smith's digital presence is primarily through academic institution websites (where he is listed as adjunct faculty) and the Forge America Mission Training Network (where he serves as National Director). His published work includes at least one book, "Red Skies," though additional publications may exist. His content focuses on missional theology, mission training, church leadership, and the future of the church.
