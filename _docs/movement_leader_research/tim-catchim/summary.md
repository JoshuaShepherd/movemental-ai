# Digital Reality Profile: Tim Catchim

**Research Date**: January 4, 2026  
**Confidence Level**: High

## Executive Summary

Tim Catchim is a multi-vocational entrepreneur, author, and church leader based in Nashville, Tennessee, recognized for his work in fivefold ministry (APEST), church leadership development, and movemental church planting. He co-authored "The Permanent Revolution: Apostolic Imagination and Practice for the 21st Century Church" with Alan Hirsch, a prominent missional movement leader, and "The Permanent Revolution Playbook" (2014). Catchim founded Trimtab, a coaching and consulting agency focused on APEST framework application, and serves as Team Leader at OneLife, a movemental church plant in Nashville.

Catchim's digital presence includes the Trimtab website (trimtab.guide), the OneLife church website (onelifenashville.wixsite.com), published books through InterVarsity Press, and active participation on Hermeneutics Stack Exchange. His content ecosystem shows strong thematic consistency around APEST, leadership development, and movemental church practices, though opportunities exist for greater content interconnection, digital amplification of embodied work, and content repurposing across multiple forms.
