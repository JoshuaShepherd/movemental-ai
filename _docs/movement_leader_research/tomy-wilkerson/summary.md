## Executive Summary

Tomy Wilkerson is a Christian movement leader and network director with an active personal blog presence and significant leadership experience in decentralized church networks. As Co-Director/Tampa Director of the Underground Network (Tampa), a decentralized network of unique expressions of the church, Wilkerson brings eight years of InterVarsity Christian Fellowship experience in planting student ministries and current trustee role with InterVarsity Christian Fellowship of Kentucky. He holds master's degrees in both Creative Writing and Ministry Leadership, and maintains an active personal blog at tomywilkerson.com where he shares thoughts on faith, life, and society.

Wilkerson's digital presence centers on his personal blog, with additional presence through Exponential Conference speaker profile and Underground Network leadership role. The blog covers book reviews, leadership reflections, personal experiences, and reflections on faith, life, and society. His work demonstrates strong alignment with Movemental's target audience of Christian movement thought leaders, authors, preachers, and practitioners, particularly in the areas of network leadership, movement building, and decentralized church expressions.
