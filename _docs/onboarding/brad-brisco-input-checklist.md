## 1. Voice & Content Samples (For AI Voice Training)

### Voice Baseline Content (Required for AI Training)

- [ ] **Content Brad considers "his best work"**
  - What he's most proud of creating
  - What best represents his voice and perspective
  - Used as reference for voice preservation

  *** Present the work TO him and ask him to select ***

## 2. Brand Assets (For Design Customization)

### Logo & Visual Identity

- [ ] **Brand colors** (if known)
  - Hex codes (if available)
  - Or "use your defaults"
  - Used for color token customization

- [ ] **Font preferences** (if any)
  - Specific fonts he uses
  - Or "use your defaults"
  - Used for typography customization

### Style References (For Design Direction)

- [ ] **2-3 websites he admires** (links)
  - Visual style references
  - Used to understand aesthetic preferences

- [ ] **3-word description of ideal site feel**
  - e.g., "professional, warm, accessible"
  - Used for design charter

- [ ] **What visitors should feel** (one sentence)
  - Emotional/experiential goal
  - Used for design direction

- [ ] **Style constraints** (if any)
  - "Don't use [color/style]"
  - "Must include [element]"
  - "Must avoid [element]"
  - Used to set design boundaries

  *** If no templates, give examples and offer selection ***

## Critical Path Items (Must Have Before Launch)

These items are absolutely required for launch readiness:

1. **Voice baseline content** (2-3 sermons/talks/writings)
2. **Brand assets** (logo, colors, or "use defaults")
3. **Vision & calling** (movemental purpose)
4. **First 3 articles** (drafted and approved)
5. **Profile information** (bio, photo)
6. **Design approval** (final design approved)
7. **Network relationships** (for scenius credibility)
8. **AI disclosure approval** (transparency language)

**If any critical path item is missing, launch cannot proceed.**
