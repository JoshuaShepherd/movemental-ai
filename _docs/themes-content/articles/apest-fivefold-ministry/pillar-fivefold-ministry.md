**Framework Connection**: Fivefold ministry as biblical foundation for movement effectiveness
**Article Roadmap**: Biblical foundation, five gifts explained, early church patterns, activation, challenges, integration

**Voice Requirements**: 2-4 epigraphs, direct address, biblical foundation, movement language

#### 2. Framework Foundation (500-800 words)

**Required Content**:
- **Biblical Foundation**: Ephesians 4:11-13
  - "He gave some to be apostles, some prophets, some evangelists, some pastors and teachers"
  - Purpose: equip saints, build body, reach unity and maturity
- **Five Ministry Gifts**:
  - Apostle: Pioneer, sent, systems builder
  - Prophet: Discerner, challenger, voice of God
  - Evangelist: Communicator, bridge-builder, passion for lost
  - Shepherd: Caregiver, community builder, nurturer
  - Teacher: Clarifier, explainer, formation guide
- **Theological Foundation**: Jesus's gift, original design, complete capability
- **Gift vs. Office**: Functional gifts for all believers, not hierarchical offices

**Historical Examples**: Early church (Acts), Paul's teams, early church fathers
