# Prophetic Ministry: Implementation Guide

**Concept**: Prophetic Ministry  
**Purpose**: Step-by-step guide for implementing prophetic ministry  
**Target Audience**: Church leaders, practitioners, implementers  
**Last Updated**: December 2025

---

## Overview

This guide provides practical steps for developing and implementing prophetic ministry in your church or organization. Prophetic ministry is essential for the church to remain faithful to God's purposes and aligned with God's values.

---

## Assessment

### Current State Assessment

**Questions to Consider**:
1. How is our church currently aligned with God's purposes?
2. Do we have people with prophetic gifting who are being released to function?
3. How do we respond to prophetic challenge?
4. Are we confronting injustice and unfaithfulness?
5. Do we have systems for testing and accountability?

### Assessment Tools

#### Tool 1: APEST Assessment

**Purpose**: Identifies prophetic gifting

**How to Use**: Use 5Q assessment tools

**Scoring**: Look for prophetic gifting and corporate expression

### Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Leadership understands prophetic ministry
- [ ] Church has identified people with prophetic gifting
- [ ] Systems in place for testing and accountability
- [ ] Culture that values prophetic challenge
- [ ] Integration with other APEST ministries

## Step-by-Step Implementation

### Phase 1: Foundation

**Duration**: 1-3 months  
**Goal**: Establish understanding and commitment

#### Step 1.1: Leadership Education

**Action**: Educate leadership on prophetic ministry

**Resources Needed**: Books, study guides, time

**Expected Outcome**: Leadership understands and commits

#### Step 1.2: Church-Wide Teaching

**Action**: Teach the church about prophetic ministry

**Resources Needed**: Teaching materials, time

**Expected Outcome**: Church understands prophetic ministry

#### Step 1.3: Assessment and Identification

**Action**: Identify people with prophetic gifting

**Resources Needed**: Assessment tools, time

**Expected Outcome**: People with prophetic gifting identified

### Phase 2: Creating Space

**Duration**: 3-6 months  
**Goal**: Create systems for prophetic ministry

#### Step 2.1: Create Listening Spaces

**Action**: Create spaces where prophetic voice can be heard

**Resources Needed**: Meeting spaces, time, facilitation

**Expected Outcome**: Prophetic voice has space to function

#### Step 2.2: Build Accountability

**Action**: Create systems for testing and accountability

**Resources Needed**: Accountability structures, time

**Expected Outcome**: Prophetic ministry has accountability

#### Step 2.3: Integrate with Other Ministries

**Action**: Ensure prophetic ministry works with other APEST

**Resources Needed**: Integration focus, leadership attention

**Expected Outcome**: Prophetic ministry integrated

### Phase 3: Functioning and Impact

**Duration**: 6-12 months  
**Goal**: Prophetic ministry functioning and impacting

#### Step 3.1: Release Prophetic Ministry

**Action**: Release people with prophetic gifting to function

**Resources Needed**: Support, resources, space

**Expected Outcome**: Prophetic ministry functioning

#### Step 3.2: Respond to Challenge

**Action**: Create culture that responds to prophetic challenge

**Resources Needed**: Humility, openness, systems

**Expected Outcome**: Church responds to prophetic challenge

#### Step 3.3: Measure Impact

**Action**: Measure alignment with God's purposes

**Resources Needed**: Metrics, evaluation, feedback

**Expected Outcome**: Impact measured and improved

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1: Only Criticism, No Energizing

**Description**: Prophetic ministry becomes only negative

**How to Avoid**: Ensure both critique and energizing

**If You're Already There**: Rebalance toward energizing

### Pitfall 2: No Accountability

**Description**: Prophetic ministry without testing

**How to Avoid**: Build accountability systems

**If You're Already There**: Create accountability structures

### Pitfall 3: Isolation

**Description**: Prophetic ministry separated from other ministries

**How to Avoid**: Integrate with other APEST

**If You're Already There**: Rebuild integration
