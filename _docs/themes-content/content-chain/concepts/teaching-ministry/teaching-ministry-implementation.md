# Teaching Ministry: Implementation Guide

**Concept**: Teaching Ministry  
**Purpose**: Step-by-step guide for implementing teaching ministry  
**Target Audience**: Church leaders, practitioners, implementers  
**Last Updated**: December 2025

---

## Overview

This guide provides practical steps for developing and implementing teaching ministry that mediates wisdom, fosters formation, and integrates with other APEST ministries without dominating.

---

## Assessment

### Current State Assessment

**Questions to Consider**:
1. Is our teaching life-oriented and participatory?
2. Is teaching integrated with practice?
3. Does teaching dominate at expense of other ministries?
4. Do teachers' lives reflect their message?
5. Are we fostering formation, not just information?

### Assessment Tools

#### Tool 1: APEST Assessment

**Purpose**: Identifies teaching gifting and balance

**How to Use**: Use 5Q assessment tools

**Scoring**: Look for teaching gifting and APEST balance

### Readiness Checklist

- [ ] Leadership understands teaching ministry
- [ ] Teaching is life-oriented, not abstract
- [ ] Integration with practice established
- [ ] Balance with other APEST ministries
- [ ] Teachers' lives reflect message

## Step-by-Step Implementation

### Phase 1: Foundation

**Duration**: 1-3 months  
**Goal**: Establish understanding and shift to life-oriented teaching

#### Step 1.1: Leadership Education

**Action**: Educate leadership on life-oriented teaching

**Resources Needed**: Books, study guides, time

**Expected Outcome**: Leadership understands and commits

#### Step 1.2: Church-Wide Teaching

**Action**: Teach the church about life-oriented teaching

**Resources Needed**: Teaching materials, time

**Expected Outcome**: Church understands life-oriented approach

#### Step 1.3: Assessment and Identification

**Action**: Identify people with teaching gifting

**Resources Needed**: Assessment tools, time

**Expected Outcome**: People with teaching gifting identified

### Phase 2: Creating Systems

**Duration**: 3-6 months  
**Goal**: Create systems for life-oriented teaching

#### Step 2.1: Shift to Life-Oriented

**Action**: Shift from abstract to life-oriented teaching

**Resources Needed**: Framework, training, time

**Expected Outcome**: Teaching becomes life-oriented

#### Step 2.2: Integrate with Practice

**Action**: Create integration between teaching and practice

**Resources Needed**: Practice opportunities, reflection, time

**Expected Outcome**: Teaching integrated with practice

#### Step 2.3: Balance with Other Ministries

**Action**: Ensure teaching doesn't dominate

**Resources Needed**: APEST balance focus, leadership attention

**Expected Outcome**: Teaching balanced with other ministries

### Phase 3: Functioning and Impact

**Duration**: 6-12 months  
**Goal**: Teaching ministry functioning and impacting

#### Step 3.1: Release Teaching Ministry

**Action**: Release people with teaching gifting to function

**Resources Needed**: Support, resources, space

**Expected Outcome**: Teaching ministry functioning

#### Step 3.2: Measure Impact

**Action**: Measure formation, integration, balance

**Resources Needed**: Metrics, evaluation, feedback

**Expected Outcome**: Impact measured and improved

#### Step 3.3: Multiply Teachers

**Action**: Develop and multiply people in teaching ministry

**Resources Needed**: Training systems, multiplication focus

**Expected Outcome**: Teachers multiplied

---

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1: Abstract Teaching

**Description**: Teaching detached from life and practice

**How to Avoid**: Focus on life-oriented, participatory teaching

**If You're Already There**: Shift to life-oriented approach

### Pitfall 2: Dominating

**Description**: Teaching dominating at expense of other ministries

**How to Avoid**: Ensure APEST balance, resituate within practice

**If You're Already There**: Rebalance, integrate with other ministries

### Pitfall 3: Information Only

**Description**: Teaching as only information transfer

**How to Avoid**: Focus on wisdom mediation and formation

**If You're Already There**: Shift to wisdom and formation focus

## Success Indicators

### Short-Term (0-3 months)
- Leadership understands life-oriented teaching
- People with teaching gifting identified
- Shift from abstract to life-oriented beginning

### Medium-Term (3-12 months)
- Teaching becoming life-oriented
- Integration with practice happening
- Balance with other ministries

### Long-Term (12+ months)
- Teaching ministry functioning well
- Formation happening
- Integration with practice established
- Balance maintained
