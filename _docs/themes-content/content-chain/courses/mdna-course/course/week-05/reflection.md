# Reflection Questions

Take some time to reflect on these questions. You might want to journal your responses, or discuss them with others who are on this journey with you.

1. **Which APEST functions do you see most clearly in yourself?** Which are dominant? Which are weaker? What is your unique APEST profile?

2. **Which APEST functions do you see most clearly in your community?** Which are present? Which are missing or suppressed? What difference does that make?

3. **What would it look like to create an APEST culture where all five forms of intelligence are valued and activated?** What would need to change? What obstacles stand in the way?

4. **How does your community express the fivefold functions?** Are all five present? Are some missing? How does this affect your community's health and mission?

5. **How can you avoid allowing any one function to dominate?** What would it look like to maintain dynamic tension between all five functions?

6. **How does APEST connect to Jesus' lordship?** What does it mean that Jesus embodies all five functions? How does this shape your understanding of the church?

7. **How does APEST connect to disciple-making and mission?** What does it mean that all five functions are needed for movement? How does this shape your understanding of ministry?

8. **What does it mean that APEST is about the ministry of all believers?** How does this shift your understanding of ministry? What would it look like to activate the ministry of all believers?

9. **How does Ephesians 4:11-13 speak to you?** What does it mean that the goal is "attaining to the whole measure of the fullness of Christ"? Why are all five functions necessary?

10. **What's your next step?** How can you discover and develop your APEST calling? How can you help create APEST culture in your context? What support do you need?
