# Reflection Questions

Take some time to reflect on these questions. You might want to journal your responses, or discuss them with others who are on this journey with you.

1. **Where have you experienced communitas?** What conditions created it? What made it different from normal community?

2. **What would it look like to create liminal spaces and shared adventure in your context?** What obstacles stand in the way? What would need to change?

3. **How does communitas differ from normal community, and why does that matter for movement?** What's the difference? Why does it matter?

4. **What would it look like to organize your church around mission rather than ministry?** How would this change your community? What obstacles stand in the way?

5. **How can you develop transformative vision that calls people out of comfort zones?** What vision would draw people into liminal space? What would that look like?

6. **What shared endeavors could your church engage in that would create communitas?** What projects, missions, or adventures could you do together?

7. **How can you put adventure back into the venture?** What would it look like to embed adventure and risk into your church culture?

8. **How does communitas connect to Jesus' lordship?** What does it mean that communitas reflects the nature of the kingdom community Jesus created?

9. **How does Acts 2:42-47 speak to you?** What kind of community is being described? What conditions created it? How does this relate to communitas?

10. **What's your next step?** How can you begin creating communitas? What support do you need? What questions do you want to explore further?
