# Reflection Questions

Take some time to reflect on these questions. You might want to journal your responses, or discuss them with others who are on this journey with you.

1. **Which element of mDNA needs the most attention in your context right now?** Why? What difference would it make if this element were reactivated?

2. **What would it look like to begin reactivating movement DNA where you are?** What's your first step? What obstacles stand in the way?

3. **What support do you need?** Who can journey with you? What resources do you need? What community can you join?

4. **What's your next step?** What concrete action can you take? What will you do this week? This month? This year?

5. **How will you remember what you've forgotten?** What practices will help you remember? What rhythms will help you reactivate?

6. **How does Revelation 21:5—"I am making everything new"—speak to you?** What does it mean that God is making everything new? How does this connect to remembering and reactivating movement DNA?

7. **What does it mean that Apostolic Genius is an emergent property?** How does this shape your understanding of how the six elements work together?

8. **What does it mean that the future lies in the womb of the present?** How does this give you hope? How does this shape your approach to reactivating movement DNA?

9. **How will you keep Jesus is Lord at the center?** What practices will help you maintain this center? How will you ensure everything flows from and returns to this center?

10. **What's your action plan?** What will you do? When will you do it? Who will you do it with? How will you know if you're making progress?
