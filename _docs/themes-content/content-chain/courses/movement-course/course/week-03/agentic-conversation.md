# Agentic Conversation

*This section will be facilitated through an interactive AI conversation designed to help you identify where you are in your journey and what questions you're bringing.*

**Focus:** Helps participants identify leverage points in their movementâ€”where are they currently intervening? What leverage do those interventions have? Where could they intervene for greater impact? What feedback loops are functioning well? Which need strengthening?

**Sample Prompts:**
- Where are you currently intervening in your movement? What changes are you making? What leverage do those interventions have?
- What feedback loops do you see functioning in your movement? Which reinforcing loops are creating multiplication? Which balancing loops are maintaining coherence?
- Where do you see delays in your system? How are you accounting for them? What interventions have you made that haven't shown results yet?
- If you were to identify the highest leverage point in your movement right now, what would it be? What would it mean to intervene there?
- What would it mean to strengthen the reinforcing loops in your movement? What would that look like? What would need to change?
- What balancing loops are maintaining coherence in your movement? Are they too strong or too weak? How do you know?

**Conversation Objectives:**
- Participants identify where they're currently intervening and assess the leverage of those interventions
- Participants recognize feedback loops functioning in their movement
- Participants identify leverage points where they could intervene for greater impact
- Participants discover how to strengthen reinforcing loops and balance balancing loops
- Participants identify one concrete leverage point where they could intervene
