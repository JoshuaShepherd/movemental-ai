# Case studies & stories — APEST

## 1. Early church (Ephesians 4)
- **Type:** Biblical.
- **Summary:** Paul names the five gifts Christ gave for the maturity and mission of the body. The church is to attain the “fullness of Christ” as all functions operate.
- **Source:** Ephesians 4:11–16; *5Q*, *The Permanent Revolution*.

## 2. Wesley and the Methodists
- **Type:** Historical.
- **Summary:** Wesley functioned apostolically; the movement activated multiple functions (evangelistic preaching, shepherding classes, teaching, prophetic challenge). All five were present in the movement.
- **Source:** *The Forgotten Ways*; movement case studies.

## 3. Contemporary APEST activation
- **Type:** Contemporary.
- **Summary:** Churches that intentionally value and release all five functions report better adaptation, mission, and health. Use local or anonymized examples when available.
- **Source:** faq-012; practical-guides.
