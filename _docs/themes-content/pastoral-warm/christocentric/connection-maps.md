# Connection maps — Christ at the Center

## Missional Church (mDNA)
Jesus Is Lord is the first element of mDNA—the center from which all others radiate. *Theme: missional-church.*

## Disciple Making
Discipleship is formation into the image of Christ; he is the content and goal. *Theme: disciple-making.*

## Formation
Formation is Christ-shaped; we are formed into his likeness. *Theme: formation.*

## Kingdom Mission
The kingdom is the kingdom of Christ; mission flows from his lordship. *Theme: kingdom-mission.*

## Reframation
Re-enchanting our vision of God and mission; putting new frames around our picture of God. *Portal: reframation.*

## Metanoia
Christo-logic—seeing the world from inside the mind of Christ; transformation toward his mind. *Portal: metanoia.*
