# Connection maps — Disciple Making

## Missional Church (mDNA)
Disciple-Making is the second element of mDNA; multiplication through formation. *Theme: missional-church.*

## Christ at the Center
Discipleship is formation into the image of Christ; “Jesus Is Lord” is the center. *Theme: christocentric.*

## Formation
Formation is the process of being shaped into Christ’s image; communitas and liminality support disciple-making. *Theme: formation.*

## APEST
Fivefold ministry equips the saints for ministry; every disciple has a role in the body. *Theme: apest.*

## Kingdom Mission
Disciple-making extends the kingdom; mission and discipleship are inseparable. *Theme: kingdom-mission.*
