# Hero & overview — Disciple Making

## Title
Disciple Making

## Subtitle (eyebrow)
Making disciples who make disciples

## Description
Making disciples who make disciples: frameworks and practices that put formation at the center, so evangelism and mission flow from a life that looks like Jesus.

## Hero quote
> The Great Commission explicitly commands us to "make disciples," and evangelism happens naturally within that process.

## Citation
*Disciplism*; ref. Matthew 28:19–20
