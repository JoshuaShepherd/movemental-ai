# Case studies & stories — Formation

## 1. Early church: communitas through persecution and mission
- **Type:** Historical.
- **Summary:** The early church experienced communitas through persecution, mission, and the radical demands of following Jesus. Acts and the epistles describe a community forged in risk and shared purpose, not comfort.
- **Source:** *The Forgotten Ways* ch. 7; story-index 01-HISTORICAL-MOVEMENT-STORIES.

## 2. Paul and his apostolic band (2 Corinthians)
- **Type:** Biblical.
- **Summary:** Paul describes whippings, beatings, imprisonment, shipwrecks—liminal experience that formed deep bonds with his companions and churches. Communitas in action.
- **Source:** *The Faith of Leap*; thematic-deep-dives 05-community-and-communitas.

## 3. Formation cohorts (contemporary)
- **Type:** Contemporary.
- **Summary:** When courses or cohorts include shared commitment, accountability, and mission (not just content), participants often report deeper formation and lasting bonds—communitas. Use local or anonymized examples when available.
- **Source:** Course design docs; story-index 02-CONTEMPORARY-EXAMPLES.
