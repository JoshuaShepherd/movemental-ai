# Connection maps — Formation

## Missional Church (mDNA)
Communitas is the sixth element of mDNA. Formation that includes communitas strengthens the whole system. *Theme: missional-church.*

## Disciple-Making
Formation is the pathway of discipleship; discipleship is formation into the image of Christ. *Theme: disciple-making.*

## Christ at the Center
Formation is Christ-centered—we are formed into the image of Jesus, and “Jesus Is Lord” is the center of a formation journey. *Theme: christocentric.*

## Liminality & Communitas (portal)
Dedicated portal content on liminality and communitas in content-research (subportal under mDNA). *Portal: liminality-communitas.*

## Metanoia
Transformation (metanoia) and formation overlap: both involve change of mind, heart, and practice. *Portal: metanoia.*

## The Faith of Leap (book)
Primary book on adventure, risk, and communitas; key resource for this theme.
