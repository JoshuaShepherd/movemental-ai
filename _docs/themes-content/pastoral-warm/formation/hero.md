# Hero & overview — Formation

## Title
Formation

## Subtitle (eyebrow)
Spiritual and leadership formation for disciples and leaders

## Description
Spiritual and leadership formation for disciples and leaders—including the kind of community that forms when people share a liminal journey: communitas, risk, and the edge where growth happens.

## Hero quote
> The most vigorous forms of community are those that come together in the context of a shared ordeal or those that deliberately initiate a risky journey.

## Citation
*The Forgotten Ways*, Chapter 7 — Liminality and Communitas
