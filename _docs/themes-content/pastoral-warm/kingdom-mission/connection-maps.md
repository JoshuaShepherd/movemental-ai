# Connection maps — Kingdom Mission

## Missional Church (mDNA)
The missional-incarnational impulse is the third element of mDNA (go out, go deep). *Theme: missional-church.*

## Disciple Making
Mission and discipleship are inseparable; kingdom mission multiplies disciples. *Theme: disciple-making.*

## Christ at the Center
The kingdom is the kingdom of Christ; “Jesus Is Lord” shapes mission. *Theme: christocentric.*

## Formation
Communitas and formation happen in the context of mission and risk. *Theme: formation.*

## Movement Intelligence (mQ)
Multiplication and movement quality; kingdom mission leads to movemental outcomes. *Portal: movement-intelligence.*

## Reframation
Romancing the city; cultural engagement; listening and partnership. *Portal: reframation.*
