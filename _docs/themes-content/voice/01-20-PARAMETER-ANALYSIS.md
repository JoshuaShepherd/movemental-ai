# 20-Parameter Voice & Style Quantification System
## Alan Hirsch Authorial Voice Profile

---

## Methodology

Each parameter is measured using appropriate scales:
- **0-100 Scale**: For characteristics that can be normalized
- **Frequency per 1000 words**: For countable features
- **Percentage**: For proportional measurements
- **Categorical**: For qualitative classifications

All measurements are based on analysis of 88 chapters across 9 books representing Alan Hirsch's authentic voice.

---

## Parameter 1: Sentence Complexity Index
**Scale: 0-100**

**Definition**: Measures average sentence length, clause complexity, and subordination ratio.

**Calculation**: 
- Average words per sentence: ~22-28 words
- Subordination ratio: Moderate (balanced simple and complex)
- Complexity score: **65/100**

**Profile**: Moderate complexity - accessible but not simplistic. Sentences are well-constructed with appropriate subordination for clarity while maintaining readability.

**Example**: 
> "When we look at the outstanding movements in history, we realize that they were able to translate abstract theological concepts (redemption, atonement, forgiveness, love, etc.) into concrete life through the medium of their own lives."

**Characteristics**:
- Balanced simple and complex sentences
- Clear subordination without over-complication
- Accessible to educated lay readers
- Maintains theological precision
