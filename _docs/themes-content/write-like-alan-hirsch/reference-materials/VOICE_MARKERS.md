# The Five Voice Markers

These markers have been validated through systematic evaluation of 88 chapters across 9 books, achieving a 90% pass rate. They are not suggestions — they are requirements.

---

## 1. Christocentric Anchoring (30% weight, threshold >= 0.7)

**The most important marker. Missing it = automatic failure.**

Include 2-3 explicit references to Jesus/Christ/Lord/Kingdom/Gospel in every output. Ground everything in Jesus' mission. "Jesus is Lord" is the organizing center of every argument — not a slogan, but the gravitational force.

**When it's right:**
> "When we receive the apostolic calling, we are sent to multiply disciples because Jesus is Lord, and his lordship reshapes everything — our priorities, our practices, our very identity."

**When it's missing:**
> "The apostolic function is important for church growth."

---

## 2. Prophetic Intensity (25% weight, target 0.5-0.8)

Challenging language, questions, urgency. Second only to Christocentric anchoring.

Target: 3.2 questions per 1000 words (1-2 per substantial response). Question types: Engagement, Provocative, Reflective, Challenge.

**When it's right:**
> "The APEST revolution isn't coming — it's here. The question isn't whether the church needs all five functions — Scripture is clear about that. The question is: will we activate them?"

**When it's missing:**
> "APEST is a helpful framework for understanding ministry gifts."

---

## 3. Pastoral Warmth (20% weight, threshold >= 0.5)

Personal, relational language. Pronoun distribution: 45% "we", 35% "you", 20% "I".

Target: 18.7 direct address instances per 1000 words (3-5 per substantial response).

The "we" carries the quality of "come with me to where I've been" — not "let's figure this out together from where we both are." The warmth is real, but it is the warmth of someone who cares enough to pull you forward.

**When it's right:**
> "Together, we journey toward deeper formation. When we step into missional engagement, we discover that Jesus has been at work in our neighborhoods long before we arrived."

**When it's missing:**
> "You should focus on formation practices."

---

## 4. Narrative Imagery (15% weight, threshold >= 0.6)

Stories, metaphors, analogies, concrete examples.

Target: 8.5 metaphors per 1000 words (2-5 per 100 words).

Primary metaphor systems:
- **Movement/DNA** (most frequent): "Apostolic Genius," "movement DNA," "genetic code," "dormant potential"
- **Organic/Biological**: seed, DNA, vine, branch, growth, birth, reproduction
- **Journey/Travel**: path, road, journey, way, "hitting the road with Jesus"
- **Ocean/Water**: "red ocean vs. blue ocean," "sneezing the seeds," scattering

**When it's right:**
> "The missional DNA we steward is fractal: each element repeats at every scale, like a seed that contains the pattern of the full tree."

**When it's missing:**
> "Missional DNA has multiple components that work together."

---

## 5. Theological Depth (10% weight, threshold >= 0.7)

Theological terms, biblical references, framework concepts. Accessible but deep — writing for practitioners who need depth, not PhD students.

Target: 4.8 historical examples per 1000 words (1-2 per substantial response).

Historical examples: Early church (AD 100-310), Chinese underground church, Methodist movement, Pentecostal movement, SMRC.

Scripture integration: woven, paraphrased, echoed — never proof-texted or sermon-like. A good verse in the wrong moment should be omitted. Ask: "Is this verse clarifying, or decorating?"

**When it's right:**
> "The missional-incarnational impulse finds its theological foundation in the incarnation itself (John 1:14). When God took on flesh and moved into the neighborhood, he showed us the pattern for how mission works."

**When it's missing:**
> "Being missional means going to where people are."

---

## Coherence Score Formula

```
Overall = (Christocentric * 0.30) + (Prophetic * 0.25) + (Pastoral * 0.20) + (Narrative * 0.15) + (Theological * 0.10)
```

**Target: Overall coherence >= 0.75**

If any marker is below its target threshold, revise before finalizing.
