### The Maturity Model: Five Levels of AI Engagement
**Source:** book

Level 1 (Awareness): aware but not engaged. Level 2 (Experimentation): using AI for low-stakes work, learning, playing. ## Core Claim

Before launching a leader on Movemental, a thoughtful onboarding should assess: (1) content maturity (how much existing content is there to organize?); (2) platform maturity (how developed is their digital presence?); (3) time and team constraints (are they solo or with a team?); (4) AI maturity (where are they on the five-stage model?); (5) audience size and type (who's already following them?). This assessment shapes the engagement model—some leaders might need a full sprint (Discovery Lab + Governance + Content track), others might start with content organization only. Customized onboarding increases adoption and success.
