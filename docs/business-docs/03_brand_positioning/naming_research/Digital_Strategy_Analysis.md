## Final Digital Strategy Recommendations

### Choose MQ If:
- You want to build a completely new, tech-forward brand
- You prefer lower digital competition and cleaner SEO
- You want maximum brandability and international appeal
- You're comfortable with 12-18 month brand building timeline
- You want to create new category/methodology

### Choose Movemental If:
- You want to leverage existing brand recognition
- You prefer faster market entry and community connection
- You want to own an established category
- You're comfortable with moderate competition
- You want authentic connection to your target audience

Both options are digitally viable with strong implementation pathways. The choice depends on whether you prefer building a new movement (MQ) or owning an existing one (Movemental).
