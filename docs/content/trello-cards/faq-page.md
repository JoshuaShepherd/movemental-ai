# FAQ Page

**Section:** Conversion Support (Trust + Decision-Making)


❓ FAQ PAGE (FULL DRAFT)

⸻

Hero

Questions worth answering.

⸻

Is this software?

No.

Movemental is not something you sign up for and use on your own.

It’s a system we build with you, based on your work or your organization.

⸻

How long does this take?

Initial systems are built over a focused period, typically around four weeks depending on scope.

From there, the system continues to evolve over time.

⸻

Do I need technical knowledge?

No.

You bring your work, your knowledge, or your organization.

We handle the system-building process.

⸻

Is this just AI?

No.

AI is one part of the system.

The core is structure:
	•	content
	•	pathways
	•	courses
	•	engagement

AI supports that structure.

⸻

Who is this for?
	•	Movement leaders with a body of work
	•	Nonprofits building systems
	•	Churches and institutions with teaching or formation

⸻

What if I’m not sure yet?

That’s expected.

This is not something most people have seen before.

The conversation exists to determine whether this makes sense for your situation.

⸻

Do I keep ownership of my work?

Yes.

For movement leaders, you own everything that is built.

Movemental participates through a revenue share, not ownership.

⸻

Is this proven?

The core system has already been built and is working.

Some applications—especially with organizations—are actively being developed and refined.
