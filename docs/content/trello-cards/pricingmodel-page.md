# Pricing/Model Page

**Section:** Conversion Support (Trust + Decision-Making)


PRICING / MODEL PAGE (FULL DRAFT)

⸻

Hero

Aligned with what this actually produces.

Movemental is not priced like software or consulting.
It’s structured around outcomes.

⸻

For Movement Leaders

You don’t pay for access.
You invest in building a system.
	•	$1,000–$4,000 upfront (increasing over time)
	•	10% of all revenue generated through your system
	•	no ongoing subscription fees

You keep 90%.
You own your work.

⸻

Why it works this way

What’s being built here would normally require:
	•	a development team
	•	content strategists
	•	infrastructure
	•	ongoing support

That has historically been out of reach for individuals.

Movemental changes that by aligning around outcomes instead of charging for components.

If your system works, we both benefit.

⸻

For Organizations

Each system is built as a focused engagement.

Typical pricing:
	•	$5,000 — Governance, AI Lab, or foundational systems
	•	$10,000 — Content or Fundraising systems

Delivered through structured, four-week builds.

⸻

What you’re paying for

Not access to a tool.

Not a report or recommendation.

You are paying for:
	•	a working system
	•	documentation
	•	a trained team
	•	a structure you can continue using

⸻

Why this is different

Most organizations pay for:
	•	software they have to figure out
	•	consulting that doesn’t get implemented

Movemental is structured so that:

the work is done by the time you’re finished
