# Start a Conversation Page/Flow

**Section:** Conversion Action


START A CONVERSATION PAGE (FULL DRAFT)

⸻

Hero

This starts with a conversation.

Movemental is not something you sign up for.

It’s something we build—with you, around your work or your organization.

⸻

What this is

This is not a demo.

It’s not a pitch.

It’s a working conversation to figure out whether there’s something here worth building.

⸻

What we’ll do together

In this conversation, we’ll:
•	understand what you’ve built or what your organization does
•	map what a system could look like in your context
•	clarify whether this is a fit

If it is, the next step is clear.

If it’s not, that will be clear too.

⸻

Who this is for

This conversation is designed for:
•	movement leaders with a body of work
•	nonprofit leaders responsible for systems or strategy
•	churches or institutions with teaching and formation

⸻

Before you book (???)

You don’t need to prepare anything.

But it helps if you can describe:
•	what you’ve built (or what your organization does)
•	where things feel fragmented or limited
•	what you wish existed but doesn’t

⸻

What happens after (we schedule the onboarding course)

If there’s a fit:
•	we outline the process
•	define the scope
•	and move into building

If not:
•	you leave with clarity about why

⸻
