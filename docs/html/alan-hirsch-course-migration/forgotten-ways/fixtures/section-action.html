Week 4 · Action

One concrete step this week

Not abstract reflection. Name one thing you will do before the next cohort call — and tell someone.

The prompt: Identify one person you are already in relationship with who could step into a disciple-making conversation with you this week. Ask them one question. Listen to the answer. Offer one practice in response.

Guidance: Keep it small enough that you cannot avoid it because of scheduling. Keep it specific enough that you cannot slip past it with "I've been thinking about" language.

Your commitment is saved to your journal and shown to your facilitator only.
