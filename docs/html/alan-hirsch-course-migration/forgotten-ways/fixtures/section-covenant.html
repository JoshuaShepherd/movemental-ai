Our cohort covenant

A brief, shared pledge that governs how we show up for one another over these eight weeks.

We covenant to:

  • Show up to each cohort call on time and with the week's work attempted.
  • Listen well before speaking well. Hold space for dissonance before rushing to resolution.
  • Keep private what is shared here — names, stories, and wounds — unless explicitly released.
  • Invite one another into honest assessment of our ministry patterns, without flattery.
  • Pray for one another by name between calls.
  • Receive the commissioning rite at the end — not as a ceremony, but as a threshold.
