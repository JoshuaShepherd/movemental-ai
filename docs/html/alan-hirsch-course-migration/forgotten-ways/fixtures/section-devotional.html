Week 2 · Opening

Lordship — Week opening devotional

Set the week in the posture that will carry it: "Jesus is Lord."

Before you open a single email, before you set a single agenda, say it out loud: Jesus is Lord. Say it until it lands in the body. Say it in the first-person plural: "We follow Jesus. We follow no other."

This is not a pious preamble. It is the gravitational correction without which the rest of the week drifts.

Closing devotional (end of week)

Revisit the declaration. Where did the gravitational center hold this week? Where did it slip? Name both without shame. The honest naming is itself a kind of worship.
