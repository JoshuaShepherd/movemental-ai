APEST Culture — prompts for your cohort

Bring one response to each prompt to your next 90-minute cohort call.

Prompt 1 · Cultural

Which of the five are exiled?

In your community, which of apostolic, prophetic, evangelistic, shepherding, or teaching gifts do you most often silence? Why?

Prompt 2 · Relational

Who already carries a gift you haven't named?

Name one person who operates in one of these gifts who has never been formally recognized. What would change for them if they heard you name it?

Prompt 3 · Structural

What structure suppresses the gifts?

Identify one meeting, budget line, or role description that currently suppresses at least one of the five. What would it take to loosen it this quarter?

Facilitator note. Don't rush to resolution. The prompts work best when the cohort holds the discomfort long enough for the structural truth to surface.
