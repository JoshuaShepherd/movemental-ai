Week 5 · Field Experiment

Incarnational presence

Spend three hours in a nearby third place without an agenda and without an evangelism strategy.

Setup. Choose a specific coffee shop, park, gym, or barbershop within walking distance of where you serve. Go alone. Bring nothing but what you would bring if you were simply there.

Expected outcomes

  • You will be bored before you are fruitful. Wait it out.
  • At least one conversation will emerge that you did not engineer.
  • You will notice at least one assumption about this place or these people you did not realize you carried.

Instructions

  1. Arrive with no script.
  2. Sit in a posture that does not signal "leave me alone."
  3. Stay for at least three hours. Do not leave when you get restless.
  4. Journal what you noticed before you leave the building.
