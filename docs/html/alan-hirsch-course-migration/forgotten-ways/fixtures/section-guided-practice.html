Week 3 · Guided Practice

Three-person covenant group

A simple, reproducible rhythm that any ordinary disciple can carry — meet weekly with two others around three questions.

  1. What is God saying to you? Describe what you've heard in Scripture, prayer, or lived experience this week — in one paragraph, not a sermon.
  2. What are you doing about it? Name the one step you've taken or will take. If the answer is "nothing," name what's in the way.
  3. Who are you helping toward Jesus? Identify one person you're praying for, listening to, or walking alongside.

Checkpoints

  • Meet for 45 minutes, not 90.
  • Everyone speaks to each question; no one dominates.
  • The third question is mandatory — this is how the genetic code starts to move through you to others.
  • End with one sentence of prayer per person.
