Week 8 · Journey Continues

The journey continues beyond this cohort

A closing word on what "finished" means in a course whose real test is what happens in the next decade.

You are not graduating. You are being commissioned.

Apostolic Genius is not something we finish learning. It is something the church keeps recovering, in every generation and in every place, because every generation and every place is tempted to cover it over again. You have the diagnostic, the practices, and a cohort who know what you are trying to recover.

Three commitments to carry forward:

  • Reassess your community's mDNA every six months with the same four-level scale.
  • Name the weakest strand and work on only that one for the next season.
  • Call one cohort member every quarter and ask them the same question you were asked in Week 1.
Commissioning line. "Go — not with certainty that you know the way, but with the Lord of every way."
