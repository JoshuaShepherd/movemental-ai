Week 2 · Exercise

Lordship Inventory

A 90-minute exercise that surfaces where the gravitational center of your community has drifted.

Purpose

Name the rhythms where Jesus actually gets the last word, and those where another lord has quietly taken up residence.

Time

~90 minutes across this week. Bring the findings to your cohort call.

Instructions

  1. Walk through five rhythms of your community: budgets, staff agendas, preaching, discipleship, and external partnerships.
  2. For each rhythm, name what currently gets the last word.
  3. Flag each rhythm green (Christ is Lord), yellow (mixed), or red (drifted).
  4. Note one specific place where you would re-center allegiance to Jesus this quarter.

Deliverables

  • A one-page inventory with color codings.
  • One specific re-centering commitment scoped to a rhythm.
  • A sentence you're willing to read aloud in your cohort.
