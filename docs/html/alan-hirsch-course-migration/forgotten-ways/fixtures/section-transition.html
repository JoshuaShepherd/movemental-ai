Between Week 5 & Week 6 · Transition

A narrative bridge

Short connective prose that names the theological hinge between weeks.

Until now, we have asked what the church is — Lord, disciples, mission, posture. From here, we ask what the church does with its distributed intelligence.

The APEST conversation will press on your structures. Most churches have unconsciously organized around the shepherd-teacher gifts because those gifts match an attendance-and-instruction logic. The question of the next two weeks is whether you are willing to let the apostolic, prophetic, and evangelistic return.

Carry this question forward: "Which of the five gifts have we exiled — and who in our midst has learned to suppress that gifting because we would not make room for it?"
