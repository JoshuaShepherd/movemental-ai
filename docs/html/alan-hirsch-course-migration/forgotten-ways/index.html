Methodology

The Weekly Engine

Every week, the cohort undergoes a rigorous 7-step pedagogical loop designed to move knowledge from the head to the hands.

  1. 01

    Dissonance

    A jarring encounter with the missional reality.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Theology that addresses the disruption.

  3. 03

    Witness

    Observing the concept lived out in practice.

  4. 04

    Practice

    Direct action in your specific context.

  5. 05

    Reflection

    Theological processing of the experience.

  6. 06

    Cohort Learning

    Shared insights and mutual accountability.

  7. 07

    Integration

    Anchoring the new rhythm into daily life.
