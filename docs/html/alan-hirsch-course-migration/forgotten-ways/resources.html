Glossary

Apostolic Genius

The latent potential and force within the people of God that, when activated, leads to transformative missional movement.

mDNA

Missional DNA: the six elements that encode Apostolic Genius — Jesus is Lord, Disciple making, Missional-incarnational impulse, Apostolic environment, Organic systems, and Communitas.

Communitas

Community forged in the context of shared ordeal, risk, or mission; deeper than mere fellowship.

APEST

Apostolic, Prophetic, Evangelistic, Shepherding, Teaching — the fivefold ministry gifts of Ephesians 4.
