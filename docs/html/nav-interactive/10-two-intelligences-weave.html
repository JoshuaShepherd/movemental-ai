FOR ORGANIZATIONAL LEADERS
NONPROFITS · CHURCHES · INSTITUTIONS

AI is already inside your organization[1].

AI is being used by people inside your organization right now[2]. Most leaders have not yet decided what is safe, valuable, or ethical for their work. The four-stage path below is how you decide, in writing, before you build anything else.

I
STAGE 1

Safety

Decide in writing what AI may and may not do in your organization, before anyone else decides for you.
II
STAGE 2

Sandbox

Disciplined exploration of valuable AI use cases and ethical concerns, without the risks of publication and privacy issues.
III
STAGE 3

Skills

Formation that produces leaders, not just users.
IV
STAGE 4

Solutions

AI integrated into how the organization actually runs, owned by formed people, governed by working policy.
