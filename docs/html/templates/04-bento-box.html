§ 01 — Premise

02 of 09

The mirror and the amplifier

AI is trained on the broad output of human writing, speech, and behavior. When you interact with it, it mirrors and amplifies what it has been trained on, and it mirrors and amplifies you specifically.

People worried about AI are often worried about humanity itself. People hopeful about AI are often hopeful about humanity. The most important thing an organization can do is deliberately gather the right humans.

Reframe

AI surfaces and amplifies what humans have already produced.

§ 02 — The Acid Test

Portable · Falsifiable

The “take AI out of the story” test

Take the AI out of the story. Look at what remains.

When the answer is yes

If what remains is people doing valuable human work that AI helped them do faster, larger, or more accessibly — the use is humanly valuable.

When the answer is no

If what remains is just AI output dressed up to look like human work — the use is suspect, regardless of how impressive the output is.

Applied to ourselves: what remains is a group of practitioners who had been circling each other’s work for years. The technology made it possible to gather. The technology did not make the gathering valuable.

§ 03 — Decision framework

Green, yellow, and red lights.

A three-tier deployment framework. Coarse-grained on purpose.

The shape of every guardrail

A human with judgment is the last hand on the work before it leaves the building.

Green

Clear human benefit, no meaningful negative consequence.

  • — Make content searchable
  • — Translate languages
  • — Surface data patterns
Yellow

Real benefit, but only with sufficient guardrails.

  • — Reviewed drafts
  • — Cited research
  • — Facilitation, not substitution
Red

Harm, regardless of how carefully it is deployed.

  • — Authoring under a name
  • — Eligibility decisions
  • — Pastoral impersonation
  • — Surveillance

§ 04 — Conclusion

We are not an AI company.

We are a company of humans who use AI as carefully as we can, in service of a network of movement leaders whose work matters.

The vendors who will help them are mostly not the people who understand the substance of what they do. That head start is what we offer.
