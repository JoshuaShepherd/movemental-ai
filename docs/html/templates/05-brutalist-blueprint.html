§ 00
Premise

The Editorial Covenant

How Movemental
approaches AI.

Most public discourse splits into “AI is a savior” or “AI is a threat.” We reject both.

AI is infrastructure. The leaders are the work. This page outlines the framing, the tests, and the boundaries that govern how we use AI at Movemental.

§ 01
Mirror

The mirror & amplifier

What gets reflected back is what was put in.

AI is trained on the broad output of human writing, speech, and behavior. When you interact with it, it mirrors and amplifies what it has been trained on, and it mirrors and amplifies you specifically.

The outputs you get back reflect both the training data and the human in the room. People who are worried about AI are often worried about humanity itself, because AI surfaces and amplifies what humans have already produced.

People who are hopeful about AI are often hopeful about humanity for the same reason. If AI mirrors and amplifies the humans it is around, the most important thing an organization can do is deliberately gather the right humans.

The thing people are saying yes to when they join Movemental is not the technology. It is each other. The platform exists to make that possible at a scale that was not possible before.

§ 02
Acid Test

The “take AI out of the story” test

Take the AI out
of the story.
Look at what remains.

If yes — the use is humanly valuable

If what remains is people doing valuable human work that AI helped them do faster, larger, or more accessibly.

If no — the use is suspect

If what remains is just AI output dressed up to look like human work — regardless of how impressive the output is.

Applied to our own work: what remains is a group of practitioners who had been circling each other’s work for years and who wanted to do something together. The technology made it possible to gather. The technology did not make the gathering valuable.

§ 03
Framework

Decision framework — three tiers

Green, yellow, and red lights.

A three-tier deployment framework. Coarse-grained on purpose — the goal is fast judgment in service of careful work, not bureaucratic adjudication.

Tier
Posture
Decision rule
Examples
Green
Deploy readily
Clear human benefit, no meaningful negative consequence.
3 examples

The washing machine of AI applications. When the question is “does this expand human capacity without trading anything important away?” the answer is yes. We use it. We do not write a memo about it.

  • Ex 01 AI used to make existing content searchable.
  • Ex 02 AI used to translate between languages a person does not speak.
  • Ex 03 AI used to surface patterns in data that would otherwise take weeks to find.
Yellow
Deploy with guardrails
Real benefit, but only with sufficient guardrails.
3 examples

The shape of the guardrail is always the same: a human with judgment is the last hand on the work before it leaves the building.

  • Ex 01 AI used to draft communications that humans review before sending.
  • Ex 02 AI used to help with research where citation and verification are required.
  • Ex 03 AI used to facilitate group conversations where the AI never substitutes for participants’ actual contributions.
Red
Do not deploy
Harm, regardless of how carefully the use is deployed.
4 examples

These are not edge cases we will figure out later. They are stop-work conditions.

  • Ex 01 AI used to author content under a human’s name without disclosure.
  • Ex 02 AI used to make eligibility decisions that affect people’s access to services.
  • Ex 03 AI used to impersonate humans in pastoral or counseling contexts.
  • Ex 04 AI used to surveil people.
§ 04
Conclusion

Closing position

We are not an
AI company.

We are a company of humans who use AI as carefully as we can, in service of a network of movement leaders whose work matters. AI is the infrastructure. The leaders are the work.

The reason we are doing this now is that AI cannot be put back. Every organization our network serves is going to face the same questions about AI, whether they want to or not.

The vendors who will help them are mostly not the people who understand the substance of what they do.

We are a few years into wrestling with these questions in a field where the most experienced person could only have three years of experience. That head start is what we offer.
