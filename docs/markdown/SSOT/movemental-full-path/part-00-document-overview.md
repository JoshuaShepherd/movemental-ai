# Document overview (cover + how to use)
AI Has Already Arrived A documentation of the AI reality facing churches, nonprofits, and institutions in 2026 The minimum viable Safety checklist that responds to it And the working source-of-truth for productizing the work Extended in this edition to Sandbox Discovery, Skills Development, and Solutions Deployment A Movemental field paper and internal source document · May 2026 How to use this document This document serves three audiences and three purposes simultaneously, and is structured so that pieces of it can be extracted for each. For organizational leaders considering the path, Parts One and Two are the reading. They document the AI reality facing organizations like theirs and define the minimum work that responsibly addresses that reality. These two parts can be lifted out as a public-facing field guide. For Movemental internal use, Part Three is the working layer for the Safety stage. For each of the seven Safety items, Part Three contains the actual boilerplate text that can be templated, the decisions the organization must make to customize the boilerplate, and Movemental's specific role at each productization level. This is the source of truth from which the free toolkit, the AI-assisted product, the facilitated engagement, and any future iteration are built. When the team builds the free downloadable toolkit, Part Three section A1 (boilerplate) and A2 (decisions) for each item are the source. When the team builds the AI-assisted product, Part Three section A2 (decisions) becomes the intake structure and section A1 (boilerplate) becomes the generation template. When a facilitator runs a $5,000 engagement, Part Three section A3 (Movemental role at facilitated level) is the working brief. The document is meant to be a single point of update — change boilerplate here, and downstream artifacts update from this source. Part Four contains the productization strategy and a reference cross-walk that maps each item to each level of delivery, with notes on what work is required where. Parts Five through Eight extend the document to the remaining three stages of the Movemental AI Path. Part Five covers Sandbox Discovery — the four-week exploratory engagement with the traffic-light evaluation system, the Use Case Portfolio, the Discernment Memo, and the Readiness Assessment. Part Six covers Skills Development — the eight-module formational course built on Movemental's LMS infrastructure. Part Seven covers Solutions Deployment — the technological deployment with five configurations and the readiness threshold for proceeding. Part Eight is the cross-stage productization view that ties Safety, Sandbox, Skills, and Solutions into a single coherent operational and commercial structure.

The pattern across all four stages is the same: each stage produces specific artifacts; each artifact has boilerplate text, decision questions, and a Movemental role-by-level specification. The path is one path. This document keeps it coherent.
