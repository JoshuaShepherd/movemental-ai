# Part four: productization strategy and cross-walk
This part documents the strategic logic that places each of the seven items at the right productization level, and provides a cross-walk reference for how Movemental delivers Safety across the three levels. Strategic logic Movemental's position is that AI built on a coherent foundation compounds rather than corrodes. The Safety stage operationalizes that position: it is the stage where the foundation gets built. This creates a strategic alignment opportunity. Movemental should productize Safety in ways that demonstrate the company's thesis rather than contradict it. Specifically: an AI-assisted product that helps organizations build their AI foundation is the most coherent possible expression of the company's philosophy. It says with action what we say with words. At the same time, Safety is the most accessible-by-design stage of the path. The documented harms apply to every organization in the sector. Withholding the framework behind a paywall would be inconsistent with the missional posture of the leaders we serve and the communities they lead. Open- sourcing the framework, while productizing the implementation, is the right balance: framework freely given, work delivered at multiple price points. The three levels of productization correspond to three buyer segments and three trust thresholds. The Free Toolkit serves organizations who cannot afford engagement and who would not pay for it; it builds top-of-funnel credibility and aligns Movemental with the missional generosity expected in the sector.

The AI-Assisted Product serves organizations with budget but a preference for self-service; it captures revenue from a segment significantly larger than facilitation can serve and demonstrates the agentic infrastructure as a customer-facing product. The Facilitated MVP serves organizations who want speed, external coordination, and a relationship with Movemental for downstream stages; it is the relationship- building tier that converts into Sandbox, Skills, and Solutions engagements. Cross-walk reference table The following describes Movemental's role for each of the seven items at each of the three levels. This is the operational reference for product configuration, facilitator training, and pricing decisions. Item 1 — AI Use Charter Free Toolkit: Boilerplate document plus decision worksheet, no human support. AI-Assisted Product: Structured intake plus AI generation plus async Q&A support. Facilitated MVP: 90-minute leadership conversation plus drafted charter plus board ratification support. Item 2 — Acceptable Use Policy Free Toolkit: Boilerplate plus decision worksheet plus AI tools reference card. AI-Assisted Product: Intake plus AI generation plus staff summary card plus quarterly-updated tools reference. Facilitated MVP: 60- minute working session plus drafted policy plus customized staff summary card plus leader talking script. Item 3 — Data Handling Standards Free Toolkit: Sector-specific boilerplate plus mapping worksheet (organization completes inventory themselves). AI-Assisted Product: Intake plus AI generation plus guided mapping workflow (organization still completes inventory). Facilitated MVP: 90-minute data inventory session plus drafted standards plus completed appendix mapping plus high-risk flow remediation note. Item 4 — Disclosure and Attribution Standard Free Toolkit: Boilerplate plus decision worksheet plus three sample standing notices. AI-Assisted Product: Intake plus AI generation plus standing notice paste-ready copy plus footnote template plus staff reference. Facilitated MVP: 30-minute trust conversation plus drafted standard plus all customized supporting copy. Item 5 — Care Boundaries Free Toolkit: Three sector-specific boilerplate documents plus decision worksheet plus sourced reflections. (Most resistant to productization.) AI-Assisted Product: Intake plus AI generation plus optional add-on $300 facilitator consultation specifically for this item plus theological-review flag for senior leadership. Facilitated MVP: 90-minute pastorally-framed conversation plus deeply customized boundaries document. (Most theologically substantive item.)

Item 6 — Voice Cloning Response Plan Free Toolkit: Boilerplate plus verification notice plus tabletop exercise script plus reporting URLs. AI Assisted Product: Intake plus AI generation plus pre-drafted communication templates plus tabletop script plus annual practice calendar reminder. Facilitated MVP: 45-minute working session plus drafted plan plus first tabletop exercise run by facilitator. Item 7 — Communication to Constituent Base Free Toolkit: Boilerplate letter plus decision worksheet plus three sample alternate forms. AI-Assisted Product: Intake plus AI generation plus optional voice-tuning capability that adapts the letter to the senior leader's actual voice from a writing sample. Facilitated MVP: 45-minute conversation with senior leader plus drafted letter in leader's voice plus rehearsal support. Build sequence and effort estimate If Movemental commits to building all three levels, the recommended build sequence and effort estimate is: Phase 1 (week 1): Refine Parts One, Two, and Three of this document into a designed downloadable Field Guide. Publish on movemental.ai as the Free Toolkit, gated by email signup. Total effort: roughly 30 hours including design. Phase 2 (weeks 2-4): Build v1 of the AI-Assisted Product. The seven intake structures are derived from the decision questions in Part Three. The seven generation templates are derived from the boilerplate text in Part Three. The product is implemented as either a structured web form connected to a Claude or comparable model, or as a Claude Skill, depending on the platform that fits Movemental's existing infrastructure. Soft launch to existing leader network as beta. Total effort: roughly 80 hours of focused build given existing agentic infrastructure. Phase 3 (ongoing): Continue offering the $5,000 Facilitated MVP using Part Three as the working brief. Train any additional facilitators using Part Three as the source of truth. Iterate on boilerplate and decision questions based on real engagement data. Update protocol This document is the single source of truth. When boilerplate language is improved, when decision questions are refined, when sector-specific data shifts, or when Movemental's offering changes, the update happens here first. Downstream artifacts — the Field Guide, the AI-Assisted Product templates, the facilitator brief — are regenerated from this source. The document is reviewed quarterly and updated as the field evolves.
