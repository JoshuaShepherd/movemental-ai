# Part eight: cross-stage productization and integration
This part documents the strategic logic across the four stages of the path and provides the integration view that ties Safety, Sandbox, Skills, and Solutions into a coherent operational and commercial structure.

### Eight-A: The full path

The Movemental AI Path is one path, expressed in four stages. Each stage presupposes the work of the prior stage. Each stage produces specific artifacts that the subsequent stage can build on. The path is not a menu where customers pick favorites; it is a sequence with logic. At the same time, organizations enter the path at different points, hold different paces through it, and can pause between stages when their season requires it. The path is firm in structure and accommodating in pace.

- Safety. Two-week MVP. Seven artifacts. The non-negotiable foundation. Free Toolkit, AI-Assisted

Product (~$1,997), or Facilitated MVP ($5,000).

- Sandbox. Four weeks. Use Case Portfolio plus Discernment Memo plus Readiness Assessment. The exploratory disciplined discovery. Self-Service Toolkit (~$497), AI-Assisted Sandbox (~$3,997), or

Facilitated Sandbox Discovery ($15,000).

- Skills. Eight weeks. Eight modules. Practitioner-to-discerner formation. Self-Paced License ($4,800/year per organization), Cohort Facilitation ($15,000), or Train-the-Facilitator certification ($25,000).

- Solutions. 4-24 weeks depending on configuration. Five configurations from optimization to network deployment. Pricing from $30,000 to $200,000+ depending on scope. The technological deployment built on the upstream foundation.

- Network Engagement. From $60,000. Multi-stage path delivered across an institution and its member organizations. The configuration for denominational bodies, training networks, and multi- site institutions.

### Eight-B: The full pathway price

The full Movemental AI Path priced separately is approximately $65,000 ($5K Safety + $15K Sandbox + $15K Skills + $30K Solutions floor). Bundled as a complete path engagement, the price is $58,500 — a 10% discount that reflects engagement efficiency rather than discount-for-discount's-sake. Movemental does not require organizations to commit to the full path at intake. Most engagements begin at one stage and continue based on what emerges. The organization that completes Safety with us is more likely to engage Sandbox; the organization that completes Sandbox is overwhelmingly likely to continue to Skills or Solutions. The path sells itself by working.

### Eight-C: The productization strategy in summary

Movemental productizes the path at three levels, with one level adapted per stage where the work warrants: Free or low-cost self-service tier. Field Guide (free) for Safety. Sandbox Toolkit (~$497) for Sandbox. Self-Paced License ($4,800/year) for Skills. Scoping consultation ($5,000) for Solutions. The self-service tier serves the broadest market and builds top-of-funnel credibility. AI-Assisted product tier. Safety AI-Assisted (~$1,997). Sandbox AI-Assisted (~$3,997). Skills Self-Paced (which is the AI-assisted equivalent for this stage). Solutions does not have an AI-assisted tier because deployment work is irreducibly facilitated. The AI-assisted tier captures the middle market and demonstrates Movemental's thesis through product. Facilitated tier. Safety Facilitated MVP ($5,000). Sandbox Facilitated Discovery ($15,000). Skills Cohort Facilitation ($15,000). Solutions Configurations A through E ($30,000 to $200,000+). The facilitated tier serves organizations that want speed, external coordination, and a sustained relationship with Movemental. Network tier (institutions only). Network Engagements starting at $60,000 that span multiple stages and multiple member organizations under a shared institutional framework.

### Eight-D: The integration view

The four stages produce artifacts that compound. Safety produces the seven governance artifacts plus the constituent communication. Sandbox produces the Use Case Portfolio plus the Discernment Memo plus the Readiness Assessment, all referencing Safety. Skills produces personal commitments, adjudication documentation, and ongoing formation plans, all building on Sandbox findings and Safety documentation. Solutions produces the deployment, the deployment governance integration document, and the operational handoff, all explicitly grounded in everything upstream. At the end of the full path, the organization holds approximately 18-22 working artifacts that together constitute its AI infrastructure: governance documents, adjudicated use cases, formed staff, deployed capability, and a documented integration that connects all of it. This infrastructure is durable. It survives staff turnover. It survives technology changes. It survives the organization's next season of work. It is, quite literally, the foundation Movemental promised at the beginning of the path.

### Eight-E: The build sequence for Movemental over 12 months

If Movemental commits to building all four stages at all three productization levels over the next 12 months, the recommended sequence is: Months 1-2: Refine Safety. Publish the Field Guide. Build the AI-Assisted Safety Product v1. Continue offering Facilitated MVP. Approximately 110 hours of focused build time given existing infrastructure. Months 3-5: Build Sandbox infrastructure. Develop the Use Case Portfolio interface. Build the assessment artifact (Claude artifact). Configure the initial recipe library for churches, nonprofits, and institutions. Publish the Self-Service Toolkit. Build the AI-Assisted Sandbox tier. Approximately 200 hours of focused build time. Months 4-8 (overlapping with Sandbox): Build Skills course. Develop the eight-module course content on the existing LMS infrastructure. Build the agentic tutoring layer. Configure the recipe sandbox environment. Pilot with one cohort. Refine. Open Self-Paced License. Approximately 350 hours of focused build time. Months 6-12 (overlapping with Skills): Solutions infrastructure maturation. Refine the Solutions architecture based on early engagements. Build the governance integration layer as a reusable component. Codify the five configurations as engagement templates. Train additional facilitators (if growth supports). Approximately 250 hours of focused build time, plus engagement-specific work. Total approximate focused build time across 12 months: 900 hours, distributed across the calendar with overlap. This is one full-time engineer-equivalent for the year, plus engagement-specific work delivered as engagements occur.

### Eight-F: Update protocol for the full path

This document is the single source of truth for all four stages. When boilerplate language is improved, when assessment questions evolve, when course content is refined based on cohort experience, when Solutions configurations are updated based on engagement learning — the update happens here first. Downstream artifacts regenerate from this source. Quarterly review of the full document by the Movemental team. Annual update of the field paper sections (Parts One and similar) as new sector data becomes available. Continuous update of the working layers as engagements produce new learning. The path is the path. This document keeps the path coherent.
