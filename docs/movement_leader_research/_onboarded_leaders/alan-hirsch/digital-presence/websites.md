### Site Structure

The site is organized as a personal author/thought-leader hub. The homepage presents Alan's identity as a missional movement strategist. The About page details his organizational roles and academic affiliations. The Books page catalogs published works with links to purchase. The Blog archives posts by category (APEST, mDNA, Missional, Leadership, etc.). A Free Resources (eBooks) section offers downloadable content. A Tests page links to the APEST assessment platform. The FAQ addresses assessment-related questions.
