## Disambiguation

There are multiple public figures named "Alan Hirsch" who must be carefully distinguished:

3. **Alan T. Hirsch, MD (1954-2017)** -- American vascular medicine researcher at University of Minnesota/University of Colorado. Deceased. This is NOT our subject.

4. **Alan R. Hirsch, MD** -- Neurological Director of the Smell & Taste Treatment and Research Foundation in Chicago. This is NOT our subject.

## Family & Personal

- **Spouse**: Debra (Deb) Hirsch -- wife and ministry partner. Co-author of *Untamed*. Co-founder of Forge Mission Training Network. Met shortly before emigrating to Australia in 1983. They describe their ministry as a full partnership. Debra led ministry to LGBTQ+ communities and served the poor through the Salvation Army during their time at South Melbourne Restoration Community.
- **Brother**: Lawrence Hirsch -- involved in Celebrate Messiah ministry in Melbourne, Australia.
- **Heritage**: Jewish South African. Grew up in a Jewish family; not observant but deeply shaped by Jewish heritage. Emphasizes Jesus as the Jewish Messiah and distinguishes between Hebraic and Hellenistic thought in his theology.
- **Conversion**: Became a Christian at approximately age 20 during military service, introduced to faith by a friend who had been born again. Describes it as "the defining moment of my life."
- **Political formation**: Became politically active against apartheid at age 13 in South Africa.
- **Community**: Active participant in The Tribe of LA, a Jesus community among artists and creatives in Los Angeles.
