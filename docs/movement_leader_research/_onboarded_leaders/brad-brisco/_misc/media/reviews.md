## Overall Assessment

Brad Brisco's books are **well-regarded within the missional church planting niche** but have not broken through to mainstream Christian publishing prominence. His strongest title by reader reception is **Next Door as It Is in Heaven** (4.27 on Goodreads, 98 ratings), which has the broadest appeal due to its neighborhood engagement focus. His most niche title, **Covocational Church Planting**, fills a unique gap but has a smaller readership. All books are co-authored with Lance Ford except Covocational Church Planting and ReThink, suggesting the Ford-Brisco partnership has been his primary publishing vehicle.
