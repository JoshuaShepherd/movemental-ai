## Executive Summary

This audit provides a comprehensive inventory of Brad Brisco's existing content assets across all formats and distribution channels. It catalogs 5+ published books, curriculum resources, articles, speaking engagements, and training programs, identifying content strengths and gaps, mapping distribution channels, analyzing performance opportunities, and outlining repurposing strategies. The audit reveals a strong foundation in practical resources with coherent frameworks (missional transition, covocational planting) but identifies opportunities for increased digital content frequency, multimedia formats, and expanded resources for emerging needs.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Complete content inventory by format and topic
- Content performance analysis and opportunities
- Content gap identification
- Repurposing roadmap
- Content architecture recommendations
