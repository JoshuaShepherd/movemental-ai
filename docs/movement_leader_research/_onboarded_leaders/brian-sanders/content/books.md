## Per-book detail

### The 6 Seasons of Calling: Discovering Your Purpose in Each Stage of Life

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Author | Brian Sanders |
| Publisher | Moody Publishers |
| Published | 2022 |
| ISBN-13 | 9780802423948 (print); 9780802499783 (ebook) |

**Description**: A framework treating vocation and calling as ongoing and dynamic across six divinely-ordained life seasons — Childhood (bonding), Adolescence (learning), Early Career (serving), Mid-Career (creating), Late Career (giving), and Transition (leaving) — rather than as a single fixed decision.

### Microchurches: A Smaller Way

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Author | Brian Sanders |
| Publisher | Underground Media |
| Published | 2019 |
| ISBN-13 | 9781088455661 |

**Description**: The operational ecclesiology of the microchurch — defined as "the coalescing of three intentional practices: worship, community, and mission" — making the case that the microchurch is not just the smallest expression of the church but the most common and most reproducible, with practical guidance on understanding and starting one.

### Underground Church: A Living Example of the Church in Its Most Potent Form

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Author | Brian Sanders; foreword/intro by Alan Hirsch |
| Publisher | Zondervan (Exponential Series) |
| Published | 2018 |
| ISBN-13 | 9780310538073 |

**Description**: The true story of a ten-year experiment in a church structured and streamlined for mission rather than for a weekly gathering — organized around the callings of individuals to the poor, the lost, and biblical justice, with a lightweight network serving many small mission-organized churches.

## Notes
- *The 6 Seasons of Calling* names six seasons: Childhood (bonding), Adolescence (learning), Early Career (serving), Mid-Career (creating), Late Career (giving), Transition (leaving). Audiobook narrated by Brian Arens.
- *Underground Church* page count 272; published March 6, 2018; an Exponential Series edition (ISBN 9781721348039 / -046) also exists; audiobook narrated by Mark Smeby (Zondervan), with Alan Hirsch introduction.
- *Microchurches: A Smaller Way* — 208 pp.; published Sept 10, 2019; audiobook self-narrated (Underground Media). Defines a microchurch as "the coalescing of three intentional practices: worship, community, and mission."
- *Different* and *Life In Intentional Christian Community* are part of an Underground Media self-published set; years approximate.
- The prompt's "Six Seasons of a Man's Life" corresponds to the published title **The 6 Seasons of Calling** (no separate "Man's Life" title verified).
