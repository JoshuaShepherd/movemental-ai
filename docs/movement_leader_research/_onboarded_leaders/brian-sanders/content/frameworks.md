# Brian Sanders — Frameworks

Grounded in his published work and the Underground Network corpus. Only frameworks traceable to his books, teaching, or organizational practice are included; no invented diagrams or component lists.

---

### The Microchurch ("a smaller way")

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2019 (*Microchurches: A Smaller Way*); developed from the Underground experiment begun 2006 |
| Components | A microchurch is "the coalescing of three intentional practices: worship, community, and mission" — small, intentional, reproducible, and mission-organized rather than gathering-organized |
| Source | [Microchurches: A Smaller Way (Goodreads)](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/53009657-microchurches) |

The microchurch reframes the church's basic unit downward — not a weekly congregational gathering but a small, intentional community defined by the simultaneous practice of worship, community, and mission, which Sanders argues is "not just the smallest expression of the church, it is the most common."

---

### The Ecclesial Minimum

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2019 (*Microchurches: A Smaller Way*); taught across the Microchurches podcast |
| Components | The irreducible core of what makes a church a church — **worship** (the lordship of Jesus, "not necessarily about singing or liturgy"), **community** (shared, accountable life), and **mission** (outward witness); strip away cultural layers and these three remain |
| Source | [Ecclesial Minimum (Brian Sanders)](https://static1.squarespace.com/static/5032a0a9e4b0dbdecd4263a5/t/628fbdb74bc51276d0f8800d/1653587385048/Ecclesial+Minimum.pdf) |

Sanders identifies worship, community, and mission as the "ecclesial minimum" — the minimum viable expression of the church that survives when every cultural and programmatic accretion is removed.

### The Underground Model (network-of-microchurches ecclesiology)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2018 (*Underground Church*); operative since 2006 |
| Components | (1) Church "structured and streamlined for mission" rather than for a weekly gathering; (2) ministry organized around the callings of individuals to outward mission; (3) a lightweight support **network** — relationship and resources (coaching, finance, media, incubation) — serving many small microchurches; (4) priority of the poor, the lost, and biblical justice |
| Source | [tampaunderground.com/who-we-are](https://www.tampaunderground.com/who-we-are), [undergroundnetwork.org](https://www.undergroundnetwork.org/) |

The Underground inverts the usual church-resource relationship — a lightweight network exists to serve and incubate many small, mission-organized churches rather than a central church running programs that people attend.

---

### Calling-Serving (apostolic) Leadership

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2018 (*Underground Church*); 2019 (*Microchurches: A Smaller Way*) |
| Components | Leaders serve members' God-given callings rather than recruit members to the leader's vision ("the best way to love and lead our people is not to call them to our vision"); empowerment of ordinary disciples so that "every Christian in every context" carries responsibility for the church's formation |
| Source | [Underground Church (Goodreads)](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/35553383-underground-church) |

Sanders reframes leadership away from vision-casting toward the discernment and release of the callings already present in ordinary disciples, so that the apostolic task is to multiply senders rather than to gather attenders.

---

### The Networked Church (seven shifts)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | c. 2018 (*The Networked Church* eBook, Exponential); paired with *Underground Church* |
| Components | A set of shifts the church must consider to move "beyond mega and multisite" toward a decentralized, mission-organized network — reorienting around mission, callings, the small, and a serving network rather than around a central campus |
| Source | [Brian Sanders on The Networked Church (Exponential)](https://exponential.org/brian-sanders-networked-church/) |

The Networked Church names the structural shifts required to liberate the church from attractional, campus-centric assumptions toward a networked ecology of small, mission-organized communities.

---

### The 6 Seasons of Calling

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2022 (*The 6 Seasons of Calling*) |
| Components | Six divinely-ordained life seasons, each with a developmental task — **Childhood** (bonding), **Adolescence** (learning), **Early Career** (serving), **Mid-Career** (creating), **Late Career** (giving), **Transition** (leaving) |
| Source | [The 6 Seasons of Calling (Everand)](https://www.everand.com/book/545673190/The-6-Seasons-of-Calling-Discovering-Your-Purpose-in-Each-Stage-of-Life) |

A vocational framework treating calling as ongoing and dynamic across six seasons of life rather than a single fixed decision, extending the Underground's "callings of individuals" emphasis across the lifespan.
