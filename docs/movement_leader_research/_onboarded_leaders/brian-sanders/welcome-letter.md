You have built one of the most operationally consequential bodies of work in the Western microchurch movement. Two flagship books anchor it: *Underground Church*, the Zondervan narrative of the ten-year experiment carrying Alan Hirsch's foreword, and *Microchurches: A Smaller Way*, the operational ecclesiology that defines the church's basic unit as the coalescing of worship, community, and mission. Around those sit *The 6 Seasons of Calling* with Moody, *Life In Intentional Christian Community*, *Different* with Mike Patz, and the earliest articulation in *Life After Church*.
