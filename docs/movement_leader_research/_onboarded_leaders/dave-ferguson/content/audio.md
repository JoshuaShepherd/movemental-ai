## Summary

Dave Ferguson **hosts/co-hosts one podcast**: the **Hero Maker Podcast** on Exponential.org, which he co-hosted with Exponential CEO Todd Wilson as part of the *Hero Maker* book launch campaign (2017–2018). In addition to this hosted series, he is a frequent **guest** on major church leadership podcasts — including multiple appearances on the Carey Nieuwhof Leadership Podcast. He also hosted or co-hosted a short run of episodes called the **Together Podcast** (2020) tied to the *Together* book. He has no dedicated personal audio podcast channel outside of Exponential's platforms.

- **Hosted/co-hosted podcast series**: 2 (Hero Maker Podcast; Together Podcast) — both on Exponential.org
- **Total guest appearances found (confirmed)**: 15+ episodes across at least 12 shows
- **Date range**: 2010 (earliest audiobook) to 2026 (ongoing)
- **Peak guest activity**: 2018–2019 (*Hero Maker* launch) and 2020–2021 (*BLESS* launch)

## Key Themes Across Podcast Appearances

### Primary Themes (appear in 5+ episodes)
1. **Hero Making / Leadership Multiplication** — The core thesis of his 2018 book; discussed in most appearances from 2017–2022
2. **Church Multiplication / Five Levels** — Why churches plateau and how to move from addition to multiplication
3. **NewThing / Exponential movement** — Organizational context and movement philosophy

### Secondary Themes (appear in 2–4 episodes)
4. **Missional living / BLESS practices** — Neighbor engagement; organic evangelism
5. **Ministry health / sustainability** — Habits for finishing well; life balance
6. **Church planting trends** — Micro-churches, meta-churches, post-COVID landscape
