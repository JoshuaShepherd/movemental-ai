Five essential practices: (1) Multiplication Thinking; (2) Permission Giving; (3) Disciple-Multiplying; (4) Gift Activating; (5) Kingdom Building. Co-written with Warren Bird (longtime Leadership Network researcher). Foreword by J.D. Greear. Anchored Exponential's 2018 conference theme.
