## Summary

Dave Ferguson is not primarily a course creator in the online-platform sense (no Teachable, Thinkific, or personal course portal identified). His training output flows through three institutional channels: (1) **Exponential** — where he creates conference-linked learning cohorts, webinar series, and resource kits tied to annual conference themes; (2) **RightNow Media / Pastors+** — where he hosts at least one multi-session professional development video course; and (3) **NewThing Network** — now merged into Exponential's "Better Together" infrastructure — which offered a "Movement System" training for church networks. He also teaches as a guest/adjunct at Wheaton College Graduate School.

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Measure What Matters |
| **Platform** | RightNow Pastors+ (subscription required) |
| **Format** | Multi-session video course |
| **Audience** | Pastors and church leadership teams |
| **Topic** | Rethinking church success metrics; building a mission-focused scorecard; "The Jesus Mission the Jesus Way" |
| **Trailer URL** | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GzaRhA0A34Y |

| **Status** | Available (subscription required) |

**Description**: Helps church teams examine what metrics they are measuring and whether those metrics align with the Jesus mission. ## Key Training Frameworks Developed by Dave Ferguson

These frameworks recur across Exponential's courses, books, and conference content:

| Framework | Source | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Five Levels of Church Multiplication** | *Becoming a Level Five Multiplying Church* (with Todd Wilson) | Level 1 = Subtracting; Level 2 = Plateaued; Level 3 = Adding; Level 4 = Reproducing; Level 5 = Multiplying. The primary diagnostic framework across Exponential |
| **Five Hero Maker Practices** | *Hero Maker* (with Warren Bird) | Multiplication Thinking, Permission Giving, Disciple-Multiplying, Gift Activating, Kingdom Building |
| **BLESS (Five Everyday Practices)** | *BLESS* (with Jon Ferguson) | Begin with prayer, Listen, Eat, Serve, Tell your Story — an everyday evangelism framework |
| **Five Awakenings** | *Finding Your Way Back to God* (with Jon Ferguson) | Five stages of spiritual awakening for seekers; used widely in seeker-series programming |
| **Church Scorecard vs. Scoreboard** | *Keeping Score* (Exponential eBook) | Theological framework for measuring what actually matters in church health |
| **The Great Collaboration** | *Together* (with Patrick O'Connell) | Great Commission + Great Commandment + Great Collaboration (John 17); church network framework |
