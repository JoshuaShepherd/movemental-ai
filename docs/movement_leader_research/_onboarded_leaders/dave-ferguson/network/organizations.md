## Organizations Map

### Founded / Co-Founded

| Organization | Relationship | Dates | Status | Description | URL |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Community Christian Church (COMMUNITY)** | Co-Founding and Lead Pastor (now Co-Founding Pastor) | 1989–present | Active | Co-founded with brother Jon Ferguson in 1989 in a suburban Chicago high school cafeteria with "four friends from college." Grew from 5 people to 5,500+ weekly attendees across 12+ Chicago-area locations. Described as "one of the most influential churches in America." Ferguson stepped back from Lead Pastor role to Co-Founding Pastor in 2025. Tri-fold vision: Impact Church, Reproducing Church, Catalyst for a Movement. | [communitychristian.org](https://www.communitychristian.org/) |
| **NewThing Network** | Founder / Visionary Leader | 2001–April 2025 (merged into Exponential) | Merged | Church-planting network launched in 2001 as the missional outreach arm of Community Christian Church. Grew to operate in nearly 70 nations, planting 6,373+ churches over ~20 years (982 in 2020 alone). Operated through a network-of-networks model: "networks" of 4–6 churches → "movements" of 4–6 networks. Merged fully into Exponential effective April 1, 2025. | [newthing.org](https://newthing.org/) (historical); [exponential.org/bettertogether](https://exponential.org/bettertogether/) |
| **Exponential** | Co-Founder / CEO & President | ~2006–present | Active | Co-founded with Todd Wilson. Billed as the world's largest annual church planting and multiplication conference. Organizes the flagship annual Orlando conference plus regional events across the U.S. and internationally (Exponential Europe). Mission: "equipping leaders to multiply healthy, reproducing faith communities." Absorbed NewThing April 1, 2025, creating a unified organization. | [exponential.org](https://exponential.org/) |
