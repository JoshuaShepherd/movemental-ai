## Network Summary

Lewis sits at the intersection of three organizational worlds: his own Atlanta congregation (Blueprint), his urban church-planting network (Rebuild/MyBLVD), and the national SBC church-planting infrastructure (NAMB Send Network). His strongest edges are as a founder/leader of his own institutions and as a former national network executive; his endorsement and peer network clusters around the SBC multiethnic-church and church-multiplication ecosystem.
