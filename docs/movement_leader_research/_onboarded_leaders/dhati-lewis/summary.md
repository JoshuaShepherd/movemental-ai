# Digital Reality Profile: Dhati Lewis

Dhati Lewis is an Atlanta-based church planter, author, and disciple-making leader — founder of Blueprint Church, founder/president of the Rebuild Initiative (MyBLVD), and former Vice President and President of the North American Mission Board's Send Network. His published and spoken work centers on disciple-making in the urban context, the church as family, and a Christ-centered "narrow path" to racial reconciliation. His content is substantive and coherent but distributed across many disconnected surfaces.

## Executive Summary

Across two major books (*Among Wolves*, 2017; *Advocates*, 2019), a companion Bible study, three hosted podcasts, and years of conference teaching, Lewis has built a coherent body of disciple-making and reconciliation work. The challenge is not message clarity but consolidation: his corpus lives in silos (personal site, Blueprint, MyBLVD, multiple podcast platforms, publisher and speaker pages), with no single searchable home.
