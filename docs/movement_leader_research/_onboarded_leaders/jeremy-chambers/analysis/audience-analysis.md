# Audience Analysis: Jeremy Chambers

**Status**: Complete
**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

---

## Overview

Jeremy "Jemo" Chambers reaches practitioners at the intersection of **contemplative formation** (Renovaré lineage) and **missional microchurch** work (Forge / 100 Movements / Hirsch-Frost stream). He teaches and writes almost always as a **dyad with Monica Paredes Chambers**.

---

## Primary Audience Segments

### Forge / Missional Training Cohort

- Aspiring and current Forge America trainees
- Microchurch planters seeking integrative formation + sentness
- Denver Front Range Pando Collective participants

### Microchurch and V3 Network Leaders

- Arise RVA alumni and BGAV/V3-affiliated planters
- Leaders resisting method absolutism ("microchurch is *a* form, not *the* form")

### Contemplative Formation Readers

- Renovaré audience; readers of *The Art of Missional Spirituality*
- Practitioners seeking daily rhythms that fold prayer and neighborhood mission together

### Book Launch / Podcast Circuit (2024)

- Podcast listeners from Renovaré, Everyday Disciple, Pivotal People, Dandelion Resourcing, Shifting Culture

---

## Content Preferences

- Rhythm-based practices over programmatic strategy
- Integration of intimacy with God and engagement with neighbors
- Practitioner memoir and essay form; accessible theology
- Co-authored voice (Jeremy + Monica)
