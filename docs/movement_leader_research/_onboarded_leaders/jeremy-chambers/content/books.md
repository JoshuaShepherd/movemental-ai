# Books

All of Jeremy Chambers' books are co-authored with his wife, Monica Paredes Chambers.

### The Art of Missional Spirituality: 31 Sacred Practices for Jesus-Followers

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | Jeremy Chambers & Monica Paredes Chambers |
| Foreword | Alan & Debra Hirsch |
| Publisher | 100 Movements Publishing |
| Published | 2023 |
| ISBN-13 | 978-1-955142-44-1 |

**Description**: Thirty-one daily practices — one per day for a month — that fuse classical contemplative disciplines (Praying the Hours, silence, solitude, immersion in Ecclesiastes) with missional-contemplative hybrids (secret acts of kindness, neighborhood blessing, hospitality, walking prayer), so that intimacy with God and engagement with neighbors stop being separate items and become one integrated rhythm of life. This is Chambers' magnum opus to date and his most accessible entry point.

### Kingdom Contours: Foundations, Paradigms, and Tools for Equipping Jesus Movements (Revised Edition)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Authors | Jeremy Chambers & Monica Paredes Chambers |
| Foreword | Alan Hirsch & Michael Frost |
| Publisher | 100 Movements Publishing |
| Published | 2024 |
| ISBN-13 | 978-1-955142-70-0 |

**Description**: A planter-facing toolkit that restates and extends Alan Hirsch's mDNA into six foundational paradigms — Christocentric and Trinitarian, Discipleship, Incarnational Living, Movement Wisdom, Organic Systems, and Deep Community — and closes with a "Baby Steps: How to Start a Micro-Church" appendix. The revised edition reissues and refines the 2020 original under the 100 Movements Publishing imprint.
