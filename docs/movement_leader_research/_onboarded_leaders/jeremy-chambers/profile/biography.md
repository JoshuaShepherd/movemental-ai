# Biography

Jeremy "Jemo" Chambers is an American church planter, urban missionary, spiritual director, author, and trainer whose vocational life sits at the seam between contemplative spiritual formation and missional-movement practice. Born around 1986, he has said he began praying about a future move to Denver at age thirteen — a vocational specificity that anticipated nearly two decades of geographic and ministerial trajectory. He earned a B.A. in Ministry Leadership from Johnson University and two master's degrees from Trinity Evangelical Divinity School (an M.A. in Intercultural Studies and a second master's in Christian Studies). In 2013, after a church-planting residency, Jeremy and his Costa-Rican-born wife Monica Paredes Chambers founded the Arise RVA Church Network in Richmond, Virginia — a network of gospel-centered microchurches that grew to roughly seven communities and affiliated with the V3 Movement under the Baptist General Association of Virginia. In late 2019 the family relocated to Denver, Colorado to help Rowland Smith launch the Denver expression of The Pando Collective, a Front Range network that connects, resources, and launches missional micro-expressions of the church. He has since become Training Director of Forge America, the U.S. arm of Alan Hirsch's globally networked missional training movement. Almost all of Chambers' published work is co-authored with Monica, who is herself a spiritual director, co-planter, and graphic designer; together they have written two books in the 100 Movements Publishing orbit and contributed essays and podcast appearances across the V3 Movement, Renovaré, and Missio Alliance. Their shared thesis is that spiritual formation and neighborhood mission belong together as one rhythm — a conviction that runs through every layer of their work.

## Ministry trajectory

- **2013:** Founded Arise RVA Church Network in Richmond, VA (V3 / BGAV affiliated).
- **2013–2019:** Grew Arise RVA into a network of microchurches.
- **Late 2019 / 2020:** Relocated to Denver, CO; helped launch the Denver expression of The Pando Collective with Rowland Smith.
- **2024–present:** Training Director of Forge America; co-leader of Pando Denver.

## Family

Jeremy is married to Monica Paredes Chambers, born in Costa Rica, who studied graphic design and serves as a co-author, co-planter, and spiritual director. The couple's published work is signed and written jointly. Children are not publicly documented.
