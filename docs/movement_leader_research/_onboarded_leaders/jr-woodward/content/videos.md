## Executive Summary

JR Woodward does **not** appear to have his own YouTube channel. His video content is scattered across third-party platforms: conference recording channels (Verge Network / Exponential), organization sites (V3 Movement, Missio Alliance), podcast video channels (Theology in the Raw, The Roys Report), Vimeo (Five Equippers series), and Seminary Now (gated educational content). He also has a growing presence through his Substack "Faithful Resistance" which hosts webinars.

- **Own YouTube channel:** No dedicated channel found
- **Twitter/X handle:** @dreamawakener
- **Facebook:** @missionalculture
- **Substack:** jrwoodward.substack.com ("Faithful Resistance")
- **Estimated total videos across all platforms:** 25-40
- **Date range:** 2011 (earliest confirmed) to 2025-2026 (ongoing)
- **Most viewed:** Likely the Verge Network / Exponential 2011 conference talks and Five Equippers Vimeo series, given their longevity and platform reach

---

## Key Themes Across Video Content

1. **Polycentric / APEST Leadership** -- Five Equippers model (Apostle, Prophet, Evangelist, Pastor, Teacher) as alternative to hierarchical or flat church leadership
2. **Missional Culture** -- Equipping the whole church for mission, not just professional clergy
3. **Church as Movement** -- Moving from institutional "industrial complex" models to missional-incarnational communities
4. **Scandal of Leadership / Domineering Power** -- Unmasking powers of domination in the church (based on PhD research)
5. **Faithful Resistance** -- Prophetic discipleship, resisting technocratic and autocratic powers (2025-2026 emphasis)
6. **Neighborhood-Grounded Church Planting** -- Practical models from Kairos Los Angeles and V3 network

## Comparison to Similar Leaders

JR Woodward's video footprint is notably smaller than peers like Alan Hirsch or Mike Breen, who have extensive YouTube presence through Forge, 5Q, and 3DM channels. Woodward's content is more dispersed and relies heavily on third-party platforms (podcast interviews, conference recordings). His 2023 book launch for "The Scandal of Leadership" generated the largest cluster of video/audio interviews in his career.
