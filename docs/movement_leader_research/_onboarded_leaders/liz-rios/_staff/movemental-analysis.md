## Movemental interpretation

### As system
Passion2Plant + Púlpito Fellows are **already-running cohort products** with multi-year structure, bilingual delivery, and explicit deliverables (three digital products per fellow). This is the platform's cohort-management primitive with live data — a true customer-zero for the system build.

### As content structure
Her writing clusters cleanly into pillars: justice-oriented ecclesiology · women in ministry · Latina/o Christianity · digital ministry · civic engagement. Natural sidebar taxonomy for a tenant site. Bilingual corpus (English primary, Spanish titles/terms) needs platform support for language pairing.

### As pathways
Three obvious learner journeys:
1. **BIPOC woman discerning church planting** → Passion2Plant cohort prep pathway
2. **Existing pastor moving to justice-oriented praxis** → DMin-dissertation content + Sojourners/PCPJ articles sequenced
3. **Latina/o leader building digital platform** → Púlpito Fellows curriculum excerpted and sequenced

### As courses
Púlpito Fellows is *already* a 10-week bilingual cohort with 10 topics, 1 in-person launch, 8 Zoom sessions, and three digital-product artifacts. It can be cloned into the Transformational Course Charter format with minor editorial work. The 3-year fellowship arc maps well to a 3-tier course ladder.

### As AI-enabled
- **RAG** over her articles, dissertations, chapters — high-value because she is repeatedly asked questions ("how do I plant as a Latina?", "what does justice-oriented mean?") whose answers are already written but scattered.
- **Voice agent** possible but **requires guardrails** on mujerista/Latina first-person claims and anything touching Daniel (her late son).
- **Bilingual retrieval** is a differentiator; platform must support Spanish tokens in retrieval and generation.
