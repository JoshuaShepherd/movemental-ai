## Primary Themes

1. **The Jesus Lens / "Jesus Glasses" hermeneutic** — Jesus as the fullness of God and Scripture's authoritative interpreter; every text read through its culmination in Christ.
2. **The Better Christian Story** — Western Christianity has told "too small" a story; recovery requires a movement-scale reformation (the eight theses of *Divine Gravity*).
3. **Divine Gravity / centered-set ecclesiology** — Jesus as gravitational center; belonging defined by trajectory toward him, not boundary positions.
4. **Cross-shaped power** — God's kingdom advances through self-sacrifice, not coercion; kenotic power as the most radical form of agency.
5. **Wounded image of God** — a distorted, fear-shaped image of God healed through encounter with the Jesus-centered God (the pastoral-therapeutic dimension).
