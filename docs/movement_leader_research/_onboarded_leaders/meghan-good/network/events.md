## Event Network Summary

### Primary Event Ecosystems

1. **Mennonite Church USA / MC denominational circuit**: MennoCon (national conventions), Ohio Conference, Central Plains Conference, AMBS commencement, Mennonite Bible Study Series — Good's home base.
2. **Jesus Collective network**: Theology Circle leadership, North American tour (2023), regional conferences (2026) — her primary post-Trinity platform.
3. **Cross-denominational / ecumenical circuit**: Church of the Brethren retreat, Woodland Hills SEM, Fresno Pacific connection, Yeovil UK — signals growing reach beyond Mennonite world.
4. **Academic and teaching**: AMBS DMin ongoing; CMU preaching courses; various live courses on preaching and biblical interpretation.
5. **Podcast circuit**: MC USA, ReKnew, Red Letter Christians, Shifting Culture — primarily promoting *Divine Gravity* (2023–2025).

### Geographic Reach

- **Primary base**: Dayton, Ohio area (as of 2025–2026)
- **National**: Across MC USA conferences (Kansas City, Cincinnati, Ohio, Minnesota, Nebraska, California)
- **International**: UK (Yeovil, November 2025), Canada (Saskatoon, Winnipeg); Jesus Collective is Toronto-based

### Event Frequency Assessment

- **High frequency**: AMBS DMin individual student sessions (ongoing); "Reading Jesus" YouTube (recurring)
- **Annual**: Typically 1–3 major denominational or conference speaking engagements per year
- **Periodic**: Podcast appearances (multiple per year since *Divine Gravity* released 2023)
- **Scheduled upcoming (2026–2027)**: Jesus Collective regional conference (April 2026), UK (November 2025), Fresno CA (March 2027)
