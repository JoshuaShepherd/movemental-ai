His framing should lean **into** the academic credentials, not around them.

- **No unified scholarly profile.** There is no Google Scholar page, no ORCID, and the only live research-network presence is an Academia.edu profile under the legacy "Sioux Falls Seminary" subdomain — easy to miss, hard to disambiguate from the NBA / actor namesakes.
- **Three of four journal articles are under-documented** (UNKNOWN titles/years for *Nova Religio*, *Pomegranate*, *JEMS*), so his peer-reviewed record cannot be cited cleanly.
- **The invited lectures (LSE, Bordeaux, Loyola, Baylor) are invisible** — no recordings, abstracts, or listings on his own surfaces, despite being among his strongest credibility assets.
- **The intellectual genealogy is thin on the record** — direct, sourced engagement with canonical missiologists (Roland Allen, Newbigin, Bosch, Schnabel) is unconfirmed pending the *Ephesiology* bibliography.

The fragmentation story for the academic layer is **strong scholarly capital that is poorly indexed and under-claimed**.
