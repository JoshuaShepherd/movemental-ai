## Signature Frameworks (training tools, not academic courses)

### Life Transformation Group (LTG)

- **What**: 2-or-3-person same-gender accountability/Scripture/prayer cell; meets weekly; uses a one-page card with reading plan, accountability questions, and prayer for non-Christians.
- **When introduced**: Operational at Awakening Chapel from 1999; codified in *Cultivating a Life for God* (1999).
- **Cole's claim**: "thousands of churches in 50 states and 50+ nations" have used the LTG framework (CMA promotional copy).
- **External adoption**: IMB (International Mission Board) adapted it as **CHAT**; cited in Curtis Sergeant, Steve Addison, and DMM-stream literature; widely used in Saddleback's PEACE Plan church-planting work.
- **Materials**: a card and the *Cultivating a Life for God* book + facilitator guide.

### Organic Church Planters' Greenhouse

- **What**: Two-stage cohort training for organic church planters.
  - **Story 1** — "Foundations for Organic Church Planting" (initial cohort weekend / multi-session intensive): Foundations, Cultivating the Soil of People's Hearts, Sowing the Kingdom Seed, Growing the Life of the Kingdom.
  - **Story 2** — Field-application stage; ongoing coaching and church-multiplication mechanics.
- **Co-author / co-architect**: **Paul Kaak** (Cole's CMA partner; brought into the framework as the "Teaching" gift in *Primal Fire*).
- **Documented cohorts**:
  - **Frisco, TX (January 2011)** — ~75 participants; trainers Cole + **Ed Waken** (Phoenix/LA-based CMA leader).
  - **Virginia Mennonite Conference (September 2012)** — Greenhouse training session.
  - Multiple Long Beach / Southern California cohorts across the 2000s and 2010s.
- **Status**: dormant under CMA Resources branding (domain lapsed); unclear whether revived under Starling Initiatives.

### ReTooling Leadership Series

- A multi-article CMA Resources curriculum on leadership, primarily authored by **Ed Waken** (Cole's CMA colleague).
- Confirmed entries on legacy CMA Resources: ReTooling Leadership 3, 5, 7 (and presumably 1, 2, 4, 6).
- Status: dormant under the lapsed domain.

### Apostolic Leadership in Organic Church Movements

## Implications for Movemental

1. **The LTG card is one of the most reproducible discipleship tools in evangelical history** and is currently distributed via a dormant domain. A Movemental-hosted, modernized LTG kit (card + facilitator guide + audio walkthrough) would be a near-universal recruiting and discipleship asset.
2. **Greenhouse Story 1 / Story 2 deserves modern productization.** Recast as a 4–6-week cohort with Cole as anchor teacher and a few alumni co-teachers (Phil Helfer, Paul Kaak, Ed Waken — all still operationally connected) it would compete directly with Exponential's microchurch tracks and KC Underground's "Find Your Place" 4-session journey.
3. **A Starling Initiatives × Movemental online course bundle** is a natural co-pub opportunity — Starling does not have the platform infrastructure to launch online courses well; Movemental does.
