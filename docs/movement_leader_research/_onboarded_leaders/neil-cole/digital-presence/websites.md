## Notes on Domain Hygiene

The `cmaresources.org` situation is the single most painful piece of digital fragmentation in Cole's footprint. Because the original domain has been allowed to lapse (or has been domain-hijacked), **every legacy citation across the missional internet** — book footnotes, blog references, conference handouts from 2002–2018 — now either 404s or, worse, resolves to a Sydney-lottery prediction site. The Wayback Machine snapshots remain the only stable surface for the legacy CMA Resources archive.

A Movemental-hosted "Cole Hub" that consolidates his canonical content (with explicit permissions from Cole) would substantially repair this damage. It would also be the single highest-leverage curatorial move available for an author with his depth-to-fragmentation ratio.
