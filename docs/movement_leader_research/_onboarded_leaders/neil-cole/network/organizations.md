## Founded / Co-founded

### Awakening Chapel (1999)

- **Type**: Local church / first organic church plant
- **Location**: Long Beach, CA — launched in the smoking section of "The Coffee Tavern" (TCT)
- **Status**: Founding congregation of the Awakening Chapels network within CMA. Current operational status of any Awakening Chapel branch is UNKNOWN — no recent updates surfaced.
- **Significance**: The proof-of-concept that became CMA's flagship case study. Multiple "Awakening Chapel" plants spawned across Southern California through the early 2000s.

### Church Multiplication Associates (CMA / CMAResources.org) — late 1990s

- **Type**: Non-profit church planting / training organization (501(c)(3))
- **Headquarters**: Signal Hill, CA
- **Founded**: Late 1990s (CMA's promotional copy says "the late 1990s," coterminous with the Awakening Chapel launch in 1999)
- **Founders / Co-founders**: Neil Cole, **Phil Helfer** (pastor of Los Altos Grace Church and Cole's primary co-founder); **Paul Kaak** also early-team
- **Founding role**: Founder & Executive Director (Cole)
- **Self-reported reach**: "Thousands of churches in fifty states and more than fifty nations" within ~20 years (per multiple Cole bios)
- **Key infrastructure**:
  - LTG (Life Transformation Group) framework — 1999
  - Greenhouse training cycles — Story 1 + Story 2
  - CMA Resources website (`cmaresources.org`) — historically the central content hub
  - "Greenhouse Happenings" event calendar
  - ReTooling Leadership content series (Ed Waken)
  - Multi-author training audio (Cole + Helfer; Cole + Rohde; Cole + Waken)
- **Current status**: **Effectively dormant.** The cmaresources.org domain has lapsed and now serves a Sydney-lottery prediction page (i.e., gambling-prediction parked content). The @CMAResources Twitter and Facebook page still nominally exist but recent posting cadence UNCONFIRMED. No formal dissolution announcement has been made publicly that this audit could surface.

### Starling Initiatives (~2018)

- **Type**: Global collective + self-publishing imprint
- **URL**: [starlinginitiatives.com](https://starlinginitiatives.com/)
- **Mission (per about page)**: "A global collective of Christ-followers seeking to build deeper relationships" with the foundational principle "listen to Jesus and do what He says"
- **Founded**: ~2018 (first Starling Initiatives Publication Series book — *Rising Tides* — released June 2018)
- **Cole's role**: Founder
- **Programs**: Private Facebook community group; monthly live Zoom training; mentorship from "global leaders" (unnamed); regional meetups (coming soon); on-site training; online courses (coming soon); books and audiobooks
- **Operational footprint**: Small and quiet, by design. No board, team page, or 501(c)(3) status visibly listed.
- **Significance**: Cole's **current** institutional anchor; a deliberately small, post-silence operation rather than a CMA scale-out.
