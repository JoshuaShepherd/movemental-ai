# Frameworks — Peyton Jones

Compiled 2026-06-02. Grounded in Peyton Jones's published work (*Church Zero*, *Reaching the Unreached*, *Church Plantology*, *Discipology*, *Cracking the Whip*) and his NewBreed Training programs. Each framework below is documented in his books or his organization's materials; nothing here is invented. Where a formal model name or diagram is not published, that is noted.

---

### Plantology (the art and science of planting churches)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2021 (*Church Plantology: The Art and Science of Planting Churches*) |
| Components | A reproducible "science" track (biblical, Acts-derived principles) interwoven with a contextual "art" track (reading the city, culture exegesis, contextual creativity); built on the premise that disciple-making — not planting — is the focus |
| Source | [Church Plantology (Zondervan)](https://www.amazon.com/Church-Plantology-Planting-Churches-Exponential/dp/0310534178) |

Plantology reframes church planting as a discipline with both reproducible scientific principles and contextual artistry, insisting that planting a church should never be the focus — churches are the byproduct of devoted disciple-making.

---

### Discipology (the art and science of making disciples)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2026 (*Discipology: The Art and Science of Making Disciples*) |
| Components | Recovery of Jesus's original disciple-making model as the engine of multiplication; an "art + science" structure paralleling Plantology; companion *Journey to Disciple-Making* journal that operationalizes the process |
| Source | [Discipology (Zondervan)](https://www.amazon.com/Discipology-Science-Making-Disciples-Exponential/dp/0310180333) |

Discipology applies the same "art and science" architecture to disciple-making and presents it as the logical antecedent to Plantology: make disciples first, and churches follow.

---

### Gospel Penetration, Saturation, and Maturation

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2021 (*Church Plantology*) |
| Components | A three-stage measure of real church-planting work — gospel **penetration** into a context, gospel **saturation** across it, and gospel **maturation** of disciples within it; offered as the metric of success in place of attendance, sites, or buildings |
| Source | [Church Plantology (Zondervan)](https://www.amazon.com/Church-Plantology-Planting-Churches-Exponential/dp/0310534178) |

This framework defines the actual work of church planting as gospel penetration, saturation, and maturation, redirecting the measure of success away from attendance and toward depth of gospel impact.

---

### First-Century / Apostolic Church Retrieval ("Church Zero")

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2013 (*Church Zero: Raising 1st Century Churches out of the Ashes of the 21st Century Church*) |
| Components | A "punk-rock" call to recover the explosive first-century leadership model the modern church has tamed — multiplying disciples and activating every believer's gifts rather than centering one paid leader; the book of Acts treated as the normative pattern |
| Source | [Church Zero (David C. Cook)](https://www.amazon.com/Church-Zero-Raising-Century-Churches/dp/1434704939) |

Church Zero argues the contemporary church has domesticated the apostolic, Spirit-dependent leadership of Acts, and calls planters to raise first-century churches out of the ashes of the twenty-first-century one.

---

### Raiders of the Lost Art (reaching the unreached)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2017 (*Reaching the Unreached: Becoming Raiders of the Lost Art*, with Alan Hirsch) |
| Components | Recovery of the "lost art" of Acts-style, Spirit-led evangelism — leading people on an adventure of genuine relationships and gospel conversations toward disciple multiplication; premised on the claim that the post-Christian West mirrors the apostles' pre-Christian world, so "if the challenges are the same, then so are the solutions" |
| Source | [Reaching the Unreached (Zondervan)](https://www.amazon.com/Reaching-Unreached-Becoming-Raiders-Lost/dp/0310531101) |

This framework recovers the apostolic "lost art" of Spirit-led evangelism for post-Christian contexts, arguing that because the West's challenge again resembles the apostles' pre-Christian world, the same first-century solutions apply.

### NewBreed Planter Training (Jump School + CityWide)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | Wales era → United States; delivered through NewBreed Training |
| Components | **Jump School** — a six-month church-planter training program (with a "Core Team Training Series Study Guide"); **CityWide** — collaborative regional church-planting teams; both grounded in the apostolic "New Breed" planter ethos |
| Source | [NewBreed Training](https://newbreedtraining.com/about/peyton-jones/), [Exponential](https://exponential.org/person/peyton-jones/) |

"New Breed" is the organizational ethos brand whose concrete delivery vehicles are the Jump School cohort and the CityWide regional-team model, through which Jones has trained leaders across three continents.
