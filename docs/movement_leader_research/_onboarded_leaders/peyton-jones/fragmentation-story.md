## The Story

Peyton Jones wrote the textbook **Church Plantology** (Zondervan, 2021) and built **NewBreed Training** to catalyze church planting leaders across three continents. The book lives on Amazon, Goodreads, and seminary syllabi. The training lives on **newbreedtraining.com**. The planting stories live in cohort rooms and NAMB-adjacent networks that are not fully indexed on disk.

What is missing is the **connective tissue**: no confirmed personal blog archive linking book concepts to training modules; no documented podcast hub; no verified social handles; no newsletter capturing the NewBreed audience into an owned list. A planter who reads *Church Plantology* may never discover the training organization — and vice versa — unless referred through a network edge.

Peyton Jones's fragmentation is the fragmentation of a **single-flagship-book trainer** whose organizational content and trade publishing are real but not yet unified under one searchable, leader-owned catalog.
