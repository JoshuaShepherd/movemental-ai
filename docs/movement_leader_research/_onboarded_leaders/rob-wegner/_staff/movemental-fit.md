- **Personal-hub fragmentation.** `robwegner.org` is a 2012-era Tumblr running stale titles; the joint Substack is co-branded; the consulting site (Starfish Initiatives) is co-led; KCU is org-branded. **No surface today is "Rob Wegner the consolidated voice."** Movemental becomes that surface.
- **Eight-book invisibility.** No single page anywhere lists all 8 Wegner books in chronological / thematic order. Goodreads, Amazon, Audible, Christianbook each show partial subsets. Movemental's bibliography view fixes this in one page.
- **Newsletter zero.** No personal Wegner newsletter exists. The 1,867 Instagram followers, 27 Goodreads followers, 231 LinkedIn connections, plus the joint Substack co-subscribers, plus *Starfish* book buyers — all of them exist with no shared email-list home. Movemental closes the loop.
- **Title drift.** Stale "North American Director / NewThing" titles persist on `robwegner.org`, aggregator sites, and possibly on speaker bios after the April 2025 NewThing-into-Exponential merger. Movemental becomes the canonical-bio source.
- **Article archive scatter.** ~20+ bylines across Exponential, Outreach, Verge, Saturate, joint Substack — none of them in one author archive. Movemental aggregates with full text or canonical-link cross-references.
- **Conference-talk catalog absence.** Exponential 2019, 2024 Central, Microchurch Pre-Conferences, KCU Microchurch Conferences, UB 2025 keynote — no catalog. Movemental builds it.
- **Podcast-appearances discoverability.** 8+ external guest episodes have no return path to Wegner; Movemental indexes them.

## What We Will NOT Do

- **Replace KCU / Exponential / Starfish Initiatives roles**: Movemental is complementary, not substitutive. Wegner's work in Kansas City, at Exponential, and with Ford/Hirsch/Robinson stays where it is.
- **Remove content from existing platforms**: Outreach, Verge, Saturate, Exponential, KCU, joint Substack — all stay. Movemental aggregates and links.
- **Push Wegner toward solo-author framing in ways that contradict his theology**: the collaborative-byline pattern is conviction, not preference. The first solo-authored book should be carefully framed as a single-byline work *because* the collaborative pattern is so well-established that one solo entry won't undercut it.
- **Compete with Starfish Initiatives or Future Travelers**: those programs are partnership ground; Movemental amplifies rather than substitutes.
- **Pressure faster than KCU operational rhythms allow**: he has co-leadership responsibilities (Brian Johnson, Michelle), a 95-microchurch network, an annual Microchurch Conference, multiple Exponential commitments.
- **Flatten him into "microchurch author"**: he is also a 25-year megachurch veteran, an Exponential principal, a Hirsch co-author, a NewThing leader, a podcast host, and an India-experienced practitioner. The frame must hold all of those.
- **Treat Wegner as an implementation buyer**: he is a trusted-voice / ecosystem-layer candidate per Movemental's canonical doctrine. Onboard him as an ecosystem peer, not as a customer of Movemental's tooling.
