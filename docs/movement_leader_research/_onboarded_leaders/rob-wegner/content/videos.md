## Assessment

**Wegner's video footprint is real but invisible.** Specifically:

- **At least 10 distinct video appearances** confirmed across at least 6 different host channels.
- **No personal YouTube channel** — anyone trying to follow "Rob Wegner on video" has nowhere to land.
- **No curated playlist** on any platform that aggregates his teaching across the 6 host channels.
- **Conference talks at Exponential** (2019, 2024, multiple regionals) almost certainly exist as recorded sessions; they live in Exponential's archive under Exponential's brand, not Wegner's.

**Movemental implication**: A single curated YouTube playlist — drawn from existing org-channel uploads with permission — could surface 5–10 hours of teaching that is currently distributed across 6+ channels. Cross-linking that playlist into `robwegner.org`, the joint Substack, and the KCU site would meaningfully consolidate the surface without requiring new video production.
