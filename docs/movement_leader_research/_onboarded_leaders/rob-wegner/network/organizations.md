## Notable partnerships

- **The KCU + Tampa Underground partnership.** KCU explicitly takes the "Underground" naming convention; KCU sent ~20 leaders to help host the 2026 Tampa Microchurch Conference; the city-movement family lineage is Sanders → Tampa → KCU (and Pulley's Carbondale before Tampa). The two networks co-promote at every level.
- **The Hirsch + Ford + Wegner triangulation.** *The Starfish and the Spirit* (Zondervan 2021) put three voices on the same cover: Hirsch (academic theological), Ford (consulting / Sentralized), Wegner (KCU practitioner). The Starfish Initiatives consulting brand subsequently formalized that triangulation as a four-leader operating partnership (adding Rich Robinson).
- **The Wegner + Brian Johnson partnership.** Co-founder of KCU; Microchurch NEXT Co-Director at Exponential; co-author of *Extraordinary Prayer* (2024) and *Deeply Rooted* (2025); co-host of Microchurch NEXT Podcast. Johnson is Wegner's closest current operational partner.
- **The Wegner + Brian Phipps partnership.** Co-author of *Find Your Place*; Wegner sits on Disciples Made executive team. Phipps founded Disciples Made out of a 2010 calling; the Find Your Place program runs jointly between KCU and Disciples Made.
- **The NewThing → Exponential merger (April 1, 2025).** Patrick O'Connell becomes Executive Director of Practice; Dave Ferguson remains CEO of the combined Exponential; NewThing is integrated as Exponential's multiplication-network arm. Wegner's GLT seat persists post-merger.
