## Platform Assessment

Roy produces consistently across books, blog, and weekly podcast — but distribution is **dispersed**: podcast on third-party directories, video on others' channels, books on retailer pages. The owned surfaces (roymoran.com, mercyalliance.org) anchor the corpus but do not yet centralize audio/video archives or email capture.
