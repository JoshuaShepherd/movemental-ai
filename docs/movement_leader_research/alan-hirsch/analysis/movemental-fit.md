## What Movemental Would Change for This Author

### Problems Movemental Solves

- **Content fragmentation**: 6+ owned domains (alanhirsch.org, 5qcentral.com, 100movements.org, forgeinternational.com, theforgottenways.org, ephesiology.com) with no unified content experience. Movemental provides a single, discoverable profile connecting all content.
- **Video homelessness**: 38+ videos scattered across 20+ third-party channels with no centralized home. Movemental provides a video library with proper organization, search, and recommendation.
- **Assessment dead-end**: 150K+ people take the APEST assessment and then... leave. No content journey, no follow-up, no nurture. Movemental can connect assessment results to relevant content (articles, courses, videos) by APEST type.
- **Email conversion failure**: 37.9K social followers with only 1.5K email subscribers (4% conversion vs. industry standard 15-25%). Movemental's engagement infrastructure drives email capture and audience ownership.
- **Content siloing**: Books exist on Amazon. Articles exist on Saturate. Videos exist on Verge Network. Courses exist on Ephesiology. None are connected. Movemental creates cross-content discovery and recommendation.
- **Invisible embodied work**: Seminary teaching, consulting, mentoring, and organizational leadership generate enormous value that never reaches a wider audience. Movemental provides a capture-and-publish pipeline.
- **Monetization gap**: Hirsch's intellectual property (frameworks, assessments, courses) is under-monetized compared to peers like Mike Breen (3DM) who have built complete digital ecosystems. Movemental provides course hosting, subscription access, and premium content infrastructure.

## What We Will NOT Do

- **Replace alanhirsch.org**: His personal website remains his personal site. Movemental is an additional platform, not a replacement.
- **Subsume organizational brands**: 100Movements, 5Q Central, Forge, and MLC retain their own identities and websites. Movemental links to them; it does not absorb them.
- **Change his voice**: Hirsch's prophetic-apostolic tone, his "not X but Y" contrasts, his theological depth -- all preserved exactly as they are. No editorial homogenization.
- **Force a posting schedule**: Hirsch's content cadence has always been irregular and book-driven. We work with his rhythm, not against it. The platform structures and surfaces content -- it does not demand production.
- **Monetize without consent**: Every paid offering (courses, premium content, subscriptions) requires his explicit approval and collaboration. No paywalling of existing free content without discussion.
- **Alter theological positions**: His missional-incarnational theology, his critique of Christendom, his APEST framework, his emphasis on "Jesus is Lord" as the organizing center -- all remain untouched. Movemental is theologically neutral infrastructure.
- **Claim ownership of his frameworks**: mDNA, APEST, 5Q, Apostolic Genius -- these are Hirsch's intellectual property. Movemental surfaces them; it does not appropriate them.
