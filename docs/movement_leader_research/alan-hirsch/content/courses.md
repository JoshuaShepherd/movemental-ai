## Key Observations

1. **Multi-platform**: Training is distributed across Ephesiology, 5Q Central, Forge (Teachable), Future Travelers, and academic institutions.
2. **Framework-centered**: All courses center on Hirsch's core frameworks (APEST/5Q, mDNA, missional movement).
3. **Assessment integration**: The 5Q/APEST assessment (150,000+ taken) is deeply integrated into the training ecosystem.
4. **Gap**: No widely accessible, low-cost or free standalone online course on a major platform (Udemy, Coursera, etc.). Training is primarily through proprietary channels.
5. **Academic credibility**: Adjunct faculty positions at multiple seminaries lend academic weight to the training content.
