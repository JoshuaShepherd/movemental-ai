### Influence Indicators
- His coined terms ("mDNA," "Apostolic Genius," "missional-incarnational impulse," "APEST," "communitas") have entered common usage in missional church vocabulary
- The phrase "the church comes out of mission" is frequently attributed to him
- His frameworks are adopted and debated by scholars and practitioners across denominational lines
