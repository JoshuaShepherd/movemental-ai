# Audience Analysis: Andrew Jones (Tall Skinny Kiwi)

**Status**: Complete
**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

---

## Overview

Andrew Jones — known online as **Tall Skinny Kiwi** — reaches a globally dispersed audience of emerging-church practitioners, missional innovators, church planters, and digital-culture observers. His readership formed over 20+ years of blogging and has followed him through San Francisco, Prague, continental Europe, and now the Orkney Islands, Scotland.

He is best understood as a **connector and chronicler**, not primarily a book author: someone who narrated the emerging church movement in real time for practitioners trying to find footing.

---

## Primary Audience Segments

### Emerging / Missional Church Practitioners

- Leaders experimenting with alternative worship, simple churches, and post-Christendom forms
- Seek rhizomatic, grass-roots planting models rather than hierarchical multiplication
- Consume long-form blog essays and podcast interviews

### Church Planters and Simple-Church Advocates

- House church and simple-church networks
- Interest in New Monasticism and contemporary renewal movements
- Practical, field-tested stories over institutional strategy

### Mission and Social-Transformation Workers

- International aid workers, impact entrepreneurs, urban transformation projects
- 30+ years across 70+ countries informs a global perspective
- Justice, financial sustainability, and urban renewal themes

### Digital-Culture and Faith Observers

- Bloggers, social media consultants, festival/conference attendees
- Interest in faith inside networked and metaverse-adjacent culture (e.g. Greenbelt 2022 talk)
