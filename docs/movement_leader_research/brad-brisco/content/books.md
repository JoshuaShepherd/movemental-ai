| Cover | — |

**Description**: Presents nine key paradigm shifts the church needs to experience when desiring to equip and activate the people of God to engage in His mission. Explores 12 missiological principles to help rethink church multiplication and change how we think about God's mission and the nature of the church. The 9 paradigm shifts include: (1) The Need to ReThink, (2) ReThink the Nature of the Church: From Church Served to Church Sent, (3) ReThink Mission: From Excarnate Mission to Incarnate Mission, (4) ReThink the Missio Dei: From Church with a Mission to Mission with a Church, (5) ReThink Culture: From Place of Privilege to Posture of a Servant, (6) ReThink Vocation: From Secular Vocations to Sacred Callings, and additional paradigm shifts through number 9.

**Key Themes**: Missional ecclesiology, paradigm shifts, missio Dei, incarnational mission, cultural engagement, vocation, church activation
