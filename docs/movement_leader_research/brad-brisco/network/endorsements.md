## Endorsement Network Analysis

### Key Patterns

1. **Alan Hirsch is the most consistent endorser** -- endorsed at least 2 of Brad's books (Missional Essentials, The Missional Quest). This reflects their close relationship through the Forge network.

2. **Endorsers span the missional movement ecosystem** -- from Forge (Hirsch), to broader evangelicalism (Stetzer, Ferguson), to emerging/missional (Kimball, Saxton), to practitioners (Engler, Briggs).

3. **Endorsements are consistently for the Brisco-Ford partnership** -- all endorsed books are co-authored with Lance Ford, reinforcing that the Brisco-Ford brand carries more weight than either individual alone in the book market.

4. **Publisher diversity in endorsers** -- endorsers come from InterVarsity Press, NavPress, and Beacon Hill circles, suggesting Brad's work bridges denominational and publishing house boundaries.

5. **Foreword by Ed Stetzer** -- having Stetzer write the foreword for The Missional Quest was a significant credibility boost, given Stetzer's broad platform in evangelical leadership.

### Endorser Tiers

**Tier 1 -- Major Platform Leaders (broad evangelical influence)**
- Ed Stetzer (foreword)

- Reggie McNeal

**Tier 2 -- Missional Movement Leaders (significant niche influence)**
