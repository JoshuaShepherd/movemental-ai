# Audience Analysis: Brian Sanders

**Status**: Complete
**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

---

## Overview

Brian Sanders reaches **microchurch practitioners, mission-organized church leaders, and disillusioned evangelicals** through the Underground Network model born in Tampa (2006). His audience spans local Tampa microchurches, 15–30+ sister movements worldwide, and readers of six published books.

---

## Primary Audience Segments

### Microchurch Planters and Coaches

- Leaders starting or coaching worship+community+mission expressions
- MicroChurch Conference and Network Intensive participants
- *Microchurches: A Smaller Way* and *Underground Church* readers

### Disillusioned Institutional-Church Christians

- *Life After Church* audience — Christians leaving traditional church seeking calling
- Intentional community practitioners (*Life In Intentional Christian Community*)

### Collegiate / Urban Mission Alumni

- InterVarsity Florida network; Tampa Urban Project legacy
- Empowerment-of-ordinary-believers framing

### Movement Network Leaders

- Sister movements (Kansas City Underground adjacency, Ignite Dublin, etc.)
- Exponential Series readers (Alan Hirsch foreword network)

---

## Content Preferences

- Practitioner narrative over academic theory
- Mission-first ecclesiology; anti-gathering-centric framing
- Justice and the poor as church identity markers
- Ordinary disciples as Kingdom agents
