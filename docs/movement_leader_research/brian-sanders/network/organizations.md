# Organizations: Brian Sanders

## Underground Network / Tampa Underground
- **Founded**: summer 2006 (~50 people leaving traditional churches to form seven house-based church groups). ([Tampa Underground](https://www.tampaunderground.com/who-we-are))
- **What it is**: a nonprofit "network of relationship and resources" — a fellowship of **microchurches** organized around mission, local ministry, the poor, the lost, and biblical justice. The large network exists to serve the needs of small microchurches. ([Underground Network](https://www.undergroundnetwork.org/))
- **Microchurch definition**: "the coalescing of three intentional practices: worship, community, and mission" — the "ecclesial minimum." ([Microchurches: A Smaller Way](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/53009657-microchurches))
- **Scale (Tampa)**: 100+ active microchurches currently; earlier reports cited 136 locations and "over 200" / "over 450" microchurches across the movement's history. ([Underground Network](https://www.undergroundnetwork.org/), [Outreach Magazine](https://outreachmagazine.com/features/18604-tampa-underground.html))
- **Sister movements**: 15–30+ city-wide movements worldwide, including Kansas City Underground (KS), Narrative Church (AR), Lantern Network (IL), Wildfire (TX), Ignite Dublin (Ireland), Yangon (Myanmar), MUG Sambahayan (Philippines), HOPE (Dominican Republic), Paris (France), Zoe (Toronto), Kx Microchurch Network (UK), Renewal Network (Cape Town), and others. ([Underground Network](https://www.undergroundnetwork.org/))
- **Current leadership**: Executive Director **Joel Repic**; Board Chair **Bill Couchenour**; Tampa Director Tomy Wilkerson; Managing Director Stacy Gaskins; Movements Director Lucas Pulley. **Brian Sanders** is founder and a Board of Directors member (former Executive Director). ([Tampa Underground — Who We Are](https://www.tampaunderground.com/who-we-are))
- **Programs/events**: MicroChurch Conference (every ~2 years, Tampa); Microchurch Network Intensive (cohort-based training); webinars; Immersive Visits; coaching, finance, media, telehealth, and nonprofit-formation services; the Microchurches Podcast. ([Underground Network](https://www.undergroundnetwork.org/))
- **Listing**: Tampa Underground appears in the Outreach 100. ([outreach100.com](https://outreach100.com/churches/tampa-underground))
