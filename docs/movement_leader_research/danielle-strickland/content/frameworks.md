# Frameworks

### The Ultimate Exodus

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2017 (*The Ultimate Exodus*) |
| Components | Naming what enslaves; the pattern of liberation drawn from the Exodus narrative; personal and structural freedom |
| Source | *The Ultimate Exodus: Finding Freedom from What Enslaves You* (NavPress / InterVarsity Press) |

A reading of the Exodus story as a repeatable pattern for finding freedom from whatever enslaves a person or a system.

### Better Together

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2020 (*Better Together*) |
| Components | Imagine a better world; understand oppression; men and women healing the gender divide; working together to transform the future |
| Source | *Better Together: How Women and Men Can Heal the Divide and Work Together to Transform the Future* (W Publishing Group) |

The conviction that men and women working together — rather than apart — is essential to healing the gender divide and transforming the future of the church.

### Living Life in Overflow

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2013 (*Boundless: Living Life in Overflow*) |
| Components | A Spirit-filled life that overflows its own borders; abundance spilling into mission and justice |
| Source | *Boundless: Living Life in Overflow* (Monarch Books), with Stephen Court |

The picture of a faith so full it overflows past personal piety into mission, justice, and service to the world.

### The Other Side of Hope

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2022 (*The Other Side of Hope*) |
| Components | Cynicism and despair as spiritual conditions; flipping the script; recovering hope as a discipline; rediscovering humanity |
| Source | *The Other Side of Hope: Flipping the Script on Cynicism and Despair and Rediscovering Our Humanity* (Thomas Nelson) |

A discipline for resisting cynicism and despair and choosing hope as the path back to a shared humanity.

### Brave (Brave Global)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | — (Brave Global / The Brave Campaign) |
