# Organizations

## Founded / led

- **Boundless Communications Inc.** — the umbrella organization through which Danielle Strickland launches and runs her ventures (registered at a Toronto address). Contact: danielle@boundlesscom.ca.
- **Brave Global** (also "The Brave Campaign") — preventing and confronting human trafficking by mobilizing and equipping young women and partner churches.
- **Amplify Peace** — global peacemaking immersions that take leaders into places of conflict (Rwanda, the Middle East) to hold grief and hope together.
- **Women Speakers Collective** — developing and platforming women communicators.
- **Infinitum** — a rule-of-life / deep-living community (co-founded).
- **Hagar's Voice** — support and advocacy for survivors of abuse.
- **IMBY (In My Backyard)** — a tiny-home / affordable-housing movement.
