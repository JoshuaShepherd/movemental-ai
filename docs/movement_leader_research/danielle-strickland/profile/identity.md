# Identity

## Roles

- Communicator, author, and global social-justice advocate
- Peacemaker and abuse-survivor advocate
- Founder / leader of **Boundless Communications Inc.** (the umbrella over her ventures)
- Founder or co-founder of **Brave Global**, **Amplify Peace**, the **Women Speakers Collective**, **Infinitum**, **Hagar's Voice**, and **IMBY (In My Backyard)**
- Host of the **Right Side Up** podcast (with co-host James Sholl)
- **Ambassador for Stop The Traffik**
- Recurring main-stage speaker at the **Global Leadership Summit**

## Tradition and formation

- Spent roughly **twenty-two years as a commissioned officer in The Salvation Army**, rising to the rank of **Major**.
- Within the Army she planted churches, founded training schools, and helped stand up **Justice Departments** and global **anti-trafficking** initiatives.
- Married to **Stephen Court**, also a Salvation Army officer and co-author of *Boundless: Living Life in Overflow*.
- The Salvation Army's fusion of evangelical faith with frontline social action is the formative grammar of her entire body of work.

## What she is known for

- Bringing **justice and the gospel into the same sentence** — trafficking prevention, peacemaking, and women's empowerment framed as spiritual work, not adjacent to it.
- **Brave Global / The Brave Campaign** — mobilizing young women and preventing and confronting human trafficking.
- **Amplify Peace** — global peacemaking immersions (Rwanda, the Middle East).
- Advocacy for **female leadership in the church**, crystallized in the curated collection *Need to Know* and the book *Better Together*.
- A frontline-practitioner posture: she has done the work on the ground before writing about it.
