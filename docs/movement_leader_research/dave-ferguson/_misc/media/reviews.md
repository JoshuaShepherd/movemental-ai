### Goodreads Reviews (Selected)

| Reviewer | Rating | Key Quote |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Via Nations student readers | Various | Group study notes; "Hero Maker" used as summer 2021 reading group text | [vianations.org](https://www.vianations.org/students/podcasts/summer-sessions-2021/hero-maker-by-dave-ferguson) |

**Assessment**: *Hero Maker* is Ferguson's most widely reviewed leadership book. Practitioners embrace the five-practice framework. The Lifeway Research rating of "Helpful" (vs. "Essential" or "Recommended") reflects a mild critique: the book is practical and encouraging but not theologically groundbreaking. The Andrews University academic journal review of the *Legacy of a Hero Maker* companion is the only confirmed academic-level engagement.

### Published Reviews

| # | Source | Reviewer | Assessment | Key Quote | URL |
|---|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | Jeff McLain (blog) | Jeff McLain | Positive | "B.L.E.S.S. provides practical tools for everyday evangelism without being formulaic." | [jeffmclain.com](https://www.jeffmclain.com/book-review-bless-by-dave-and-jon-ferguson/) |
