## Event Network Summary

### Primary Event Ecosystems
1. **Exponential Conference**: Ferguson's primary and defining platform — co-founder, CEO, and annual keynote for nearly 20 years
2. **NewThing Network Events**: Catalyst Communities and movement leadership gatherings across 60+ nations (pre-2025 merger)
3. **Amplify Conference**: New partnership event (Wheaton College, 2025)
4. **Podcast Circuit**: Consistent presence across major church leadership podcasts; especially Carey Nieuwhof, Ed Stetzer / ChurchLeaders, unSeminary
5. **Wheaton College Graduate School**: Academic teaching presence

### Geographic Reach
- **Primary base**: Naperville / Chicago, Illinois
- **National**: Primary Exponential conference in Orlando + regional Exponential events across U.S.
- **International**: Exponential Europe (Berlin 2020 confirmed); NewThing networks in nearly 70 countries

### Event Frequency Assessment
- **Annual**: Exponential Conference (flagship Orlando event + regional)
- **Regular**: Podcast interviews (multiple per year across platforms)
- **Ongoing**: Wheaton Graduate School teaching
- **Periodic**: Guest speaking at external conferences (Verge, Amplify, Catalyst — confirmed or likely)
