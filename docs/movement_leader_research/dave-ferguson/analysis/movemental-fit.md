## Fit Score Summary

**8.2/10 — Very Strong Fit.** Dave Ferguson is one of the highest-value candidates in the Movemental research pipeline. He combines an enormous institutional infrastructure (the world's largest church-planting conference) with a genuinely thin personal owned digital audience — exactly the gap Movemental is designed to close. His content volume, quality, audience alignment, and network effects are exceptional. His digital maturity and owned audience size lag significantly behind his stature, creating both urgency and opportunity. The *Multiplier* (2026) launch is the natural inflection point for engagement.

**Adjustments from the institutional factor**: The score would be higher (potentially 9.0+) if all his reach were personally owned. The institutional dependency — the fact that Exponential owns his email list, conference platform, and podcast — is the primary moderating factor. Movemental's value proposition is most powerful for leaders in exactly this situation: enormous influence, thin owned audience, high motivation to change the equation. Ferguson's situation is perhaps the clearest possible argument for why a leader of his scale needs a personally owned hub.

## What Movemental Would Change for This Author

### Problems Movemental Solves

- **Institutional dependency**: Ferguson's most significant reach channels (Exponential conference email list, NEXT Podcast listenership, conference registration database, NewThing network contacts) are institutionally owned. Movemental provides a personally owned hub — daveferguson.movemental.com or equivalent — that is his regardless of institutional circumstances.

- **The Substack URL problem**: daveferguson3.substack.com is a permanent credibility friction point. A Movemental-hosted newsletter with a clean URL resolves this and creates a foundation for subscriber growth.

- **No email capture on personal website**: daveferguson.org has no email capture form. Movemental's author profile includes a native newsletter signup that captures personally owned subscribers.

- **No course platform**: The Hero Maker five practices, the Five-Level framework, the BLESS program, and the RPMS gauges are fully developed pedagogical systems with zero self-paced digital course expression. Movemental's course infrastructure makes "Hero Maker in 5 Sessions" and "The Five Levels: Diagnosing Your Church" possible immediately.

- **No community infrastructure**: Thousands of leaders have read *Hero Maker* and attended Exponential with no ongoing community to practice what they learned. A Movemental-hosted Hero Maker Practitioner Network provides year-round community extending the conference experience.

- **Fragmentation across multiple platforms**: Personal website, Substack (awkward URL), 3 book companion sites (heromakerbook.org, bless-book.org, multiplierbook.com), Exponential institutional content, social media, and podcast appearances operate as disconnected islands. Movemental provides a unified hub with cross-referenced content.

- **No video library**: Ferguson's most impactful teaching video lives on Exponential's channel. Movemental's video infrastructure gives Ferguson a personally owned video home to aggregate and build.

- **No Wikipedia page**: Despite 10 trade books, 6,373+ churches planted, and CEO of the world's largest church-planting conference, Ferguson has no Wikipedia page. While Movemental does not create Wikipedia pages directly, the Movemental author profile provides the canonical public-facing summary that supports a Wikipedia draft.

## What We Will NOT Do

- **Replace Exponential**: Movemental is a personal brand platform, not a replacement for Exponential's organizational role. Exponential continues as the conference, training, and network infrastructure. Movemental is Ferguson's personally owned voice, independent of and complementary to his institutional role.
- **Compete with publisher book sales**: Books continue to be sold through Zondervan, WaterBrook/PRH, Salem Books, and Amazon. Movemental links and promotes but does not extract or replace.
- **Dilute the framework accessibility**: Ferguson's signature value is making complex ideas simple and reproducible. Movemental will surface these frameworks accessibly, with visual design and interactive tools — not academic treatments that change their character.
- **Change his theological positions**: Multiplication theology, hero-making leadership, BLESS neighboring, Restoration Movement heritage — all preserved exactly as they are.
- **Force social media activity beyond his natural cadence**: If Ferguson posts weekly on Substack and periodically on X, that rhythm continues. Movemental enhances what exists rather than demanding Instagram Reels or TikTok content.
- **Compete with Exponential's email list**: Exponential's institutional email list is Exponential's asset. Movemental builds Ferguson's personal email list through personal channels (newsletter, lead magnets, book companion integration) — distinct and additive, not competitive.
- **Pressure monetization beyond what serves the audience**: Some of Ferguson's best content (free Exponential eBooks, free BLESS chapter download, free podcast appearances) is deliberately free and accessible. Movemental provides monetization infrastructure but does not require its use for content that serves audiences best as free resources.
