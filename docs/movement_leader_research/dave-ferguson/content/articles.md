## Summary

Dave Ferguson does not maintain a high-volume personal blog on the model of a full-time blogger. His written output is distributed across three channels: (1) his personal site daveferguson.org (light blog), (2) Exponential.org (where he posts as a leader and contributor), and (3) third-party publications including Outreach Magazine, Lewis Center for Church Leadership, Christianity Today, and Global Leadership Network. He also maintains a newer Substack newsletter (launched early 2026). Total confirmed articles across all platforms: 30–50+.

## Key Thematic Clusters Across Articles

### Primary Themes (appear in 5+ pieces)
1. **Church Multiplication / The Five Levels** — Exponential's framework for categorizing churches from addition to multiplication; appears throughout 2014–present
2. **Hero Making / Leadership Multiplication** — The shift from addition-thinking to multiplication-thinking in leadership development; peak 2017–2021
3. **Evangelism and Missional Outreach** — BLESS practices, "lost cause" evangelism revitalization, seeker-focused approaches; peak 2015 and 2022–2023

### Secondary Themes (appear in 2–4 pieces)
4. **Spirit-Empowered Ministry** — Exponential 2022 conference theme; four articles in the "Empowered" series
5. **Church Collaboration / Networks** — Together framework, network-based movement; 2020 emphasis
6. **Church Planting Pipeline** — Recruiting, training, and deploying church planters; recurring across Outreach and Lewis Center pieces
