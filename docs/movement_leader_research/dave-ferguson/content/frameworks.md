# Dave Ferguson — Signature Frameworks

**Catalog Date**: 2026-06-02
**Status**: Grounded in Ferguson's published books and primary teaching sources (see `profile/theology.md`, `content/books.md`, and the linked URLs).

Each framework below is drawn from Dave Ferguson's own books and teaching. The one-sentence prose summary under each table is the dossier card body.

---

### Five Practices of a Hero Maker

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2018 (*Hero Maker: Five Essential Practices for Leaders to Multiply Leaders*, Zondervan, with Warren Bird) |
| Components | Multiplication Thinking; Permission Giving; Disciple Multiplying; Gift Activating; Kingdom Building |
| Source | [heromakerbook.org](https://heromakerbook.org/) |

A hero maker is a leader who stops trying to be the hero and instead makes heroes of others, multiplying leaders through five practices — multiplication thinking, permission giving, disciple multiplying, gift activating, and kingdom building.

---

### BLESS — Five Everyday Missional Practices

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2020 (*B.L.E.S.S.: 5 Everyday Ways to Love Your Neighbor and Change the World*, Salem/Regnery, with Jon Ferguson) |
| Components | Begin with prayer; Listen; Eat; Serve; Share your story |
| Source | [bless-book.org](https://www.bless-book.org/) |

BLESS distills everyday incarnational neighboring into five repeatable practices — begin with prayer, listen, eat, serve, and share your story — so that mission belongs to every ordinary Christian rather than to a program.

### ICNU — "I See in You" (Permission Giving)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2018 (*Hero Maker*, practice two — Permission Giving) |
| Components | Naming latent leadership in another; the ICNU conversation; calibrated release across six permission-giving levels |
| Source | [exponential.org/hero-maker-2-permission-giver](https://exponential.org/hero-maker-2-permission-giver/) |

ICNU is the practice of seeing and naming the leadership potential in someone before they see it in themselves — the "I see in you" conversation that releases people into ministry and forms the engine of permission giving.

### Five-Step Apprenticeship Model

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2010 (*Exponential*, Zondervan, with Jon Ferguson); developed further in *Hero Maker* (2018) |
| Components | I do, you watch, we talk → I do, you help, we talk → You do, I help, we talk → You do, I watch, we talk → You do, someone else watches |
| Source | [unseminary.com — CCC leadership succession](https://unseminary.com/embracing-the-future-with-humility-community-christians-leadership-succession-with-ted-coniaris/) |

The five-step apprenticeship model makes disciples who make disciples by moving an apprentice through progressive stages — from watching to being watched — until they can reproduce the practice in someone else.

### Five-Level Multiplying Church Typology

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2016 (*Becoming a Level Five Multiplying Church*, Exponential, with Todd Wilson and Alan Hirsch) |
| Components | Level 1 Declining; Level 2 Plateaued; Level 3 Growing; Level 4 Reproducing; Level 5 Multiplying |
| Source | [theunstuckgroup.com — Level Five interview](https://theunstuckgroup.com/becoming-level-5-multiplying-church-interview-dave-ferguson/) |

Ferguson's five-level typology diagnoses where a church sits on the spectrum from declining to multiplying, arguing that most churches plateau at "growing" (Level 3) and that genuine movement requires reproducing and multiplying churches (Levels 4–5).

### The Great Collaboration (the "Three Greats")

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2020 (*Together: The Great Collaboration*, Exponential, with Patrick O'Connell) |
| Components | Great Commission (Go); Great Commandment (Love); Great Collaboration (Together) |
| Source | [exponential.org/bettertogether](https://exponential.org/bettertogether/) |

The Great Collaboration holds that Jesus' John 17 prayer adds a third "great" alongside the Commission and the Commandment — that reproducing churches advance the kingdom together as networked relationships, not as isolated congregations.

---

### Five Awakenings (Finding Your Way Back to God)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2015 (*Finding Your Way Back to God: Five Awakenings to Your New Life*, WaterBrook, with Jon Ferguson) |
| Components | Awakening to Longing; to Regret; to Help; to Love; to Life |
| Source | [waterbrookmultnomah.com — Finding Your Way Back to God](https://waterbrookmultnomah.com/books/235828/finding-your-way-back-to-god-by-dave-ferguson-and-jon-ferguson/) |

The Five Awakenings map the spiritual journey of the returning person through the lens of the Prodigal Son — longing, regret, help, love, and life — giving churches a shared language for evangelism and re-engagement.

### RPMS — Four Leader-Health Gauges

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2026 (*Multiplier: How Healthy Leaders Create Lasting Impact*, Zondervan) |
| Components | Relational; Physical; Mental; Spiritual (daily self-assessment, drawn from Luke 2:52) |
| Source | [globalleadership.org — combat drift](https://globalleadership.org/articles/leading-yourself/do-this-every-day-to-combat-drift/) |

RPMS gives leaders four daily gauges — relational, physical, mental, and spiritual — to monitor their own health and resist drift, on the conviction that you reproduce who you are, so only healthy leaders are worth multiplying.
