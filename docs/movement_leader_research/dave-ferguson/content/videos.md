## Executive Summary

Dave Ferguson **has a personal YouTube channel** (https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCr4AirrEAgHM_mIdcebNm-w), though its subscriber count and exact video count were not retrievable at research date. His video content is substantial but distributed across multiple platforms: his personal channel, the Exponential YouTube channel (where he appears in annual conference sessions), RightNow Media (gated/subscription-based educational content), and podcast video channels. He also appears in Vineyard USA videos, GCM, and Australian Exponential productions.

- **Estimated total videos across all platforms**: 60–100+
- **Date range**: 2010 (earliest confirmed) to 2026 (ongoing)

- **Notable**: Ferguson personally narrates his *Multiplier* audiobook (2026) and has appeared on video-podcast channels in recent years

## Key Themes Across Video Content

1. **Hero Making / Leadership Multiplication** — Five practices for shifting from hero to hero maker; the dominant content theme from 2017–2022
2. **Church Multiplication Framework (5 Levels)** — Becoming Level Five; the Exponential multiplication scale
3. **Missional Living / BLESS** — Neighbor engagement; organic evangelism (2020–2021)
4. **Spirit-Empowered Ministry** — Exponential 2022 conference theme; four-part teaching series
5. **Church Planting Trends** — Micro-churches, meta-churches, network leadership (2021–2024)
6. **Evangelism Revitalization** — "Lost Cause" series; reframing evangelism for post-Christian contexts (2022–2023)
7. **Multiplier / Leader Health** — Four gauges of leader health; sustainable multiplication (2025–2026)
