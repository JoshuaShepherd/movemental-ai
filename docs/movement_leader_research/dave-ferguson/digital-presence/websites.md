### Site Structure

daveferguson.org functions as a personal author/leader hub. The homepage leads with three featured books (*Multiplier*, *B.L.E.S.S.*, *Hero Maker*), a short bio, and links to the Substack newsletter (labeled "my latest thoughts"). Navigation is minimal: Home, Books, About Dave, Blog, Contact. The contact page has a freeform message form.

The blog archive is sparse — 17 posts total, most from 2015–2016, with a gap until 2021 (four posts) and then a further gap until October–November 2024 (two posts). The blog is not the primary content channel; the Substack newsletter has largely replaced it.

Most prominent CTA network: book purchase links (Amazon) and the Substack newsletter link. The website does not run its own email list or lead-magnet funnel.
