# Press Coverage & Media Mentions: Dave Ferguson

**Research Date**: 2026-05-27
**Subject**: Dave Ferguson — church planter, movement leader, author; CEO/President of Exponential; Co-Founding Pastor, Community Christian Church
**Confidence Level**: High (primary sources verified via web search)

---

## Summary

Dave Ferguson's media presence is substantial and recurring — significantly greater than many peer movement leaders — concentrated in three zones: (1) Outreach Magazine (his most consistent institutional press home), (2) the national evangelical/church leadership media circuit (Christianity Today Pastors, ChurchLeaders, Christian Post), and (3) major church leadership podcasts. He has been featured in Outreach Magazine more than a dozen times across 20 years, appeared on CT Pastors as a contributing author, and received broad institutional coverage for his role at Exponential and as an author. Community Christian Church's inclusion in the Outreach 100 (2007, 2011) contributed early visibility.
