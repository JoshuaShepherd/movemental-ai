Notes:
- **The Tangible Kingdom** has multiple printing ISBNs across editions: hardcover 9780470188972, a paperback 9780470580233, and an e-book 9780470435335. Foreword by Alan Hirsch (Forge); endorsements include Neil Cole and Doug Pagitt (per book front matter / 9Marks review).
- **A Righteous Brood: Making Your Family the Front Line of Mission** (Goodreads 17403261) is an earlier/variant title of the *Righteous Brood* material; treated as a variant, not a separate title.
- **Equip: Practical Ways to Train Missional Leaders** (Verge Network eBook) is a multi-contributor compilation (Dave Ferguson, JR Woodward, Reggie McNeal, Lance Ford, Rob Wegner, Hugh Halter, et al.) — a contribution, not a Halter-authored book. ISBN unknown.
- **Winning the Trading Game** (with Noble Drakoln) appears on the Goodreads author list but is a financial-trading title by a different "Hugh Halter" namesake — EXCLUDED as not the subject.

## Co-authorship summary
- **Matt Smay** — co-author of *The Tangible Kingdom* (2008), *The Tangible Kingdom Primer* (2009), and *AND: The Gathered and Scattered Church* (2010). Primary writing partner.
- **Taylor McCall** — co-author of *Brave Cities* (2024).
- **Dave Ferguson & Sue Ferguson** — collaborators credited on *Righteous Brood* (2023).
