# Organizations

Jeremy Chambers' organizational life spans three connected worlds: missional-movement training, microchurch networks, and the contemplative-formation tradition. His affiliations are documented below.

**Forge America** is the U.S. arm of Alan Hirsch's globally networked missional training movement, and Chambers serves as its Training Director — a national role current as of 2024–26 that gives him curricular stewardship over Forge's training pipeline and positions him as a coach and consultant to denominational and church leaders. ([forgeamerica.com](https://www.forgeamerica.com/our-people))

**The Pando Collective** is a Front Range network, headquartered in Colorado Springs, that exists to connect, resource, and launch missional micro-expressions of the church across Colorado Springs, Denver, and the surrounding region. Chambers leads the Denver expression, which he helped launch beginning in 2020 in partnership with Pando founder Rowland Smith — the connection that came through Forge. ([thepandocollective.com](https://thepandocollective.com/))

**The Arise RVA Church Network** (also referenced as the Arise Network) is the network of gospel-centered microchurch communities that Jeremy and Monica Chambers founded in Richmond, Virginia in 2013. It grew to roughly seven communities, affiliated with the V3 Movement under the Baptist General Association of Virginia, and is the seedbed of the couple's microchurch practice. ([ariserva.com](http://www.ariserva.com/), [bgav.org](https://www.bgav.org/church-directory/arise-rva-church))

**The V3 Church Planting Movement** is the Baptist General Association of Virginia's church-planting ministry, led nationally by JR Woodward. Chambers is an affiliated planter and a longtime contributing author to its blog (2018–2021). ([thev3movement.org](https://thev3movement.org/author/jeremy-chambers/))

**Renovaré** is the spiritual-formation network in the Richard Foster / Dallas Willard tradition. Chambers has been a contributor of articles and a podcast guest since 2022, reflecting the contemplative pole of his integrative thesis. ([renovare.org](https://renovare.org/))

**100 Movements Publishing** is the Hirsch-founded publishing arm of the Movement Leaders Collective and the publisher of both of Chambers' current books (*The Art of Missional Spirituality* and the revised *Kingdom Contours*). ([100mpublishing.com](https://100mpublishing.com/our-authors/))

He also holds a denominational affiliation with the **Baptist General Association of Virginia** via Arise RVA and V3, and has contributed an essay to **Missio Alliance** (2025). No board memberships or formal educational appointments beyond his TEDS alumnus status are documented.
