**Urgency**: Medium. Fixable in Month 1–3.

## What this gap means for positioning

Because Joel's primary gap is **uncreated content rather than fragmented content**, Movemental's value proposition to him is different from the typical "bring your scattered content under one roof" pitch:

- **For a Brad Brisco**: *"Aggregate your existing content into a single owned hub."*
- **For a Joel Repic**: *"Steward and externalize the body of work you have lived but never written."*

Both are legitimate. The second framing requires more editorial partnership and more trust. It is also a higher-value relationship on both sides — the leader gets durable preservation of their life's work, and the platform gets content that didn't exist in the ecosystem before.

---

## Risk: the "second home" trap

A specific risk for Joel: if Movemental launches his author hub while Tampa Underground is also launching new institutional content under his name, the two could become overlapping without a clear division of labor.

Recommended division:

| Surface | Content type |
|---|---|
| Tampa Underground (tampaunderground.com / undergroundnetwork.org) | Institutional teaching / announcements / Microchurches Podcast / network resources |
| Movemental (joelrepic.movemental.ai or via hopeisprotest.com) | Joel's personal / biographical / reflective / ED Letters / frameworks / memoir |

Clear lanes prevent audience confusion and prevent the Underground from treating Movemental as a competing surface.
