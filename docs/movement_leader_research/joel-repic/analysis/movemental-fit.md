## Why the score isn't higher

- **Content volume penalty**: a working Movemental author hub typically benefits from more pre-existing content than Joel has. If content volume weighted higher (or if we were a content-volume-first platform), the score would be lower. Because Movemental's doctrine explicitly frames movement leaders as ecosystem-layer voices, this penalty is muted.
- **Digital maturity penalty**: he is not a digital-native content creator. Some Movemental authors arrive with established newsletter infrastructure, YouTube habits, or Substack experience. Joel does not. This means onboarding requires more editorial partnership than with a digitally-mature author.
- **Education uncertainty**: his credentials are not publicly established. For certain audience contexts (academic, some denominational), this is a mild weakness until clarified.

## Why the score isn't lower

- **Everything else is very strong.** The four highest-weight dimensions (audience alignment, platform need, network effects, content quality) are all 8+ scores.
- **Doctrinal fit is perfect.** Joel is precisely the shape of leader the `movement-leaders-as-ecosystem-layer` doctrine describes.
- **The timing is unusual and valuable.** Onboarding a first-year ED of a 20-year-old network is a rare window.
