## Strategic observation

Audio is Joel's strongest content modality. His voice is preserved more in sermons and podcast appearances than in any other form. This has implications for Movemental onboarding:

1. **Transcript extraction from existing audio** is the single largest content-generation opportunity. 6 sermons + 4 podcast appearances = probably **15+ hours of unedited Joel Repic audio** that can be transcribed and turned into:
   - Written essays (lightly edited sermon transcripts)
   - Pull quotes / social media content
   - Framework documents (extracted from the Exponential interview)
   - Topical content clusters

2. **Audio-first new content** suits his voice better than video-first or text-first. An oral-history interview series produced as audio, with transcripts published, is the right format for capturing the 20-year Aliquippa chapter.

3. **The Gospel Tabernacle sermon archive is at risk.** Distributed across podcast mirrors that do not guarantee permanent hosting. Movemental should prioritize downloading and preserving these files early in the onboarding process.
