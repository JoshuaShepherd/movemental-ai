## Observations

1. **Sermon distribution mirroring is unusually redundant** — the same Gospel Tabernacle feed is surfaced through at least 6 podcast apps and 4 platforms. This is not a strategy; it's default podcast syndication behavior. It means Joel's sermons are technically discoverable but narratively untagged — no single index says "these are sermons by Joel Repic."
2. **The institutional-to-personal ratio is heavily institutional.** His audience encounters him primarily through Underground Network, CMA, Exponential, Leadership Network — not through hopeisprotest.com. This is exactly the doctrinal shape Movemental's "trusted voice" framing names: he is an **ecosystem-layer leader**, not an individual brand.
3. **A Movemental author hub under hopeisprotest.com branding** could serve as the missing aggregation layer — linking to (rather than replacing) the 13+ existing surfaces, with the newsletter as the connective tissue.
4. **The legacy CMA archive problem is urgent.** A 301 redirect means Google will eventually stop crawling those URLs entirely; the content may already be lost. This is the item most requiring immediate direct outreach to CMA communications.
