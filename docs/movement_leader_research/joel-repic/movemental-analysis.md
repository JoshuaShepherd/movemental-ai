## What Movemental would change for Joel

### Problems Movemental solves

1. **No owned digital hub.** hopeisprotest.com is dormant, Tampa Underground's site is his employer, the CMA archive is redirected, sermons live on a church feed he does not control. Movemental provides the first owned surface under his name that aggregates everything else.
2. **No audience he owns.** He has ~934 Instagram followers and an unknown social reach otherwise, but zero email subscribers. An author newsletter solves this in week one.
3. **Twenty years of ministry IP never externalized.** Aliquippa Impact, Greenhouse Network, CMA planting practices, and his own theology of hope are in his head and in the people he has formed. Movemental's structured capture tooling (long-form interviews, written frameworks, course modules) is the right infrastructure to get that out.
4. **At-risk archive.** The legacy CMA URL, the Gospel Tabernacle podcast feed, and the regional press articles are deteriorating — some already inaccessible. Movemental provides retention.
5. **Fragmented network visibility.** He is a node in at least three ecosystems (Underground, Greenhouse, CMA Western PA) but none of them know his full network. A Movemental author graph makes those connections legible and navigable.
6. **No narrative arc visible.** Anyone encountering him today through a single channel sees a slice. Movemental can tell the whole Pittsburgh → Aliquippa → Tampa arc as a single coherent story.

## What we will NOT do

- **Replace the Tampa Underground or undergroundnetwork.org surfaces.** Those are institutional; Movemental is additive.
- **Remove hopeisprotest.com.** We suggest repointing it or embedding it, not deleting it.
- **Reframe his theology.** His voice and convictions stay untouched. No ghostwriting, no "voice rewrite," no imposed framework.
- **Monetize before readiness.** He has no newsletter, no audience-owned list, no relationship of accountability with readers. Monetization comes only after a year of building trust.
- **Treat him as an audience-funnel segment.** Per canonical doctrine, he is a trusted-voice / ecosystem-layer onboarding — not an implementation-audience sale.
- **Recruit him as a "nominate a movement leader" roster entry.** He is onboarded through relationship, not form-filling.
- **Promise a visibility windfall.** His natural audience is medium-sized, earnest, and practitioner-heavy. Movemental's value to him is not virality but durable stewardship.
