# Josh Shepherd — Movement Leader Research

**Subject**: Joshua "Josh" Shepherd — Founder & CEO, Movemental LLC (Kansas City, MO)
**Research Date**: 2026-04-21
**Method**: Local-only (repos, business docs, resume SSoT, memory). No web sources by design — Josh is the platform's founder, not a public-facing thought leader.

## Why This Profile Exists

Every other folder in `docs/movement_leader_research/` profiles a public-facing missional leader in or adjacent to Alan Hirsch's network. This profile is different: it is the **builder of the platform itself**, profiled using the same template so the team has a comparable, structured read on the founder's identity, surfaces, voice, and posture.

Because Josh is not a public figure, the standard "public footprint" inventory comes back near-empty. The profile compensates by treating his **internal authored work** (Movemental business docs, founder narrative, values doc, code, GPTs, and resume SSoT) as the equivalent corpus.

## Headline Findings

1. **He is Movemental.** 70% equity, sole technical owner, sole operator. The other three founders (Hirsch, Brisco, Robinson) hold 10% each as theology / network / coaching strategic advisors.
2. **Public visibility is near-zero, and this is largely intentional.** The Movemental thesis — *scenius over genius* — pushes against founder-as-celebrity moves.
3. **Internal expression is enormous.** ~30 foundational business docs, the full platform, two production tenant builds (Hirsch, Brisco), hundreds of GPTs, a values architecture.
4. **The actionable gap is posture, not capability.** Almost everything needed to surface him cleanly already exists in his SSoT — it just hasn't been published anywhere.
5. **Recommended posture: "named anchor, quiet voice."** Surface him on the Movemental platform he is building (founder card, short first-person paragraph, `/team/josh` page, four-way founder team layout). Do not launch external broadcasting. See `movemental-analysis.md` for detail.
