## Voice Characteristics

- **Disposition**: Builder-pragmatic with formation-language warmth. Sentences tend to be claim + reason + concrete proof point.
- **Vocabulary**: "ship," "system," "infrastructure," "scenius," "voice preservation," "amplification," "movement multiplication," "credibility," "formation," "properly ordered."
- **Argument shape**: Strategy → Implementation → Justice / Mission framing. Rarely leaves an idea at the abstract level without grounding it in code, metrics, or relational outcome.
- **Stance toward AI**: Pro-capability, anti-homogenization. Treats "preserve voice" and "disclose use" as ethical floors, not aspirational ceilings.
- **Theological register**: Light-touch but unmistakable — operates inside the missional / Hirsch ecosystem; quotes formation language ("formation," "calling," "mission," "incarnational") without performing theology. The theological air is in the docs more than the marketing copy.
