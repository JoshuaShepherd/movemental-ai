# Gap Analysis: Josh Shepherd

**Research Date**: 2026-04-21
**Frame**: Most leader gap analyses ask "where is the embodied work that has not yet become digital expression?" For Josh, the picture is inverted — he has produced enormous digital expression (a platform, hundreds of GPTs, 30+ business docs), but virtually none of it is *attributed to him in public.* The gap is not embodied-vs-digital; it is **builder-as-system vs. builder-as-named-author.**

## What Movemental's Stack Already Solves For Him

The platform he is building is, in effect, the answer to most "movement leader" gaps for *other* leaders — owned platform, content centralization, voice-preserving AI, network discoverability, ethical economic model. He has full access to all of it.

**That means his actual gap is not capability — it's posture.** Specifically:

1. **Founder-as-author identity is undeclared.** The about page names him "Founder & Lead Engineer" with one paragraph. There is no equivalent of Alan Hirsch's body-of-work surface for Josh.
2. **No first-person primary source.** Almost everything he has authored is institutional voice (Movemental's docs) rather than first-person ("I think… I've learned… here's how I'd build this…").
3. **No record of the build is being kept publicly.** A platform like Movemental, built end-to-end by one founder/engineer, is itself a case study. None of that is currently in the open.
4. **No public proof of the formation lineage.** His decade in Mission House is the strongest credibility asset he has *with the audience Movemental serves* — and it is nearly invisible externally.
5. **No bridge to peers.** Builders who would naturally find him (mission-tech founders, AI-native CTOs in nonprofit/ministry, missional church planters thinking about platforms) currently have no surface to land on.

## Specific Gaps to Close (If He Chooses To)

### Tier 1 — Inside the Platform He Is Already Building
- A short, named **founder essay** on the about page (1,500–2,500 words) explaining why Movemental exists from his perspective. Most of the raw material already exists in `09-founder-narrative.md`.
- A **build log / behind-the-scenes** stream on the platform (not on a third-party site) — short notes on what is being built and why. Reuses the platform's own content stack.
- A small **personal page** (`/team/josh` or `/about/josh`) with bio, contact, and pinned writing. The about page already has the team card.

### Tier 2 — Selective Public Surfaces
- A LinkedIn presence (the SSoT is ready; just publish).
- A short, narrowly scoped public catalog of representative GPTs / agents (sanitized).
- A single anchor essay — for example, "I shipped a multi-tenant platform for movement leaders by myself in a year. Here's what I learned." Concrete, defensible, in-character.

### Tier 3 — Optional / Later
- Podcast guesting (not hosting) — easier credibility lift than launching one.
- A public version of the AI Integration Maturity Model, since it is already named as a planned artifact.
- A "scenius" surface — public roster of who he's collaborating with, since *that is the thesis.*

## What He Should NOT Do (Discipline / Scope Guards)

These follow directly from the values doc and his stated posture:

- **Do not pivot to thought-leader brand-building.** The Movemental thesis is scenius over genius. A loud personal platform contradicts the product.
- **Do not publish anything that competes with tenants for attention.** His tenants (Hirsch, Brisco) need to be the foreground.
- **Do not externalize private SSoT.** The personal/marriage/relationship folder under `docs/career/personal/` is explicitly private and must remain so.
- **Do not turn the build log into marketing theater.** The voice-preservation principle applies to him too.
- **Do not race to publish for visibility.** The credibility thesis is verification through network and embedded relationships, not search-engine surface area.

---

## Opportunities for Network Effect (Internal-Only)

Even with no public posture change, two internal moves would compound:
1. **Make him discoverable inside the Movemental network** — every tenant onboarding sees a one-page "who built this and why."
2. **Make him discoverable inside the founder cohort** — a small, structured intro page for prospective tenants, partners, and investors who already get a referral.

These are network-internal — they reinforce scenius rather than perform broadcast.

## Confidence on the Gap Reading

- **High confidence**: that public visibility is low and that this is mostly intentional / posture-driven, not accidental.
- **Medium confidence**: on which Tier-2 surfaces would actually pay off, since there is no audience-research data on prospective tenants asking "who is the builder?"
- **Low confidence**: on whether Josh wants any expansion of personal surface at all. This report assumes the question is open, not decided.
