## Executive Summary

Joshua "Josh" Shepherd is the **founder, CEO, and lead engineer of Movemental LLC**, a Kansas City-based mission-tech company building a multi-tenant credibility-amplification and content/LMS platform for ~100 curated movement leaders centered on the Alan Hirsch ecosystem. He owns 70% of the company; Alan Hirsch, Brad Brisco, and Rich Robinson hold 10% each as strategic-advisor founders.

He is **not** a public-facing thought leader, and this appears to be deliberate — the platform's stated thesis is *scenius over genius,* and Josh's self-positioning is "the system layer that holds Movemental together," not a marquee author brand. As a result, the standard "movement leader research" frame (embodied work → digital expression gap) inverts for him: he has produced enormous *digital* expression — a working multi-tenant platform, ~30 foundational business docs, hundreds of Custom GPTs, full tenant builds for Alan Hirsch and Brad Brisco — but very little of it is *attributed to him under his own name in public.*

**The actionable gap is therefore one of posture, not capability.** The recommended posture (detailed in `movemental-analysis.md`) is **named anchor, quiet voice**: surface him cleanly *on* the Movemental platform he is building, without launching an external personal-brand effort that would contradict the platform's own values.
