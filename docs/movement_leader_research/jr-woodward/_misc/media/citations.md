## Academic Context & Influence

### Theoretical Contributions

Woodward's academic work makes distinct contributions in three areas:

1. **APEST/Polycentric Leadership**: Creating a Missional Culture is recognized as "one of the most thorough developments" (Dr. Carlus Gupton) of the Ephesians 4:11 fivefold ministry framework for practical congregational application.

2. **Theology of the Powers & Leadership Failure**: The Scandal of Leadership applies Walter Wink's powers theology, Rene Girard's mimetic theory, and William Stringfellow's framework to analyze church leadership abuse -- a unique interdisciplinary synthesis.

3. **Movement Ecclesiology**: The Church as Movement contributes to the practical literature on missional-incarnational community formation, bridging theory and praxis.
