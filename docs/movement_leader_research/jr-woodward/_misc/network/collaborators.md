## Key Relationship Clusters

### The Missional-Incarnational Circle
Woodward sits at the intersection of several overlapping networks:
- **100Movements / Movement Leaders Collective**: Alan Hirsch's ecosystem. Woodward serves on MLC council and publishes through 100M.
- **Missio Alliance**: Co-founded by Woodward. Connects him to David Fitch, Deb Hirsch, MaryKate Morse, Leroy Barber, Jo Anne Lyon, and others.
- **V3 Movement**: Woodward's primary operational role. Connected to Dan White Jr. and Eun Strawser.
- **Forge Mission Training Network**: Alan and Deb Hirsch's training network. Woodward's "Creating a Missional Culture" is in the Forge Partnership Books series.
- **Fuller Theological Seminary**: Academic home connecting to Soong-Chan Rah, the Global Mission Advisory Council, and Church Initiatives Board.

### Mentorship / Influence Lines
- **Alan Hirsch** appears to be a key mentor/endorser figure (forewords for 2 books, published through his imprint).
- **David Fitch** is a close theological ally (foreword, shared board service, Northern Seminary base).
- **Dan White Jr.** is the primary collaborative partner (co-author, V3 coach, mutual endorsement).
