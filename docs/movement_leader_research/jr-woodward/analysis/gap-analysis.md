## Executive Summary

JR Woodward is a deeply credentialed practitioner-scholar (PhD Manchester, 3 authored books, 7 organizations founded/co-founded, 1,000+ church planters trained through V3, adjunct professor at 4 seminaries, 8 board roles) whose digital presence significantly under-represents his embodied expertise. His content is fragmented across 12+ organizational platforms he does not own, he has no personal YouTube channel, no audiobooks, no online course on any discoverable platform, no hosted podcast, and a personal website that underperforms his stature. The single biggest structural gap is **format poverty** -- despite a rich corpus of conference talks, seminary teaching, V3 training cohorts, and 30+ years of church planting wisdom, almost none of this has been repurposed into scalable digital formats. The second is **fragmentation** -- his influence flows through V3, Missio Alliance, 100 Movements, Forge, Fuller, Substack, and scattered podcast appearances, with no centralized hub connecting them. His Substack "Faithful Resistance" (launched 2025) represents an emerging owned-audience strategy, but it serves a narrower political-prophetic niche rather than his full intellectual range.

## Embodied Work vs. Digital Expression

### Strong embodied work NOT captured digitally

1. **30+ years of church planting stories** -- NLCF (Virginia Tech), Kairos LA (Hollywood, West LA, South LA), and V3-planted churches across North America, Africa, and Asia. No case studies, testimonials, video documentation, or published stories from any of these plants.
2. **V3 training curriculum** -- The Eight Movement Competencies, the Praxis Community experience, the Immersion courses represent years of refined pedagogy. This content is gated behind cohort enrollment and invisible to anyone not already in the V3 pipeline.
3. **Seminary teaching (4 institutions)** -- Years of lectures at Fuller, Central, Missio, and America Evangelical have no digital footprint. No syllabi, recordings, or course content published.
4. **Praxis Gathering (annual, 300+ attendees)** -- An annual conference with curated practitioners sharing street-level content. No recordings found online.
5. **Board-level strategic influence** -- Woodward serves on 8 boards/councils including Reliant Mission (500+ missionaries), Movement Leaders Collective, and Fuller Global Mission Advisory Council. This strategic influence is entirely invisible digitally.
6. **Solis Foundation and The Unembraced** -- Two Kenya-focused organizations with no discoverable digital presence. Represents a whole dimension of Woodward's work (global justice, extreme poverty) that is completely undocumented.
7. **East Hollywood Neighborhood Council service** -- Civic engagement that embodies his theology of neighborhood-rooted mission but is never referenced in his published work.

### Digital content NOT connected to embodied work

1. **Substack "Faithful Resistance"** -- Strong writing but disconnected from V3 training pipeline. Political-prophetic content does not reference or connect to his church planting methodology.
2. **Scattered podcast interviews** -- 11+ episodes without a consistent CTA, landing page, or connection to any owned platform.
3. **Facebook @missionalculture** -- Social media identity ("missional culture") does not connect to his later Powers/resistance work.
4. **Legacy blog (jrwoodward.net)** -- Domain expired; content from 2007-2016 may be partially or fully lost. Represents a decade of writing that should be archived and migrated.
