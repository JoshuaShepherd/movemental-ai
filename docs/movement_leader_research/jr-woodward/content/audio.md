## Summary

JR Woodward does **not host his own podcast**. He is exclusively a **guest** on other shows, with a concentrated burst of appearances in 2023-2025 tied to the release of *The Scandal of Leadership* (Spring 2023). ## Key Themes Across Appearances

### Primary Themes (appear in 5+ episodes)

1. **The Scandal of Leadership / Domineering leadership** — the central thesis of his 2023 book and PhD research; discussed in nearly every appearance from 2023 onward
2. **Polycentric leadership** — the alternative to hierarchical, CEO-model church leadership; drawn from Ephesians 4 five-fold framework
3. **Mimetic desire and scapegoating** (Rene Girard) — how rivalry and imitation drive leadership dysfunction
4. **Powers and principalities** (Walter Wink, William Stringfellow) — systemic spiritual analysis of institutional power

### Secondary Themes (appear in 2-4 episodes)

5. **Four spaces of belonging** — Woodward's framework for community structure
6. **Christlike servant leadership / self-emptying spirituality** (kenosis)
7. **Missional culture and discipleship** — creating communities oriented toward mission
8. **Kingdom vs. brand** — churches focused on God's kingdom rather than institutional growth

### Book-Specific Themes

- **Creating a Missional Culture** (2012): Five equippers, cultural architects, polycentric leadership, missional ethos
- **The Church as Movement** (2016): Movement ecclesiology, missional-incarnational communities, theology of place
- **The Scandal of Leadership** (2023): Powers of domination, mimetic theory, hierarchical vs. polycentric structures, leadership failures
