**Description**: Based on Woodward's PhD research, this book addresses the epidemic of leadership failures in churches by offering a comprehensive diagnosis of power abuses and the critical role of imitation. Drawing on Scripture and the scholarship of Walter Wink, Rene Girard, and William Stringfellow, as well as positive examples such as Oscar Romero, the book develops a robust theology of the Powers (Satan, the demonic, principalities and powers). The central thesis proposes an imitation-based framework: leaders either mirror Christ's self-emptying example or unconsciously replicate the fallen world's power structures.
