## Key Training Frameworks (Developed by Woodward)

These frameworks recur across V3 training, books, and teaching:

| Framework | Source | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Eight Core Competencies** | *The Church as Movement* | Movement Intelligence, Polycentric Leadership, Being Disciples, Making Disciples, Missional Theology, Ecclesial Architecture, Community Formation, Incarnational Practice |
| **Five Equippers** | *Creating a Missional Culture* | Dream Awakeners (apostles), Heart Revealers (prophets), Story Tellers (evangelists), Soul Healers (pastors), Light Givers (teachers) — from Ephesians 4:11-13 |
| **Four Spaces of Belonging** | V3 training / blog | Framework for discipleship through different relational spaces; applied to how Jesus made and multiplied disciples |
| **Theology of the Powers** | *The Scandal of Leadership* | Diagnostic framework for understanding domineering leadership via Wink, Girard, Stringfellow; imitation-based alternative |
