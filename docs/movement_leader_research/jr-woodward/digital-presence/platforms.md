### Score Interpretation

JR Woodward has a **broad institutional presence with an emerging content voice**. His influence is felt most strongly through:
- His **organizational role** (V3 Movement, National Director) which carries his training methodology to 1,000+ planters
- His **published books** which are well-rated (4.25 avg on Goodreads) and distributed across retail/library platforms
- His **academic credibility** (PhD Manchester, 4 adjunct roles, Academia.edu profile with 477 followers)
- His **Substack** which represents a distinct, more politically-engaged voice drawing from his doctoral research

### Key Gaps for Movemental to Address

1. **No centralized YouTube/video presence** — significant content scattered across Seminary Now, V3, and conference channels
2. **Modest personal social following** — 15.8K on X and 6.3K on Facebook; V3 org channels supplement but don't replace personal brand
3. **Website underperforms** — WordPress/Astra is functional but lacks the polish expected for an author of his stature; no newsletter CTA, no book showcase, no embedded media
4. **No Wikipedia page** — unlike Alan Hirsch; represents a discoverability gap
5. **No hosted podcast** — only guest appearances despite strong audio/teaching presence
6. **Instagram dormant** — handle @dreamawakener exists but minimal activity
7. **Domain fragmentation** — jrwoodward.com, .net, .me, and .blogspot create confusion; need consolidation
8. **No free lead magnet** — no downloadable resource to drive email list growth
9. **Newsletter list building** — "hundreds" of subscribers is early; no visible funnel from website or social to Substack
