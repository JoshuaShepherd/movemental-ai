## Event Network Summary

### Primary Event Ecosystems
1. **V3 Movement / Praxis Gathering**: Woodward's home base. Annual conference + ongoing immersion trainings.
2. **Missio Alliance**: Co-founded. Regular contributor to alliance gatherings and content platform.
3. **100Movements / Movement Leaders Collective**: Connected through publishing and council membership.
4. **Seminary Circuit**: Fuller, Central, Missio Seminary teaching roles.
5. **Podcast Circuit**: Multiple appearances promoting "The Scandal of Leadership" (2023-2024).

### Geographic Reach
- **Primary base**: Washington DC area (current) / Los Angeles (Kairos LA)
- **National**: North America-wide through V3 Movement
- **International**: Africa, Asia through V3 network; UK through University of Manchester connection
- **Founding work**: Blacksburg, VA (Virginia Tech) through Los Angeles, CA

### Event Frequency Assessment
- **High frequency**: V3 immersion trainings (multiple per year), Substack newsletter (regular)
- **Annual**: Praxis Gathering
- **Periodic**: Conference speaking (Awakenings, Exponential), podcast appearances
- **Ongoing**: Seminary teaching (3 institutions)
