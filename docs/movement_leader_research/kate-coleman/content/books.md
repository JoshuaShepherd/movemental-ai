# Books

### 7 Deadly Sins of Women in Leadership: Overcome Self-Defeating Behaviour in Work and Ministry

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Published | 2020 |
| Publisher | Zondervan (revised edition); originally self-published 2010 via Next Leadership Publishing |
| ISBN | 9780310119975 (Zondervan, 2020); 9780956557209 (original, 2010) |
| Foreword | Elaine Storkey |
| URL | https://www.amazon.com/Deadly-Sins-Women-Leadership-Self-Defeating/dp/0310119979 |

**Description**: Coleman's foundational work names seven self-defeating behaviours that hold women — and leaders generally — back: limiting self-perceptions, failure to draw the line (boundaries), inadequate personal vision, an unhealthy work-life rhythm, the "disease to please," colluding instead of confronting, and neglecting the inner circle and family. First self-published in 2010 and later revised and released by Zondervan in 2020 with a foreword by Elaine Storkey, it pairs careful research with narrative and practical mentoring toward transformation rather than shame.

### Metamorph: Transforming Your Life and Leadership — Inspired Wisdom from the Extraordinary, Ordinary People of the Bible

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Published | 2024 |
| Publisher | 100 Movements Publishing / Next Leadership Publishing |
| ISBN | 9781955142601 |
| URL | https://www.amazon.com/Metamorph-Transforming-Leadership-Inspired-Extraordinary/dp/1955142602 |

**Description**: Released in November 2024, *Metamorph* moves from diagnosis to transformation, reading leadership formation through biblical figures such as Moses, Esther, and the Woman at the Well — "extraordinary, ordinary people of the Bible." Coleman frames metamorphic leadership along three lines: godly self-discovery, healthy relationships, and Spirit-led mission. The book carries endorsements from Christine Caine (A21 / Propel Women), Alan Hirsch (Movement Leaders Collective), Danielle Strickland (Boundless Communications), Oneya Okuwobi, and Ben Lindsay (Power the Fight).
