# Organizations & Network

## Founded / leads

- **Next Leadership** (https://www.nextleadership.co.uk) — leadership development organization Coleman founded and directs, serving the public, private, voluntary, and church sectors.
- **The Regeneration Centre, Birmingham** — innovative local church plant she founded and led from 2000.

## Held office / pastored

- **Baptist Union of Great Britain** — first Black woman President (2006–2007), under the theme "Stay Focused."
- **Evangelical Alliance** — first Black woman Chair of the Evangelical Alliance Council (2012–2014).
- **Chalk Farm Baptist Church**, London — first pastorate; site of her ministerial accreditation.
