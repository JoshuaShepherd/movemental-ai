# Identity

## Roles

- **Founding Director, Next Leadership** — leadership development organization serving the public, private, voluntary, and church sectors.
- **First Black woman accredited as a Baptist minister in the United Kingdom.**
- **First Black woman President of the Baptist Union of Great Britain** (2006–2007), serving under the presidential theme "Stay Focused."
- **First Black woman Chair of the Evangelical Alliance Council** (2012–2014).
- Church planter — founded and led **The Regeneration Centre**, Birmingham (from 2000).
- Former pastor of **Chalk Farm Baptist Church**, London.

## Traditions

- Baptist (Baptist Union of Great Britain).
- British evangelical leadership.
- Women-in-leadership and biblical-equality stream (CBE International).

## Known for

- Naming the seven self-defeating behaviours that limit women leaders, through *7 Deadly Sins of Women in Leadership*.
- "Metamorphic leadership" — transformation read through the ordinary, extraordinary people of the Bible.
- Strategic advising, mentoring, and coaching of leaders across four sectors over thirty-five-plus years.
- A string of historic "firsts" as a Black woman in British Baptist and evangelical leadership.

## Credentials

- Certified Stakeholder Centered Coach.
- Former Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts (FRSA).
- Executive education, Saïd Business School, University of Oxford.
- Background in biochemistry and pharmacology, University of Southampton.

## Recognition

- Named among the 20 most influential Black Christian women leaders in the UK (2008).
- One of the top 25 Women Racial Justice Champions, Churches Together in Britain & Ireland (2021).
