## Identity resolution

This is the UK-based Christian leadership author and Baptist minister Rev Dr Kate Coleman — founding director of Next Leadership (nextleadership.co.uk), author of *7 Deadly Sins of Women in Leadership* and *Metamorph: Transforming Your Life and Leadership*, the first Black woman accredited as a Baptist minister in the UK and the first Black woman President of the Baptist Union of Great Britain (2006–2007). ## Bio synthesis

Born in Ghana before moving to England, Kate Coleman came to Christian faith while studying biochemistry and pharmacology at Southampton University. She was called into leadership soon after, first serving as a church elder in a congregation that initially opposed women leaders — an experience she has described as a "baptism of fire." Her first pastorate was at Chalk Farm Baptist Church in London, where she gained accreditation as the first Black woman accredited Baptist minister in the UK. In 2000 she moved to Birmingham and planted The Regeneration Centre, an innovative local church plant.

She became the first Black woman President of the Baptist Union of Great Britain (2006–2007), serving under the presidential theme "Stay Focused," and the first Black woman Chair of the Evangelical Alliance Council (2012–2014). She has preached at the opening services of both Labour and Conservative Party conferences, and has worked with the United Nations on leadership development curriculum for women leaders. She was named among the 20 most influential Black Christian women leaders in the UK (2008) and one of the top 25 Women Racial Justice Champions by Churches Together in Britain & Ireland (2021).

She has more than 35 years of leadership experience across the church, charity, voluntary, and business sectors. She is a Certified Stakeholder Centered Coach and a former Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts (FRSA), with executive education from Oxford's Saïd Business School.

## Current role

**Founding Director of Next Leadership** (nextleadership.co.uk) — a leadership development organization equipping leaders in the public, private, voluntary, and church sectors. She works as a strategic advisor, mentor, and coach to leaders and organizations at local, national, and international levels.

## Signature frameworks

- The **seven self-defeating behaviours** of *7 Deadly Sins*: limiting self-perceptions, failure to draw the line (boundaries), inadequate personal vision, an unhealthy work-life rhythm, the "disease to please," colluding instead of confronting, and neglecting the inner circle / family matters.
- **Metamorphic leadership** (from *Metamorph*): godly self-discovery, healthy relationships, and Spirit-led mission, read through biblical figures such as Moses, Esther, and the Woman at the Well.
- The **four questions** she uses in talks and coaching: "Why me? Why here? Why this? Why now?"
