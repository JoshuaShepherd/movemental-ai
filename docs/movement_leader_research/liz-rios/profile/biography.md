# Biography — Rev. Dr. Elizabeth "Liz" D. Rios

## Identity
- **Full name**: Elizabeth D. Rios
- **Preferred**: Liz Rios / Dr. Liz
- **Self-identifiers**: Afro-Boricua, Nuyorican Afro-Latina, mujerista theologian, "multi-hyphenate creative"
- **Birthplace**: Brooklyn, NY
- **Raised**: Lower East Side, Manhattan
- **Current base**: Central Florida + Puerto Rico (formerly Miramar / South Florida for 20 years)
- **Family**: Married to Rev. Hiram Rios (35+ years); sons Samuel and Daniel (who had special needs, transitioned 9/22/2022 at age 23)
- **Sorority**: Delta Sigma Theta, Inc. (39-year member; charter member, South Broward Alumnae Chapter)

## Call to ministry
At age 11 she discerned a call to leadership. Formation was shaped by Latino Pentecostal (Assemblies of God) roots in NYC, initially under Bishop Raymond Rivera / Latino Pastoral Action Center (LPAC), with early credentials through Primitive Christian Church (PCC), NYC.
