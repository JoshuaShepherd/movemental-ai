# Summary — Rev. Dr. Liz Rios

**One-page executive synthesis. Last updated 2026-04-20.**

## Who
Rev. Dr. Elizabeth "Liz" D. Rios. Afro-Boricua theologian, ordained Disciples of Christ (prior AG). 35+ years in ministry. BA, MA, EdD (Nova Southeastern), DMin (Southeastern), MA Social Justice (Union, 2025). Based Central Florida + Puerto Rico.

## What she runs
- **Passion2Plant** (founded 2020) — only national BIPOC-woman-led church planting network in the U.S.
- **The Passion Center** — her missional online faith community and training platform.
- **Púlpito Fellows** — 3-year preaching fellowship, Lilly Endowment funded, bilingual 10-week cohorts. Director.
- **Latinas of Legacy**, **Cafecito Conversations** — her own video series.

## Current affiliations
Board, Sojourners · Executive Team, Latino Leadership Circle · Adjunct, Fuller Seminary · Senior Consultant, Freedom Road (Lisa Sharon Harper) · Consulting Editor, *Outreach Magazine* · Commission on Ministry, Disciples of Christ · Senior Mentor, R.E.A.L. THRIVE · Co-vocational, Urban Strategies.
