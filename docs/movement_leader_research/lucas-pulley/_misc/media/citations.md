## Citation pattern

- **Quoted more often than cited.** Lucas is being *quoted in show notes and feature articles* more than he is being *academically cited.* This is consistent with his practitioner-intellectual positioning.
- **His frameworks are starting to travel.** "Ecclesial minimum," "servant equipper," and "priesthood of all believers activated" are phrases now appearing in adjacent material (Koinesúnē, Hubology Substack, Exponential materials) without always carrying a direct Lucas Pulley attribution. This is the pattern of a framework entering a tradition's vocabulary.

## Implication

He is at an inflection point where his language is becoming common-property inside the decentralized-church tribe. A publication moment (book, Movemental hub) would convert that diffusion into durable attribution and citation.
