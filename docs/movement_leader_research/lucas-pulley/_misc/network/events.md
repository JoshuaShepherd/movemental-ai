## Assessment

- **Annual flagship cadence is strong.** The Microchurch Conference is the anchor; Tampa Underground's internal calendar provides weekly/monthly teaching repetitions; Exponential gives national-stage presence.
- **Recording infrastructure is inconsistent.** Conference sessions exist as recorded artifacts but are not systematically surfaced on either the Tampa Underground YouTube channel, Exponential's channel, or Lucas's personal surfaces. Movemental's distribution layer could close that gap.
- **Reach is concentrated in the microchurch / decentralized-church tribe.** He is a central node in one ecosystem rather than a broad-evangelical speaker. This is both a strength (credibility inside the tribe) and a ceiling (he is not yet breaking out into adjacent audiences).
