## What Movemental would change for Lucas

### Problems Movemental solves

- **Platform fragmentation.** 12+ surfaces he doesn't own collapse into one owned corpus with proper cross-linking and SEO.
- **Owned-audience zero.** His 128 Medium followers, unknown-but-modest Twitter/Instagram/LinkedIn counts, and Airtable form convert into a real email list with discoverable archive.
- **No book.** The *10 Thoughts* manifesto + *Underground as Creedal Community* teaching + the 14 frameworks + the Hubology collaboration is a book-in-waiting. Movemental provides the publication path.
- **Content-format poverty.** Essays + podcast appearances + one Teachable session become courses, video series, cohort programs, and a structured learning path.
- **Lent-audience-to-owned-audience conversion.** His Theology in the Raw / Shifting Culture / Exponential / Missio Alliance reach is large; none of it currently accrues to Lucas. Movemental closes that loop.
- **Discoverability.** *The Parable of the White Pastor* is one of the most serious essays on multicultural-church power in the last decade and sits at a Missio Alliance URL that most of Lucas's audience has never seen. Movemental's discovery surface and framework-tagging architecture resurface it.
- **Title ambiguity and bio drift.** Movemental becomes the canonical bio source — a single bio that can propagate correctly to podcast hosts, conference programmers, and press.

### Content Movemental would surface

- **10 Medium essays** (plus *I'm HYPED* inline) + **1 Missio Alliance essay** + **14 named frameworks** — organized by theme, tagged, cross-linked.
- **8+ podcast appearances** indexed with topical tags and cross-references to the essay corpus.
- **Conference talks** (Exponential 2023 Orlando, Exponential 2024, Exponential Global 2025, Microchurch Conference 2023–2026) as embedded video / audio with descriptive transcripts.
- **Underground Essentials 2020 Session 4** ("Underground as Creedal Community") with permission from Underground Network — either as part of a Movemental course or as a free preview of the full Teachable course.
- **Network map** of Lucas's collaborators with links to their Movemental profiles (as they are onboarded).
- **Calling Lab** / **Microchurch Conference** distribution partnership for live-event capture and post-event hosting.

### New content Movemental would enable

- **The first book** — likely titled *Underground as Creedal Community*, or *10 Thoughts After 10 Years*, or *The Ecclesial Minimum*, or *Church as a Verb*. Any of these brand directions would work; the material is there.
- **The Ecclesial Minimum cohort course** — 6–8 sessions, cohort-based, practitioner-oriented.
- **Hubology Academy** — co-taught with Rob Wegner and Lance Ford. Three-voice course; cross-promotes three future Movemental profiles.
- **The Servant-Equipper newsletter** — biweekly, 500–900 words, seeded from his Medium archive and Hubology collaboration, with one curated link.
- **Audio essays** — narrated versions of his top 8 essays as a "Lucas Pulley Essays" mini-podcast / YouTube playlist.
- **Short-form video library** — 30–50 clips extracted from podcast appearances and conference talks; a core social-distribution engine.
- **"Movements Director Diaries"** — short-form reflections from Lucas's translocal work supporting sister Underground movements worldwide. This lane is vacant in the tradition.

## What We Will NOT Do

- **Replace Tampa Underground / Underground Network role**: Movemental is complementary, not substitutive. Lucas's work in Tampa stays where it is.
- **Remove content from existing platforms**: Missio Alliance, Koinesúnē, Exponential, Starfish Initiatives, Teachable — all stay. Movemental aggregates and links.
- **Hero-framing that contradicts his theology**: anti-celebrity ecclesiology is not a pose for Lucas; it is conviction. Platform framing must center the Tampa Underground team and the broader ecosystem.
- **Pressure him to produce faster than his pastoral rhythms allow**: he has three kids, leads a neighborhood microchurch, serves an international movements portfolio, and writes slowly on purpose.
- **Flatten him into a single-segment "microchurch author."** He is also a multicultural-church voice, a covocational leader, a systems-theorist, and a Fuller-trained missiologist.
- **Compete with Exponential / Starfish and the Church**: those relationships are assets; Movemental amplifies them rather than substitutes.
