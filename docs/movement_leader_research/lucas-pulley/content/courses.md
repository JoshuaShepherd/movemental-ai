## Recommended course strategy (for Movemental onboarding)

1. **Adapt *Underground as Creedal Community*** (his 53-min session) into a public-facing standalone course with updated video, discussion questions, and cohort-ready materials.
2. **Package *10 Thoughts After 10 Years*** as a 10-session cohort course — one session per thought, with guest interviews from Tampa Underground executive peers (Joel Repic, Tomy Wilkerson, Stacy Gaskins, Jeremy Stephens).
3. **The Servant-Equipper Academy** — a 6-session course on Hubology leadership, co-taught with Rob Wegner and Lance Ford (Starfish and the Church). Cross-pollinates three voices onto the Movemental platform.
4. **Calling Lab** — adapt Lucas's recurring "Calling Lab" one-day intensive into an asynchronous course or cohort for Movemental learners asking "Who am I?" and "What was I made to do?"
5. **Microchurch Starter Kit** — a low-cost / free introductory course drawing from the *What is a micro church?* Koinesúnē material, designed as a top-of-funnel asset that funnels interested practitioners toward the deeper cohort offerings.
