## What this inventory means

Three things.

1. **He has a real audience — but it is *lent* audience, not *owned* audience.** Every platform above reaches actual readers/listeners; none of them are his. If Medium shuts down tomorrow, his strongest 10 essays disappear. If Missio Alliance's author pages reorganize, *The Parable of the White Pastor* gets a new URL he doesn't control. If Tampa Underground's Teachable configuration changes, *Underground as Creedal Community* is re-shelved without notice.
2. **His strongest content asset per-platform is a single long-form interview or essay, not a corpus.** No single platform he uses has five or more Lucas-Pulley-authored artifacts. That fragmentation is exactly what Movemental solves.
3. **The network density around him is rich.** The list above reads as a who's-who of the microchurch / Hubology / missional-incarnational cluster (Preston Sprinkle, Joshua Johnson, Rob Wegner, Lance Ford, Jeff Reed, Brian Johnson, Tampa Underground, Exponential, Missio Alliance, Koinesúnē). Onboarding Lucas opens doors to the other voices on that list.
