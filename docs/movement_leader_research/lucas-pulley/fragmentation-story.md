## The Story

Lucas Pulley has been planting and directing microchurch networks for **14+ years**. He founded the **Carbondale Movement** (2009) while on **InterVarsity Christian Fellowship** campus staff at Southern Illinois University. He moved to Tampa to become **Tampa Movement Director** for Brian Sanders' **Underground Network**. When the Sanders family relocated to Ireland in January 2019, Lucas inherited the executive lane — publicly styled Executive Director by May 2020. In 2024–25 the Underground restructured its top team; **Joel Repic** is now Executive Director, and Lucas occupies the **Movements Director** seat on the Executive Team — the translocal / apostolic lane supporting Tampa's 100+ microchurches *and* dozens of sister movements worldwide. He still pastors a neighborhood house church in Tampa's subsidized housing projects with his wife Jami and their three kids. He holds a **Master of Global Leadership (Missions & Missiology)** from Fuller Theological Seminary and a **BA in Pure Mathematics** from SIU Carbondale, with a mathematical-research stint at **TU Braunschweig** in Germany before ministry.
