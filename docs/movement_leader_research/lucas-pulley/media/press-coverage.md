- *Christianity Today* or *Christianity Today Pastors*

- *Baptist Press* (consistent with his non-SBC affiliation)
- *The Gospel Coalition*
- *Religion News Service*
- Mainstream secular press (Tampa Bay Times, *New York Times*, *The Atlantic*, etc.) — despite the Tampa Underground being a substantive Tampa-area nonprofit with a 20-year history and national network.

This absence is striking given the institutional profile of Tampa Underground (100+ microchurches, 20+ years). It reflects both Lucas's low-profile posture and a systemic gap — microchurch networks tend to be covered more by movement-internal outlets (Missio Alliance, Koinesúnē, Exponential) than by general Christian or secular press.
