## Fit Score Rationale

The 7.1/10 reflects a voice whose content quality and thematic alignment are exceptional but whose current audience size and digital maturity are genuinely modest. This is not a deficiency in her work — it is a gap between what she has built in person and what has been captured digitally. The Movemental opportunity is precisely to close that gap. Compare to JR Woodward (7.7/10): Woodward scores higher on content volume (4 books vs. 2, 30+ training sessions vs. AMBS faculty), network effects (7 co-founded organizations vs. concentrated but smaller network), and digital maturity (active Substack, though still modest). Good scores comparably or higher on content quality (Buechner Award has no Woodward equivalent; Goodreads ratings are comparable), growth potential (career inflection is sharper and more recently triggered), and audience alignment (the deconstruction/reconstruction audience is a larger and faster-growing segment than the missional church planting audience). The 7.1/10 is a strong score — it places her among the upper tier of Movemental candidates while honestly accounting for the platform development work required.

---

## Career Inflection Timing — A Key Signal

The timing of a Movemental engagement is unusually favorable. Good resigned from Trinity Mennonite Church on May 2, 2025, explicitly stating she was pursuing broader network work. The Jesus Collective role she moved into immediately (Resource Development Manager) is itself a platform-building function — she is developing resources for a movement, not merely teaching within an institution. The "Reading Jesus" YouTube channel's 54+ episodes in 2026 alone indicate that she is actively investing in digital content production for the first time as a primary vocational focus rather than as an add-on to pastoral ministry.

This is the window. Authors who build their platform infrastructure during a career transition — before the audience expectations are fully set, while they are actively seeking new reach — develop loyal audiences. Authors who wait until they are established at their new scale often find their platform is already fragmented in hard-to-fix ways. The Movemental opportunity has unusually high return on effort at this specific moment in her career.

## What Movemental Would Change for This Author

### Problems Movemental Solves

- **Disconnected content ecosystem**: YouTube, newsletter, books, speaking calendar, Jesus Collective affiliation, and AMBS role all operate as separate islands. Movemental provides a unified hub that connects them, with consistent cross-referencing in every direction.
- **No course infrastructure**: Her two books (with their free study guide and group Zoom Q&A format) are already pointing toward course content. Movemental provides the platform for recording, hosting, and selling structured courses without requiring Good to build or maintain her own course platform.
- **No audience ownership across channels**: The newsletter list (site-native, subscriber count unknown) is the only owned audience channel beyond YouTube. Movemental provides the infrastructure for a consolidated, segmented audience database that grows from multiple input streams.
- **Invisible preaching credential**: The Buechner Award is mentioned in bios but has not generated a content brand, a course offering, or a preaching-focused audience. Movemental can surface this credential and create the course context that makes it discoverable and valuable.
- **Sermon archive silence**: 16+ years of award-winning narrative preaching at Trinity Mennonite is unarchived. Movemental cannot recover what was not recorded, but it can provide the platform for selective archiving and future production.
- **No structured companion materials**: "Reading Jesus" YouTube devotionals are produced but unaccompanied by study guides, reading plans, or connections to the books. Movemental can host and distribute companion guides that extend the YouTube series into fuller formation pathways.

- **2 trade books** — cataloged with summaries, key frameworks (Jesus Lens, Divine Gravity, Eight Theses, Cross-Shaped Power, Wounded Image of God, Dialogic Authority), endorsement quotes, Goodreads ratings, Herald Press purchase links, and cross-references to YouTube episodes, journal essays, and courses

## What We Will NOT Do

- **Replace Jesus Collective**: Movemental is complementary to her Jesus Collective Resource Development Manager role. Theology Circle work, Jesus Collective resource development, and network relationship-building continue unchanged. Movemental amplifies Good's personal voice and extends her reach independently of any organizational channel.
- **Change her Herald Press relationship**: Both books remain published by and sold through Herald Press/MennoMedia. Movemental aggregates and links; it does not pull content from publisher distribution.
- **Replace the denominational context**: Her Mennonite Church USA ordination, AMBS faculty role, and denominational relationships continue independently. Movemental does not position her as post-denominational; it presents her Anabaptist tradition as an asset, not an obstacle.
- **Alter her theological voice**: The Christocentric hermeneutic, cross-shaped power theology, centered-set ecclesiology, and "better Christian story" framing are preserved exactly as articulated in her books and teaching.
- **Force social media performance**: If Instagram, Twitter/X, and Facebook are not strategic priorities for her, Movemental does not pressure their development. The YouTube channel is the primary video platform; the newsletter is the primary audience-building tool.
- **Oversell the audience size**: Honest representation of her reach tier (micro-to-growing) is essential. Platform development for Good is a 3–5 year project, not a 6-month transformation.

## What Remains the Same

- **Her voice**: The narrative theologian-preacher register — accessible-literary, honest about doubt, moving from confession to theological resolution to invitation. The Buechner inheritance.
- **Her theology**: The Jesus-lens hermeneutic, dialogic biblical authority, divine gravity ecclesiology, cross-shaped power ethics, wounded image of God pastoral framework — the coherent system developed across two books and 16+ years of pastoral ministry.
- **Her relationships**: Greg Boyd, Dennis Edwards, Richard Mouw, Brian McLaren, David Gushee, AMBS colleagues, Jesus Collective Theology Circle — all continue independently.
- **Her books**: Both Herald Press titles continue to be published and sold through existing channels.
- **Her academic appointments**: AMBS Faculty of Record (Teaching Leadership, DMin program) and any Northern Seminary or other adjunct roles continue independently.
- **Her speaking calendar**: Conference speaking, retreat leadership, and university lecture engagements continue independently.
- **Her YouTube channel**: "Reading Jesus" continues as her primary video/audio content channel; Movemental provides complementary infrastructure and companion materials without displacing it.
