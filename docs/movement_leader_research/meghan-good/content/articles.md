## Summary

Meghan Larissa Good's article output is concentrated in three venues: (1) a **quarterly Bible column** in *Anabaptist World* (Nov 2020 – Aug 2022, 8 confirmed pieces); (2) a growing **personal blog/journal** at meghanlarissagood.com (active 2024–2026, 20+ posts cataloged); and (3) occasional pieces in **Missio Alliance** and **The Other Journal**. She does not maintain a Substack. Her writing is accessible and pastoral in register — brief essay-length reflections (800–1,500 words) that apply Jesus-centered biblical interpretation to everyday questions. Her blog posts are categorized under Theology, Christian Living, Spirituality, Gospel, Discernment, and Jesus-centered interpretation.

- **Total confirmed articles / columns**: 8 Anabaptist World columns + 1 Missio Alliance + 2 The Other Journal + 20+ personal blog posts
- **Date range**: December 2013 (The Other Journal) to April 2026 (personal blog)
- **Active newsletter**: Weekly devotional newsletter (subscribe at meghanlarissagood.com)

## Thematic Analysis

Good's writing across all venues clusters around:

1. **Jesus-centered interpretation** — how reading through the lens of Jesus transforms Scripture's meaning ("Jesus glasses")
2. **The Christian story** — what it means to recover an authentic, non-distorted gospel narrative
3. **Practical theology for doubt and questioning** — addressing the reader who is unsure, skeptical, or wounded
4. **Women in Scripture and ministry** — the Luke 1 piece and Barbara Brown Taylor interview signal sustained interest
5. **Preaching and homiletics** — craft of communication runs implicitly through the pastoral voice of her writing
