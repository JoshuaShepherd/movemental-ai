## Executive Summary

Meghan Larissa Good **hosts her own YouTube channel** ("Reading Jesus") — which distinguishes her from some peers — but her broader video presence is distributed across institutional channels (Mennonite Church USA, Jesus Collective, Our Daily Bread Ministries) and podcast video feeds (Shifting Culture, Greg Boyd / ReKnew). Her largest identifiable video presence comes from her role as a main conference speaker at MennoCon19 (2019) and MennoCon21 (2021) and as a featured guest on the Our Daily Bread "Discover the Word" series (2024). No dedicated personal YouTube channel distinct from the "Reading Jesus" channel was identified.

## Comparison to Similar Leaders

Good's video profile is **smaller but more personal** than many contemporaries. She owns a YouTube channel ("Reading Jesus") which gives her a direct relationship with an audience — unlike JR Woodward who has no dedicated YouTube presence. However, her institutional appearances (MennoCon, Our Daily Bread) are high-reach and suggest she is gaining visibility beyond the Anabaptist world.
