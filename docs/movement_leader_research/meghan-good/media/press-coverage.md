# Press Coverage & Media Mentions: Meghan Larissa Good

**Research Date**: 2026-05-27
**Subject**: Meghan Larissa Good — Mennonite pastor-theologian, author, Jesus Collective Resource Development Manager
**Confidence Level**: High (primary sources verified via web search and fetch)

---

## Summary

Meghan Larissa Good's media presence is concentrated in Anabaptist/Mennonite denominational media and the broader progressive-evangelical book ecosystem. Key coverage pillars: (1) Herald Press/MennoMedia press releases and author features; (2) *Anabaptist World* — her primary ongoing denominational platform as columnist and news subject; (3) Outreach Magazine's 2019 Resource of the Year award, her most prominent crossover media moment; (4) conference preview and event coverage from MC USA, Ohio Mennonite Conference, and the Church of the Brethren; (5) podcast appearances, which expanded significantly with *Divine Gravity*'s release in 2023. She has not received feature coverage in *Christianity Today*, *Christian Century* (beyond a passing mention in the Anabaptist Study Bible interview), or mainstream evangelical outlets.
