## Key Relationship Clusters

### The Boyd / Jesus Collective Circle
Good's most prominent cross-tradition collaboration is with **Greg Boyd**. They have written forewords for each other's books, Boyd endorsed *Divine Gravity*, Good chairs the Theology Circle at Boyd-adjacent Jesus Collective, and Boyd hosted her on his podcast. The relationship spans 2018 (*The Bible Unwrapped* foreword) through 2025 (*God Looks Like Jesus* foreword + podcast).

### The Anabaptist-Mennonite World
Good is embedded in the Mennonite Church USA ecosystem: AMBS faculty, MC USA ordained minister, *Anabaptist World* columnist, MennoCon preacher, and Herald Press author. Key colleagues within this world include AMBS faculty member **Safwat Marzouk** and Portland Seminary advisors **Dottie Escobedo-Frank** and **Jamie Noling-Auth**.

### The Broader Progressive-Evangelical Circle
Endorsements from **Richard Mouw** (Fuller), **Brian McLaren**, **David Gushee**, and **Dennis Edwards** (Northern Seminary / Missio Alliance) position Good in a cross-denominational progressive-evangelical conversation around Jesus-centered renewal. Edwards in particular bridges the Missio Alliance world and Good's work.

### Mentorship / Influence Lines
- **Dottie Escobedo-Frank** appears to be a key formative mentor (primary DMin advisor; endorsed debut book).
- **Greg Boyd** functions as the most prominent peer endorser and platform amplifier outside the Mennonite world.
- **Dennis R. Edwards** is the key co-collaborator on *Divine Gravity* (foreword) and shares a theologically adjacent orbit.
