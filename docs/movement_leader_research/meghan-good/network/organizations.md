## Note on AMBS Board Status

The May 2019 AMBS commencement record explicitly identifies Good as a "member of the AMBS Board of Directors." The current (May 2026) board roster does not include her name. This indicates she served on the board — most likely from approximately 2019 — but her term has since concluded. The claim in the research prompt that she has been on the board "since ~2019" is confirmed for the period around 2019; ongoing status should be treated as UNKNOWN — needs verification.
