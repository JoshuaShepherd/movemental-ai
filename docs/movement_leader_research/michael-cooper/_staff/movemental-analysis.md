## Why the fit is strong

- **The content asset base is exceptional and finished.** Movemental does not need to commission a corpus — it needs to *surface, consolidate, index, and cross-link* one. Lower-risk than any pre-book leader.
- **The owned-layer gap is the easiest gap to close.** Aggregation, indexing, a real newsletter, and a single canonical hub are exactly what the Movemental platform is.
- **Doctrine alignment is near-perfect.** "Missiology of movements," "movemental ecclesiology" (a named course on his LMS), movement DNA — his vocabulary is Movemental's.
- **The network compounds.** Single-hop to Alan Hirsch (lead endorser), Kurt Nelson / East-West, EMS / Missio Nexus, Motus Dei (Warrick Farah), Gailyn Van Rheenen, John W. Morehead, GACX — plus a possible Tampa Underground cross-tie via Bill Couchenour.

## What Movemental solves

Informational fragmentation (17 titles, 6+ imprints → one hub); owned-audience near-zero (~120–175 followers → a real list); lent-to-owned conversion (East-West / Kairos / Mission India reach → owned relationship); discoverability (no Wikipedia / Scholar profile, misspelled Patheos slug); and bio drift.

## What we will NOT do

Compete with his accredited LMS; strip his scholarly register; reroute his institutional audiences; adopt CPM/DMM jargon on his behalf; publish unverified entities (no "Ephesiology Coaching Network"; flag Frost's 2026-book role as UNKNOWN); or flatten him into a single segment.
