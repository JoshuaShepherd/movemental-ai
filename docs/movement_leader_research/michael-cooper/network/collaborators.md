## Collaborator-density observation

Cooper is **collaborator-rich but cluster-narrow**. He has a wide co-author roster (10+ named co-authors/editors across his self-imprint output), which signals a generous, convening temperament — he repeatedly gathers other evangelical academics into edited volumes. But almost all of those ties sit inside one cluster: conservative-evangelical / EFCA-adjacent / EMS / TEDS missiology. The roster builds depth within that world rather than reach beyond it.

## Collaborator-density observation — the bridge version

His two highest-value *external* edges are **Alan Hirsch** (confirmed endorser; the clean bridge into the broader Movemental ecosystem) and the **unconfirmed Couchenour/Frost pair**. If either the Couchenour-Tampa-Underground identity or the Frost co-authorship is verified, Cooper jumps from "adjacent academic the Movemental world quotes" to "one hop from the Underground/microchurch network and from Frost." Verifying those two facts is the single most leveraged research action on his network — and both are currently UNVERIFIED.
