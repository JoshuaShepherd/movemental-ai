## Structural observations

1. **The Roland Allen comparison recurs across the endorsement ring.** At least two endorsers (Newell, Stamoolis) independently liken Cooper to Roland Allen. This is the consensus framing his endorsers reach for — confirming that the *Missionary Methods* lineage is how the field reads him, even though Cooper's own bibliography citations remain unverified.

2. **Endorsement gravity is evangelical/EFCA/missiology-academic.** The *Ephesiology* ring is dense with EFCA (Burke, Doyal), TEDS (Netland), Missio Nexus (Newell), and his own org (Nelson). The *When Evangelicals Sneeze* ring shifts toward NRM/interfaith voices (Morehead, Wyman, Sheffey). Two different audiences, two different rings.

3. **The Hirsch lead blurb is the asset to leverage.** Of 24 named endorsers across both books, Alan Hirsch is the only one already inside the Movemental target set, and he wrote the *lead* blurb for the flagship. This is the cleanest, highest-confidence bridge in Cooper's entire credentialing trail.

4. **The endorsement trail flows inward, not outward.** Cooper is endorsed *by* many and is *quoted/reviewed* in respected journals, but has no surfaced record of endorsing *others*. He is a receiver and a convener, not yet a public blurb-giver — the inverse of his collaborator profile, where he is unusually generous.
