## Org-level observations

1. **Vertically-integrated own-seminary model.** Cooper's distinctive structure is that he owns the framework (Ephesiology), the content (227-episode podcast, books via his own imprints), the pedagogy (Master Classes / Seminary@YourChurch), AND a credentialing path (certificate credits laddering into accredited Kairos University degrees, plus the Mission India MA he directs). Very few movement-targeted leaders own the full stack from neologism to accredited degree. This is the single most important structural fact about him.

2. **Evangelical / EFCA-adjacent center of gravity.** His institutional orbit is conservative-evangelical, free-church missiology: TEDS, EMS, William Carey Publishing, Missio Nexus, East-West. Endorser density on the EFCA side is high (Rick Burke / ReachGlobal-EFCA, Kerry Doyal / EFCA district superintendent). He is not in mainline, Anglican, or SBC structures.

3. **Global South spread is real, not decorative.** Active teaching/degree nodes in India (Nagpur), Nepal, and South Korea (Seoul), plus a decade church-planting in Romania and equipping work across Africa, Europe, the Americas, South and Southeast Asia. The Mission India MA is the degree-bearing expression of the framework — the Global South is where Ephesiology is actually being institutionalized, not just the US.

4. **East-West is the financial substrate, not the brand.** The ~$33M East-West Ministries International budget and ~50-country footprint give Cooper a donor/missionary base far larger than his personal platform metrics (122 X followers, ~174 Instagram, 12 Apple podcast ratings) would suggest. His personal reach is niche; his institutional reach is large. Movemental should not mistake small social numbers for small influence.

5. **TELOS. is the legal/operational entity behind "Ephesiology."** The public-facing "Ephesiology" brand and the legal 501(c)(3) "TELOS." (TELOS.Go) are the same vehicle. All monetized surfaces (Master Classes storefront, certificates at $35–250/mo, Kairos degrees at $300–600/mo) run through it. Enrollment numbers UNKNOWN.

6. **Whether an "Ephesiology Coaching Network" exists is UNVERIFIED.** Only the Master Classes / Seminary@YourChurch program and the Mission India MA are confirmed institutional structures. Do not assert a coaching network as a named entity.
