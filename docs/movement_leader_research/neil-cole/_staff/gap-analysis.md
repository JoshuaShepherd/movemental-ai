## What Cole Has and What He Lacks

### What he has

- **One of the largest published organic-church corpora in North America** (17+ books, 5 publishers).
- **A working signature framework** (LTG) widely adopted globally.
- **A second working framework** (Greenhouse) with 20+ years of training cohort data behind it.
- **A third framework** (DNA — D/N/A) embedded in the missional vocabulary.
- **Mutual-endorsement and co-design relationships** with Hirsch, Logan, the Dales, Sanders, Wegner.
- **A clear, distinctive personal narrative arc** (1999 → 2014 → 13-year silence → re-emergence).
- **Academic credentials** (M.Div., DD, current Kairos Pacific faculty seat, DTS lectureship).
- **Audiobook production capacity** (he now narrates his own books).
- **Re-emergence momentum** (2024–2025 podcast cluster).

### What he lacks

1. **No canonical author hub.** There is no `neilcole.com`, `neilrcole.com`, or maintained Cole-anchored author site.
2. **No working primary domain.** `cmaresources.org` is lapsed/hijacked; `cole-slaw.blogspot.com` is dormant; `starlinginitiatives.com` is small and quiet.
3. **No public newsletter.** Zero subscriber-list ownership visible.
4. **No solo-hosted podcast.** Cole appears as guest-only.
5. **No public CRM / community platform** beyond a private Facebook group.
6. **Fragmented social presence.** Twitter @Neil_Cole exists; activity status post-silence UNKNOWN; Instagram likely nonexistent; Bluesky / Threads / Mastodon UNKNOWN.
7. **Fragmented YouTube.** Multiple channels and one playlist with unclear canonical relationships.
8. **Broken legacy citations.** Every CMA Resources URL in the 2005–2018 missional literature is now broken.
9. **No academic bibliography index.** No comprehensive citation count or dissertation-citation tracker.
10. **No translation inventory.** International reach exists but is unmapped.
11. **No comprehensive endorsement archaeology.** Cole has likely endorsed 30+ books over 25 years; only a handful are catalogued.
12. **No Greenhouse alumni network surface.** Tens of thousands of LTG / Greenhouse practitioners have no shared post-CMA home.
13. **No comprehensive event archive.** Conference talk inventory is fragmentary.
14. **No "where to find Cole now" hub.** A reader who finds *Organic Church* on a friend's bookshelf in 2026 has no easy on-ramp to discover that he is alive, teaching at Kairos, on Substack-equivalent surfaces, currently producing audiobooks, and recently re-emerging on podcasts.
