## Major Platform Articles (third-party, attributed)

### Christianity Today

**"Long Live the Organic Church: A Response"** — Neil Cole, January 12, 2010
- URL: [christianitytoday.com/ct/2010/januaryweb-only/12-21.0.html](https://www.christianitytoday.com/ct/2010/januaryweb-only/12-21.0.html)
- Context: Response to Mark Galli's "Long Live Organic Church!" SoulWork column.
- Key themes: Defines success as "finding out what God wants you to do and doing it" (attributing the phrase to **Bob Logan** — Cole's named mentor); quotes Ralph Winter on risk; argues that legacy is examples and cultural shifts, not permanent institutional structures.

### Frank Viola / Beyond Evangelical (joint interview)

**"Missional/Organic Church — An Interview with Neil Cole and Frank Viola"** (interviewer: Keith Giles) — September 7, 2010
- URL: [frankviola.org/2010/09/07/missional-organic-church-an-interview-with-neil-cole-frank-viola/](https://www.frankviola.org/2010/09/07/missional-organic-church-an-interview-with-neil-cole-frank-viola/)
- Significance: One of the only direct public conversations between Cole and Viola, surfacing both the agreement (Christocentric, organic, family-on-mission) and the disagreement (Cole = rapid multiplication; Viola = mature-disciples-first).
