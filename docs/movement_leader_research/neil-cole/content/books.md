## Master Bibliography (chronological)

### 1999

**Cultivating a Life for God: Multiplying Disciples Through Life Transformation Groups**
- Publisher: ChurchSmart Resources, Carol Stream, IL
- ISBN: 978-1889638065 (1889638064)
- Pages: 125
- Source: [archive.org/details/cultivatinglifef0000cole](https://archive.org/details/cultivatinglifef0000cole)
- Significance: His **first book**. Functions as the operating manual for the Life Transformation Group (LTG). Pre-dates *Organic Church* by 6 years. Re-issued by Starling Initiatives under ISBN 978-0984862184 (date unconfirmed).
- Goodreads: 164 ratings.

### 2005

**Beyond Church Planting: Pathways for Emerging Churches** (with Robert E. Logan, Holly Morgan, Julie Becker, Michelle Dodd; contributing authors Mike Perkinson and Tom Johnston)
- Publisher: ChurchSmart Resources
- ISBN: 978-1889638492
- Date: September 1, 2005
- Significance: First major co-authorship — Bob Logan was Cole's most-cited mentor (named in his 2010 *CT* response). Logan brought CoachNet/Fuller Seminary credibility; Cole brought organic-church practitioner content.

**Organic Church: Growing Faith Where Life Happens** (foreword by Leonard Sweet)
- Publisher: Jossey-Bass / Leadership Network Series
- ISBN: 978-0787981297 (078798129X)
- Date: 2005
- Significance: **The book that put "organic church" into the missional vocabulary.** Cole's signature work. Endorsed by Alan Hirsch as "one of the foremost thinkers and practitioners [of] organic forms of church." Foreword by Leonard Sweet places it firmly inside the Leadership Network / emergent-adjacent stream of the era.
- Goodreads: 996 ratings, 3.90 avg — his single best-known book by a wide margin.

### 2008

**Search & Rescue: Becoming a Disciple Who Makes a Difference**
- Publisher: Baker Books
- ISBN: 978-0801013096 (0801013097)
- Date: April 1, 2008
- Pages: 240
- Significance: The **lifeguard-metaphor disciple-making book**. Weaves Cole's pre-ministry LA County lifeguard career with the LTG framework. Re-titled in some editions as *Search and Rescue: An Urgent Call to Make and Multiply Disciples*.
- Goodreads: 71 ratings.

### 2009

**Organic Leadership: Leading Naturally Right Where You Are**
- Publisher: Baker Books (later in Shapevine series)
- ISBN: 978-0801013102 (0801013100); paperback ISBN 978-0801072383
- Date: February 1, 2009
- Pages: 314
- Significance: Cole's **anti-hierarchy leadership book**. Reggie McNeal contributed (Goodreads/Christianbook list him alongside Cole). Argues leadership emerges naturally from the body rather than being credentialed in.
- Goodreads: 164 ratings, 3.83 avg.

### 2010

**Church 3.0: Upgrades for the Future of the Church** (foreword by Francis Chan)
- Publisher: Jossey-Bass / Leadership Network Series, Vol. 49
- ISBN: 978-0470529454 (0470529458)
- Date: January 26, 2010
- Pages: 304
- Significance: The **next-step book to *Organic Church***. Tackles practical objections (finances, children, heresy, leader training, rituals/ordinances) that *Organic Church* had left aside. Highest-controversy of his trade catalog — drew the 9Marks critical review.
- Goodreads: 223 ratings, 4.00 avg.

**Colonel Surrey's Insanity** (fiction)
- Publisher: UNKNOWN
- Date: 2010
- Significance: Fiction outlier in his catalog. Goodreads: 2 ratings. Genre and status UNKNOWN — possibly self-published.

### 2011

**Journeys to Significance: Charting a Leadership Course from the Life of Paul**
- Publisher: Jossey-Bass / Leadership Network Series
- ISBN: 978-0470529447 (047052944X)
- Date: 2011
- Significance: Five-stage Pauline-leadership-development model. Foreshadows the later *Journeys* (2024) book by 13 years.
- Goodreads: 62 ratings.

**Ordinary Hero: Becoming a Disciple Who Makes a Difference**
- Publisher: Baker Books
- ISBN: 978-0801072772 (0801072778)
- Date: January 1, 2011
- Pages: 237
- Significance: Re-packaged disciple-making content with the lifeguard frame. Companion to *Search & Rescue*.
- Goodreads: 17 ratings.

### 2012

**Church Transfusion: Changing Your Church Organically — From the Inside Out** (with Phil Helfer)
- Publisher: Jossey-Bass / Leadership Network Series Book 63
- ISBN: 978-1118131282
- Date: 2012
- Significance: **Practical transition guide for conventional churches** that want to integrate organic-church patterns. Co-authored with Phil Helfer, Cole's CMA co-founder and pastor of Los Altos Grace Church. The "we're not anti-attractional, we're transitionally pro-organic" book.
- Goodreads: 37 ratings.

**Stability in Mind**
- Publisher: UNKNOWN — likely self-published
- Date: 2012
- Significance: Smaller / self-published title; details UNKNOWN.
- Goodreads: 2 ratings.

### 2014

**The Black Swan Effect: A Response to Gender Hierarchy in the Church** (contributor; ed. Felicity Dale; co-contributors Peggy Batcheller-Hijar, Jan Diss, Katie Driver, Dave Ferguson, Michael Frost, Alan Hirsch, Suzette Lambert, Floyd McClung, and others)
- Publisher: House2House / CreateSpace independent
- ISBN: 978-1497300187
- Date: 2014
- Significance: Cole's **chapter contribution alongside Hirsch, Frost, Ferguson, McClung, the Dales** to a coordinated egalitarian response to gender hierarchy. One of the densest single-volume snapshots of the simple/organic/missional voices of the era.

**Primal Fire: Reigniting the Church with the Five Gifts of Jesus** (with Dezi Baker [prophetic], Ed Waken [evangelistic], Phil Helfer [shepherding], Paul Kaak [teaching])
- Publisher: Tyndale Momentum
- ISBN: 978-1414385501
- Date: April 2014
- Significance: Cole's **APEST book**. Cole writes from the apostolic chair; the four co-thinkers each carry a different gift. Functionally Cole's parallel to Hirsch's *5Q* / *Permanent Revolution*.
- Goodreads: 112 ratings, **4.21 avg — his highest-rated major book.**
- 2025 reissue under Starling Initiatives ISBN 9798992152609.

### 2015

**One Thing: A Revolution to Change the World with Love**
- Publisher: Thomas Nelson
- ISBN: 978-0718032869 (0718032861)
- Date: November 5, 2015
- Pages: 224
- Significance: His **last major trade-house book before the silence period.** Pivot toward more interior, love-shaped material; foreshadows the post-silence Starling Initiatives books.
- Goodreads: 22 ratings.

### 2018

**Rising Tides: Finding a Future-Proof Faith in an Age of Exponential Change**
- Publisher: Starling Initiatives Publication Series (self-published)
- ISBN: 978-0990660484 (0990660486)
- Date: June 11, 2018
- Significance: **First Starling Initiatives book.** Explicitly a self-published imprint; marks the beginning of the post-trade-house era. Subject: navigating exponential cultural and technological change.
- Goodreads: 16 ratings.

### 2019 / 2022

**Pray: Finding Ways for Ordinary People to Connect with God in All of Life**
- Publisher: Starling Initiatives
- ISBN: 978-1792352003 (179235200X)
- Original publication 2019; audiobook 2022 (narrated by Neil Cole, 3 hrs 42 min)
- Significance: Interior / contemplative book on prayer; representative of the post-silence voice.
- Goodreads: 29 ratings.

### 2021

**VIRAL: Hearing God's Voice in a Global Pandemic… and Beyond**
- Publisher: Starling Initiatives
- ISBN: 978-0578257181
- Date: October 25, 2021
- Significance: COVID-era reflection on hearing God in disruption. Continues the silence-period theology.
- Goodreads: 2 ratings.

### 2024

**Journeys: Discovering God's Paths of Spiritual Formation Building to a Strong Finish**
- Publisher: Starling Initiatives
- ISBN: 9798218377076
- Date: 2024
- Audiobook: 2024, 6 hrs 54 min, narrated by Neil Cole
- Significance: **The first major post-silence book.** Discussed at length on *Shifting Culture* Ep. 178 (April 2024). Re-engages the Pauline-journey frame from *Journeys to Significance* (2011) but from inside the lessons-of-silence era.

### 2025

**Primal Fire** (Starling Initiatives reissue)
- Publisher: Starling Initiatives Publication Series
- ISBN: 9798992152609
- Date: 2025/2026
- Audiobook: 2026, 10 hrs 14 min, narrated by Neil Cole
- Significance: Re-issue of the 2014 Tyndale Momentum book under his own imprint, presumably after Tyndale rights reverted.
