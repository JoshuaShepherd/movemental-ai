## Direct Co-Authors

### Phil Helfer

- **Relationship**: Co-founder of Church Multiplication Associates (CMA); pastor of Los Altos Grace Church.
- **Co-authored book**: *Church Transfusion: Changing Your Church Organically — From the Inside Out* (Jossey-Bass, 2012).
- **Other collaborations**: *Foundations of Organic Leadership* CMA Resources audio set; *Primal Fire* (2014) — Helfer carried the "Shepherding" gift in the five-author construct.
- **Significance**: Cole's longest and deepest co-authorship relationship.

### Paul Kaak

- **Relationship**: Co-architect of the Organic Church Planters' Greenhouse curriculum.
- **Co-authored book**: *Organic Church Planters' Greenhouse* (training manual, with Cole).
- **Other collaborations**: *Primal Fire* (2014) — Kaak carried the "Teaching" gift.

### Robert (Bob) E. Logan

- **Relationship**: **Cole's named mentor**. Logan is the former church-planter, Fuller Seminary teaching faculty, and executive director of CoachNet International.
- **Co-authored book**: *Beyond Church Planting: Pathways for Emerging Churches* (ChurchSmart, 2005), with Holly Morgan, Julie Becker, and Michelle Dodd; contributing authors Mike Perkinson and Tom Johnston.
- **Other collaborations**: *Raising Leaders for the Harvest* (also co-authored).
- **Quoted in**: Cole's 2010 *Christianity Today* response: *"Success is finding out what God wants you to do and doing it."* — attributed to Logan.
- **Significance**: One of the most-named relationships in Cole's public commentary; the elder/coach voice in his career.

### Ed Waken

- **Relationship**: CMA-team trainer, based in Phoenix and Los Angeles.
- **Co-authored book**: *Primal Fire* (2014) — Waken carried the "Evangelistic" gift.
- **Other collaborations**: Greenhouse training co-trainer (Frisco 2011); ReTooling Leadership series author on legacy CMA Resources.

### Dezi Baker

- **Relationship**: Five-author *Primal Fire* (2014) co-thinker — carried the "Prophetic" gift.

### Ross Rohde

- **Relationship**: CMA-team partner.
- **Collaboration**: *Apostolic Leadership in Organic Church Movements* CMA Resources MP3 set with Cole.

### Holly Morgan, Julie Becker, Michelle Dodd

- **Relationship**: Co-authors on *Beyond Church Planting* (2005). All three brought ChurchSmart / coaching / women-leadership perspectives to the Logan + Cole project.

### Mike Perkinson

- **Relationship**: Contributing author on *Beyond Church Planting* (2005).

### Tom Johnston

- **Relationship**: Contributing author on *Beyond Church Planting* (2005).

### Reggie McNeal

- **Relationship**: Listed alongside Cole on the Shapevine paperback edition of *Organic Leadership* (Baker, 2010).
- **Significance**: McNeal's *The Present Future* and Cole's *Organic Leadership* sit in the same Baker / Shapevine series and are sometimes packaged together.
