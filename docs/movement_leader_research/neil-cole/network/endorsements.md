## Endorsements GIVEN (by Cole, on others' books)

### Alan Hirsch — *The Forgotten Ways* (original 2006 / updated 2016)

Cole has endorsed both editions. Verified language:

- *Original 2006*: *"a seminal work that will change our thinking, our vocabulary, and hopefully our way of being the church in this new century"* and *"I have already read the book twice and will probably devour it again."*
- *Updated 2016*: *"a road map for missional movements… been updated with even more insight and ten more years of learning."* (Cole identified as "Movement Catalyst and author of *Organic Church*, *Church 3.0*, and *Primal Fire*.")

Significance: One of the most-quoted Cole endorsements; appears in Hirsch's most-distributed book; serves as a mutual-endorsement anchor for the master network graph.

### Brian Sanders — *Underground Church* (Zondervan, 2019)

Cole is one of seven named endorsers on the Underground Network book site ([undergroundnetwork.org/undergroundchurchbook](https://www.undergroundnetwork.org/undergroundchurchbook)). The full endorser list:

- **Dave Ferguson**
- **Linda Bergquist**
- **Michael Frost**
- **Peyton Jones**
- **Neil Cole**
- **Hugh Halter**
- **Felicity Dale**

Significance: The Underground / Tampa Underground lineage that produced Brian Sanders → Lucas Pulley → Joel Repic → Tomy Wilkerson treats Cole as a foundational elder voice. This is the bridge edge into the Underground / KCU cluster.
