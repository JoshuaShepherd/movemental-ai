## Pattern of Engagement

The events catalog reveals the same arc as the books:

- **2005–2014**: Heavy public conference + training-cohort cadence. Mass-trained at Greenhouse cohorts (Frisco 2011, Virginia 2012, plus many uncatalogued); spoke regularly at Verge and Exponential and Simple Church Conference.
- **2015–2021**: Visible thinning. The 100 Movements launch (Nov 2015) is the last public-cadence high; afterward, public events are very few in the catalogued record.
- **2022 onward**: Re-emergence beginning at DTS (Nov 2022), then podcast cluster (2023 → 2025).
