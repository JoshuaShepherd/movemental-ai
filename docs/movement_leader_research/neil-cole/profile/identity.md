### Note on the silence period

In *Shifting Culture* Ep. 178 (April 26, 2024) and on KC Underground's *Upside Down Kingdom* (Aug 2025), Cole describes a 13-year season in which God told him to stop speaking publicly in the U.S., refrain from social media, and cease blogging. The Cole-Slaw blog goes dormant after June 7, 2017; his last big-house trade publishing release is *One Thing* (Thomas Nelson, Nov 2015). Self-published books continue to appear under Starling Initiatives (*Rising Tides* 2018, *Pray* 2019/2022, *VIRAL* 2021, *Journeys* 2024, *Primal Fire* re-issue 2025) but the public-platform/teaching/conference cadence drops sharply during this window. His own characterization on the KCU episode: *"I never felt like Jesus left me. Everyone else did — but He never did."*

The exact start date is not stated. Working hypothesis: silence began ~2011–2012 and concluded ~2024 (consistent with the *Shifting Culture* re-emergence and the KCU appearance), making 13 years.
