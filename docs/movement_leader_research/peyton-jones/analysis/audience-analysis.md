# Audience Analysis: Peyton Jones

**Status**: Complete
**Last updated**: 2026-06-03

---

## Overview

Peyton Jones serves **church planting leaders and practitioners** worldwide through NewBreed Training and his textbook *Church Plantology* (Zondervan, 2021). His audience spans three continents of on-the-ground planting experience.

---

## Primary Audience Segments

### Church Planters and Planting Teams

- Practitioners seeking systematic methodology ("art and science" of planting)
- Leaders catalyzed through NewBreed Training programs
- NAMB-adjacent Southern Baptist planting networks (historical affiliation)

### Training Cohort Participants

- NewBreed Training alumni — "thousands of leaders" trained globally (per organizational claims)
- Kingdom-expansion focused leaders

### Book Readers and Adjacent Learners

- *Church Plantology* adopters in seminaries and planting networks
- Readers seeking field-tested, actionable strategies

---

## Content Preferences

- Textbook/systematic format over narrative memoir
- Practical, proven methodologies
- Balance of creative (art) and reproducible (science) framing
- International and cross-cultural planting examples
