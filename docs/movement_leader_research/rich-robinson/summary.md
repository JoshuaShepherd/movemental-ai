## Executive Summary

Rich Robinson is a spiritual entrepreneur and movement leader based in Edinburgh, Scotland, with over 20 years of experience in missional disciple-making, innovation, and organizational change. He is the founder of Catalyse Change and 100Movements, director of the 5Q Collective, and team leader for the Movement Leaders Collective. He also serves as a team member of the World Evangelical Alliance (WEA) Disciple-Making Team. Robinson has authored "All Change: Unlocking Kingdom Potential in a World We Weren't Prepared For" and works globally to equip leaders and organizations for spiritual renewal and social transformation.

Robinson's digital presence includes multiple organizational platforms (100Movements.org, Catalyse Change, 5Q Collective, Movement Leaders Collective), a published book, and association with WEA. The content ecosystem shows strong thematic consistency around missional disciple-making, innovation, organizational change, and movement leadership, though opportunities exist for greater content interconnection, digital amplification of embodied work, and improved discoverability. Search visibility is significantly affected by the prominence of musician Rich Robinson (The Black Crowes) in search results.
