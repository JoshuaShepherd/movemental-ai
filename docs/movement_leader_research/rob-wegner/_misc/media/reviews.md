The Reagan Review and matichuk reviews both date to within a week of *Missional Moves'* September 2012 publication — typical book-launch review pattern. Both are favorable.

### Quotation pickup

Wegner's signature line from *Missional Moves* — **"It's not that the church has a mission. It's that a mission has the church"** — is widely picked up across Christian leadership writing, often with attribution to Wegner, sometimes with attribution to Hirsch (who wrote the foreword). The line has become **the single most-quoted Wegner sentence**.

## Strategic implication

The *Starfish and the Spirit* review profile is genuinely strong (4.25 Goodreads, 4.7 Audible, 5.0 Apple Podcasts companion) and includes one substantive academic review. The earlier *Missional Moves* has a single famously-quotable line that has propagated widely. The other six books have thin standalone review profiles, which is consistent with their positioning (free Exponential resources, photo-essay India fundraiser, personal-discovery calling tool, conference-theme companion).

For Movemental purposes:

- **The strongest review-driven case for Wegner is *Starfish and the Spirit*** — that's the book to lead with externally.
- **The most-quoted Wegner line is the *Missional Moves* "It's not that the church has a mission..." line** — the most reusable surface for any Movemental copy referencing Wegner.
