## Total program count

- **At least 8 distinct training surfaces** Wegner is named on (Find Your Place, Missionary Pathway, Microchurch Pre-Conference, BLESS Rhythms, Church Expressions NEXT, Microchurch NEXT Video Series, Future Travelers, Starfish Initiatives consulting), plus involvement with State of Microchurch in the West, Disciples Made, and the dormant EnterMission.

This is substantially more than the existing flat-file research recorded ("Find Your Place 4-session program; Exponential training resources; NewThing training resources; KC Underground training resources" — most of which were marked UNKNOWN at line-item level).

The pattern: **Wegner has many concurrent training surfaces, none of which is a single Wegner-personal course.** Like the books, the courses are co-led / co-branded. A consolidated Movemental "Rob Wegner Learning Path" — assembling sessions from each — would be the first time the corpus is gathered in one place.
