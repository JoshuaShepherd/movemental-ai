## Voice across platforms

Where visible, Wegner's voice is **consistent across surfaces**. The Instagram bio, LinkedIn title, KCU staff page, Goodreads bio, Audible Author bio, and `robwegner.org/about` all use variations of the same identity language: KC Underground director, NewThing leadership, Microchurch NEXT, *Starfish and the Spirit* co-author, husband/father. The collaborative-byline pattern is preserved across platforms.

The biggest inconsistency is **organizational title timing**: `robwegner.org/about` and several aggregator profiles still list "North American Director / NewThing" — but NewThing was integrated into Exponential effective April 1, 2025, which means several of his outward-facing profiles carry titles that no longer exist as standalone roles. Cleaning up the title string across all surfaces is one of the easier high-leverage moves.
