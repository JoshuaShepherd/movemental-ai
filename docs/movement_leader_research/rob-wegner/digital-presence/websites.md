## What this map shows

- **Wegner has no canonical personal hub.** `robwegner.org` is stale; `entermission.typepad.com` is dormant; the `starfishinitiatives.com` site is a four-leader consulting brand, not a Wegner-personal site; the joint Substack carries the *Starfish* brand, not a personal identity.
- **The body of work is distributed across at least 7 owned-or-rented surfaces** (KCU, Exponential, NewThing-into-Exponential, Outreach, Verge, Saturate, Substack, plus 2 retired blogs).
- **The strongest current personal surfaces are Instagram and the Substack** — Instagram is publicly indexed at 1,867 followers and 761 posts; the Substack is co-owned with Ford and not solo-Wegner.
- **The Granger and Westside archives represent the largest unverified pool** — 25 combined years of teaching that may or may not still be accessible on `granger.cc` or `westsidefamily.church`. Direct outreach to those orgs is the only way to confirm.
