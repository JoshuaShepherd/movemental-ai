## Strategic implication

Wegner's press coverage is **dominated by trade-Christian podcasts and church-leadership networks** (Stetzer, Bobby Harrington, Roys, Outreach, Leadership Network, multiplication networks like Multiply PDX and City Network Boise). He does not appear in mainstream Christian press in a sustained way; he does not appear in mainstream secular press at all.

This is a real gap. *The Starfish and the Spirit* is Zondervan-published, has a foreword by an NYT-bestselling author (Brafman), and has a clear public-interest hook (decentralized leadership, post-pandemic church). A targeted **CT or RNS pitch** is one of the more obvious next moves for Movemental to amplify Wegner's voice into broader-reach Christian press.
