## Pattern observations

- **Wegner's strongest endorser network is Hirsch, Ford, Halter, Ferguson** — the cluster he is structurally inside.
- **Hugh Halter and Dave Ferguson reciprocate** — Wegner endorsed the Halter/Ferguson *Righteous Brood* and the Ferguson *BLESS*; Halter and Ferguson endorsed *Starfish and the Spirit*. The endorsement loop is formal in the Wegner-Halter-Ferguson triangle.
- **The full back-cover praise rings of Wegner's books are not publicly searchable** without a print copy. This is a substantial research gap and is likely the second-richest source of network signal after his co-author roster.
- **The self/specialty publications (*Share the Well*) carry no surfaced endorsements** — consistent with their fundraising-first publishing intent.
- **The Exponential-published books (*Made for More*, *Find Your Place*, *Extraordinary Prayer*, *Deeply Rooted*) do not have endorsements visible on the Exponential product pages** — Exponential's marketing is platform-driven (the books are free at `exponential.org`) and de-emphasizes endorsement marketing.

## Implications for Movemental

1. **Direct outreach is required to fill in the endorser rings.** A print copy of *Starfish and the Spirit* is the single highest-leverage source.
2. **The endorsement loops surface natural Movemental network candidates.** Hugh Halter, Dave Ferguson, Brad Brisco, Bobby Harrington, Lance Ford, Alan Hirsch, Brian Sanders, Todd Wilson — every endorser ring will surface 8–12 names that overlap heavily with the master TAM list.
3. **Wegner is plausibly an endorser on more than the two confirmed books.** A direct ask to him would surface a much fuller picture of his given-endorsement history.
