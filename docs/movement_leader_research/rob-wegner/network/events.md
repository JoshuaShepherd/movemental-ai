## Pattern

- **Wegner's primary annual conference home is Exponential** (Global, Central, West, plus regional events).
- **The Microchurch Conference (KCU's own annual event)** is the primary KCU-branded gathering; it has scaled from KC to international with the 2026 Tampa event.
- **The Microchurch Pre-Conference** (Exponential pre-conference) is Wegner's signature recurring training-intensive surface.
- **United Brethren keynote 2025** is the most notable cross-denominational invitation surfaced.
- **The pre-2019 conference history is sparse.** Verge Network articles date to 2011; Wegner's *Missional Moves* book launched in 2012, but specific Granger-era conference appearances were not surfaced. This is a research gap.
