## Content Thematic Analysis

### Primary Themes

1. **Missional Discipleship — "Joining Jesus Outside the Walls"**
   - Anchored in *Life Out Loud: Joining Jesus Outside the Walls of the Church* (100M Publishing, 2019)
   - Core thesis: every Christ-follower is invited into adventurous, everyday-missionary living in the places they live, work, and play
   - Reinforced by his Pulpit Rock role as "Pastor of Missional Culture"

2. **The Future of the Church — *Red Skies* Conversations**
   - Curated/edited *Red Skies: 10 Essential Conversations Exploring Our Future as the Church* (100M Publishing, 2022)
   - Conversations span: leadership, race, sexuality, contextual intelligence, technology, creation care, church economics, migration
   - Multi-author chorus including Alan Hirsch, Michael Frost, Debra Hirsch, Brian Sanders, Gregory Coles, Mark DeYmaz, Rich Robinson, Oneya Okuwobi, Tyler Kleeberger, Lisa Rodriguez-Watson, Jay Y. Kim, Leonard Sweet, Michael Adam Beck, Anna Robinson
   - Companion site `https://redskiesfuture.com` + tied podcast series via Missio Alliance

3. **Mission-Training Methodology — Action-Reflection Discipleship**
   - Codified in his Missio Alliance essay *An Action-Reflection Approach to Discipleship unto Jesus*
   - Powers the Forge America curriculum: "learning by doing" before propositional knowledge
   - Operationalized through Forge cohort design and training intensives

4. **Micro-Church Multiplication — The Pando Collective Model**
   - Front-Range Kingdom network connecting, activating, and launching micro-expressions of the church
   - Sits inside Pulpit Rock; functions as a hub for house churches, non-profits, ministries, and Kingdom-focused businesses
   - Inspired by Tampa Underground; supports leaders doing Kingdom work outside (or alongside) traditional local-church structures

5. **Business-as-Mission**
   - Lived through Third Space Coffee (founded with his wife) — coffee shop as missional community intersection
   - Theme surfaces in *Life Out Loud* and his Pando talks on Kingdom-focused businesses

6. **Pastoral / Cultural Posture**
   - Mosaix talk: *A Needed Posture of the Church for Our Culture Today*
   - Emphasis on incarnational presence over programmatic ministry

### Theological Focus

- **Missional theology** (missio Dei) — primary register
- **Incarnational ecclesiology** — how the church embodies presence in a place
- **Practical theology** — pedagogy informed by Fuller's transformative-learning tradition (his dissertation pulls these together)
- **Multi-ethnic ecclesiology** — engaged via Mosaix and *Red Skies*' race conversation
- **Movement theology** — direct lineage from the Forge / Hirsch tradition

### Movement Focus

- Missional movements (Forge America national leadership)
- Micro-church networks (Pando regional leadership)
- Mission training cohorts (Forge America + Denver Seminary DMin in New Missional Movements)
- Multi-author conversations on the future of the church (*Red Skies*)

## Voice & Style Analysis

### Writing Style

- **Practitioner-academic**: Field-tested observations framed in missional theological language; not jargon-heavy but not casual
- **Story-led**: Bio language emphasizes "diverse voice in seeing God's kingdom at work in the church, in missional business models, and in incarnational neighborhood presence"
- **Conversational invitation**: Title and framing of *Life Out Loud* signal accessible, invitational, narrative voice
- **Curatorial generosity**: *Red Skies* shows him as orchestrator and host of conversation rather than sole authority

### Tone and Approach

- **Missional**: Constant — every channel ties back to "joining Jesus on his mission"
- **Embodied**: Frequent reference to specific places (Colorado Springs, Front Range), specific businesses (Third Space Coffee), specific cohorts
- **Movement-oriented**: Network thinking dominates; emphasis on multiplication, not maintenance
- **Pastoral**: Posture more than polemic; *A Needed Posture of the Church for Our Culture Today*

### Audience Levels

- **Practitioners** — church planters, missional pastors, marketplace ministers
- **Cohort participants** — Forge alumni, Pando network leaders
- **Academics / DMin students** — at Fuller, Denver, and GCU
- **Conference audiences** — Exponential, Mosaix, Awakenings
- **Multi-author book readers** — pastors and lay leaders engaging *Red Skies*
