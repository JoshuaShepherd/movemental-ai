## Gap Identification

### What Exists in Embodied Work But Not Digitally?

1. **Cohort curriculum** — Forge intensives, Denver DMin teaching weeks, Pando coaching content live in the room and on private LMS surfaces, not as public artifacts
2. **Network case studies** — Pando's micro-church multiplication on the Front Range is documented embodied but rarely captured as public stories on `pandocollective.com` or `rowlandsmith.net`
3. **Sermon teaching arc** — Pulpit Rock messages exist but are not curated by topic or interlinked with the books
4. **Conference talks** — Exponential / Mosaix / Awakenings sessions exist but are not aggregated on a personal "talks" page
5. **Action-reflection methodology** — Codified in one Missio Alliance essay and dissertation; not yet a public series, mini-course, or downloadable practitioner kit
6. **Business-as-mission story** — Third Space Coffee referenced in bios but not given its own case study / retrospective on his platforms

### What Digital Content Exists But Isn't Connected?

1. **Personal site ↔ everything else** — `rowlandsmith.net` does not aggregate his books, essays, podcast appearances, or speaker pages
2. **Two books ↔ one another** — *Life Out Loud* and *Red Skies* are sold as separate products with no canonical "where to start" guide on his own platform
3. **Forge America ↔ Pando ↔ Pulpit Rock** — Three orgs he leads, each with its own site; Pando lives inside Pulpit Rock, sponsors Forge methodology, and is led by him — yet the public can't see the lattice in one view
4. **Editorial pieces ↔ books** — Missio Alliance essays are not surfaced from book pages or his bio
5. **Conference pages ↔ his hub** — Speaker pages on Mosaix, Exponential, Awakenings do not link back to a unified "talks" page

### Opportunities for Digital Amplification

1. **Content repurposing**:
   - DMin teaching weeks → public essay series

3. **Content interconnection**:
   - Cross-link books, essays, podcast appearances, speaker pages, and cohort applications from a single hub

4. **Series / cadence development**:
   - Newsletter (monthly) tied to next conversation in the *Red Skies* arc

### Discoverability Gaps

1. **Personal hub authority** — `rowlandsmith.net` exists but does not rank as canonical; needs schema.org Person, books, courses, podcasts metadata; reciprocal links from Forge / Pando / Pulpit Rock; speaker-page backlinks
2. **Surname collision** — multiple unrelated public figures share variants of his name; only context anchors disambiguate. A stronger personal hub helps Google resolve the entity.
3. **Editorial cadence** — intermittent rather than weekly; reduces fresh-content signals
4. **Owned audience channel** — no newsletter / no email list opt-in surfaced
5. **Direct social channels** — LinkedIn / personal Instagram presence not URL-verified; could be amplifying his reach but currently invisible
