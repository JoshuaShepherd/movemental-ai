# Digital Reality Profile: Rowland Smith

**Research Date**: January 2025 (verified April 2026)
**Confidence Level**: High

## Executive Summary

Dr. Leo Rowland Smith ("L. Rowland Smith") is a Colorado Springs–based pastor, missional practitioner, author, and academic. He is the **National Director of Forge America Mission Training Network**, **Founder/Director of The Pando Collective** (a Front-Range micro-church network), and **Pastor of Missional Culture at The Church at Pulpit Rock**. He also serves as adjunct faculty at Fuller Theological Seminary (DGL Program), Denver Seminary (Leadership and Doctor of Ministry in New Missional Movements), and Grand Canyon University, holding an MA in Global Leadership and a DMiss from Fuller. His prior chapters include church-planting, worship pastoring, founding the missional B-a-M coffee shop **Third Space Coffee** with his wife, and a career in portfolio management / investment analysis.

He has authored *Life Out Loud: Joining Jesus Outside the Walls of the Church* (100 Movements Publishing, 2019) and curated/edited *Red Skies: 10 Essential Conversations Exploring Our Future as the Church* (100 Movements Publishing, 2022) — a multi-author conversation with Alan Hirsch, Michael Frost, Debra Hirsch, Brian Sanders, Gregory Coles, Mark DeYmaz, Rich Robinson, Oneya Okuwobi, Tyler Kleeberger, and others. His Fuller dissertation, *Missional Emergence*, has a Google Books authority record under the byline Leo Rowland Smith.

His digital presence is **distributed across many domains he influences but does not centralize**: a thinly-used personal site (rowlandsmith.net), an active X account (@Rowland_L_Smith), three seminary directory pages, two Wix-built network sites (forgeamerica.com, thepandocollective.com), the Pulpit Rock staff page, the *Red Skies* companion site (redskiesfuture.com), Missio Alliance editorial archive, and conference partner pages (Exponential, Mosaix, Awakenings Gathering). He guests on podcasts (Forge America Missional Podcast, Curious Friends, Red Skies/Missio Alliance, Koinesúnē) but does not yet host a personal show or run a personal newsletter.

6. **Discoverability** — surname collisions hurt; personal hub does not rank as canonical

## Movemental Opportunities

2. **Content interconnection** — link books → essays → podcast appearances → cohort content → sermons; build a real bibliography and reading-order

3. **Roles** — National Director, Founder/Director, Pastor of Missional Culture, Adjunct Faculty all continue
