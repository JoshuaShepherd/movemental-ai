## How to read the three columns

- **Churches**: Local congregation leadership, church planting / multiplication, pastoral team, megachurch teaching team, microchurch network as *church* expression, denominational ministry to congregations.
- **Nonprofits**: Mission agencies, justice / media NGOs, community-development orgs, national Christian networks with governance, grant-funded programs, consulting to nonprofits — *not* primarily a local church staff role.
- **Institutions**: Seminaries / universities, accreditation-facing graduate programs, denominational commissions, national training conferences as **field-shaping infrastructure** (distinct from “I spoke once at a conference” unless role is recurring and documented).

For each person: **Experience** (what they did), **Expertise** (domain depth), **Authoritativeness** (recognized roles, publishers, networks), **Trust** (long tenure, board / faculty / national scope where documented).

## Strategic notes for Movemental positioning

1. **Triple-layer voices (all three segments with strong research support)**: **Alan Hirsch**, **JR Woodward**, **Liz Rios** — use when copy must speak simultaneously to **pastoral**, **fiduciary / justice NGO**, and **seminary / accrediting** anxieties about AI.
2. **Church + seminary bridge (institutions column)**: **Rowland Smith** (DMin + three schools) and **JR Woodward** (PhD + multiple seminaries) are the cleanest **“president / provost / D.Min. director”** adjacencies.
3. **Church multiplication systems (denominations / Send)**: **Brad Brisco** is the strongest **NAMB / Send Network**-shaped credential on the site — different trust graph than Forge/V3.
4. **Megachurch → decentralized bridge**: **Rob Wegner** is the unique **large-church executive** + **microchurch network operator** proof point.
5. **Microchurch / network operator without seminary chair**: **Lucas Pulley** and **Tim Catchim** — excellent for **practitioner trust** with churches and some nonprofits; pair with an **institutions** voice when the buyer is accreditation-facing.
6. **Builder-founder with ministry + nonprofit ground**: **Joshua Shepherd** — strongest when buyers care about **who shipped the platform** and **who lived inside formation and nonprofit operations** before building AI infrastructure; not interchangeable with bibliographic or seminary EEAT.
