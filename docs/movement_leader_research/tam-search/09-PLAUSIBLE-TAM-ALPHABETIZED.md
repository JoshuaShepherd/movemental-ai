# Plausible TAM: Movement Leaders Alphabetized

A network-mapped field of leaders who plausibly belong in a Movemental / mDNA / missional / multiplication / formation-oriented ecosystem. Inclusion is based on verifiable connection markers: co-authorship, endorsement, conference participation, organizational membership, podcast appearance, and publisher curation. This list is intended for human editorial cultivation, not algorithmic scoring.

**Generated**: 2026-04-05
**Method**: Parallel research across co-authorship chains, book endorsements/forewords, conference speaker graphs, podcast guest networks, organizational rosters, publisher series catalogs, and international network mapping. Every connection cited was found through live web search.
**Scope**: ~150 individuals. Includes the full plausible field; editors should expect to narrow this to ~80-100 for the active scenius.
