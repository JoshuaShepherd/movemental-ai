## Strengths (why this is an onboard)

- **Exceptional productized funnel.** Free 7-day devotional → fortnightly email → self-paced Teachable courses → church licence ("50 Days") → leader cohorts → coaching. This is a real, working free-to-paid funnel that most leaders in the set lack — Movemental feeds it rather than builds it.
- **Credible academic standing.** A PhD (Alphacrucis 2020) and a Brill monograph ([GPCS vol. 47, 2023](https://brill.com/display/title/68992)), with peer-reviewed articles and endorsements from Vondey, Macchia, Archer, and Grey. She brings scholarly weight, not just popular reach.
- **A coherent, ownable framework.** "God Conversations," the Three Tests, phenomenological equivalency, and the ecclesial "church who hears God's voice" model give her teaching strong structural identity (see [content-analysis.md](content-analysis.md)).
- **Spirit-led-discipleship doctrine.** Genuinely complementary to Movemental's formation emphasis — discipleship that listens to and follows the Spirit, governed by Scripture and community.
- **An egalitarian female voice that diversifies the roster.** A credible, ordained female Pentecostal pastor and former church planter broadens a trusted-voices set that skews male and Western.

## Risks / constraints

- **Niche reach.** Owned audience is modest — low-to-mid-thousands of (overlapping) social followers, <100 distinct Goodreads ratings, no Wikipedia, an under-leveraged YouTube, an undisclosed (likely modest) email list. Reach is a **growth target**, not a present asset (see [gap-analysis.md](gap-analysis.md)).
- **Doctrine adjacency.** She is a hearing-God / Spirit-led-discipleship specialist, **not** a movement-ecclesiology theorist. This is the principal reason she sits below the anchor band, and framing must keep her as a **trusted voice** on her axis, not a movement-theory peer.
- **Must complement, not cannibalize, her courses.** The Teachable courses and the 50-Days church licence are her own revenue engine. Movemental must host public-facing corpus and audience growth without competing with her paid products.
- **Single-topic concentration.** Brand-coherent but breadth-limiting; her audience can only widen so far without an adjacent lane.
- **Anti-pattern respect.** Never present her as a hype-prophetic "words-of-knowledge" performer, nor as a Scripture-bypassing individualist — both are positions she actively dismantles.
