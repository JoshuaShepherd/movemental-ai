## Assessment

Harris has the **most complete and mature productized funnel** in the comparison set — and it is her single strongest onboarding asset. Unlike a leader with strong content but no commercial pathway, the issue here is **scale and integration, not absence**:

- **The funnel is genuinely complete** — free devotional → email → low-cost course → flagship course → church licence → live cohort → 1:1 coaching, on a real LMS, with international multi-currency pricing.
- **There is a real B2B/institutional tier** — the 50 Days church licence (with leadership training and a launch event) turns the individual message into a church-wide adoption pathway. This is the natural Movemental fit: organizations as the primary implementation audience.
- **International productization is proven** — a German-language edition plus multi-currency pricing reflect a genuine AU/NZ/UK/Europe footprint, not just aspiration.
- **Pricing transparency is partial** — the self-paced courses list clear prices, but the church licence, group training, and German edition prices are not public, creating friction for institutional buyers.
- **Funnel integration with the content layer is the gap** — the podcast (106 eps), YouTube, and books are not visibly wired as top-of-funnel feeders into the paid courses and church licence.

The story for courses is **a strong, complete funnel that needs reach and integration, not construction**: the commercial machinery exists; growing the audience into it and connecting the content surfaces to it is the opportunity.

## Recommended course strategy (for Movemental onboarding)

1. **Lead with the church-licence product** — the 50 Days licence is the cleanest organization-facing on-ramp; surface it as the primary institutional offer with transparent pricing.
2. **Wire the content layer into the funnel** — route the podcast, the "How to Hear God's Voice" YouTube videos, and the books deliberately toward the free devotional → email → course path so the existing content becomes top-of-funnel (see [audio.md](audio.md), [videos.md](videos.md), [books.md](books.md)).
3. **Surface pricing on the institutional tiers** — publish church-licence and group-training pricing (or a clear "request a quote" path) to reduce buyer friction.
4. **Promote the international / localized assets** — the German edition and multi-currency pricing are differentiators; feature them for the AU/NZ/UK/Europe audience the funnel already serves.
5. **Map the funnel as one navigable "Work with Tania" surface** — unify the free devotional, the two courses, the church licence, the group training, and the coaching into one pathway page rather than scattered Teachable and product URLs.
