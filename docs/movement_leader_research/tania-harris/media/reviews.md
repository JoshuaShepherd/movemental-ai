## Assessment

- **The reception is real, positive, and credentialed — but genuinely low-volume.** Healthy Goodreads averages (≈3.9–4.0) on tiny counts (under ~130 total), a 5.0★ podcast on 29 ratings, and five credentialed academic endorsements on the Brill monograph. That is a credible footprint with very little *mass*.
- **The load-bearing assets are the academic endorsements and the podcast rating.** Vondey/Grey/Macchia/Archer/Newton on the monograph, plus the 5.0★ podcast, are the strongest praise signals and should anchor any future jacket copy or Movemental hub.
- **The gaps are at the volume/retail tier, not the credibility tier.** No high-volume Amazon wall, no surfaced journal review, and Goodreads counts split across editions — the audience is small and not concentrated on any one review surface.
- **Recommended:** consolidate the Brill endorsements, the Pneuma Review feature, and the podcast rating into a single praise band; merge the split Goodreads editions where possible; and run a live re-check of Amazon and the Pentecostal-studies journals before treating those as confirmed-absent.
