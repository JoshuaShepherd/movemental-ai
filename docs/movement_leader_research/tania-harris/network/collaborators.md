## Collaborator-density observation

Harris is **collaborator-light by design**. Unlike convener-type leaders who accumulate co-authors and co-hosts, she works almost entirely solo: sole-authored across all four books, solo-hosted podcast, single-founder ministry. Her genuine collaborative ties are concentrated in one place — the **Alphacrucis PhD supervisory team (Newton, Cettolin, Cronshaw)** — plus one edited-volume editor (Rocha). This matches her voice profile (a single clear teacher's voice) and her productization (a tightly-owned funnel), but it also means she brings **few network bridges of her own** into the Movemental ecosystem. The richest latent edge is **Darren Cronshaw**, the missional academic on her supervisory team.
