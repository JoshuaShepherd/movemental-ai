# Frameworks

### The 3, 12, & 72 (Jesus' Relational Circles)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Introduced | 2025 (*"When Church Community Disappoints: Rethinking Connection Through the Lens of Jesus,"* missionalmayhem.com) |
| Components | The **3** — inner circle (Peter, James, John): sacred, vulnerable moments. The **12** — discipleship circle: those walked with daily, taught deeply, shaped for ministry. The **72** — broader community: trained and sent, part of the movement with less intimacy |
| Core claim | Jesus "modeled intentional, limited, and scalable relationships"; "every circle has its purpose, and confusing them sets us up for relational hurt" |
| Source | [When Church Community Disappoints](https://missionalmayhem.com/2025/06/06/when-church-community-disappoints-rethinking-connection-through-the-lens-of-jesus/) |

A model of discipleship and community drawn from Jesus' own concentric circles of relationship — the 3, the 12, and the 72 — arguing that intentional, limited, and scalable relationships protect against burnout and relational hurt.
