Tiffany

You have built one of the more quietly substantial practitioner records in missional leadership, and almost none of it carries your name on the spine. Two years as an International Mission Board missionary in Japan opened more than two decades of mobilizing leaders. You moved through the Southern Baptists of Texas Convention and the Send Institute. Today you serve as Send Network Strategies Coordinator at the North American Mission Board, and that role will matter to everything below.

Your own voice lives at missionalmayhem.com, a blog and coaching front carrying essays like *Resolve to know nothing...* and the relational framework you call the 1, 3, 12, & 72.
