# Auxano

## 1. Current state

- **Headquarters**: Operates as a brand within LifeWay Christian Resources (LifeWay HQ: Nashville, TN)
- **Founded**: February 2004 by Will Mancini [^1]
- **Leadership**: **Will Mancini sold Auxano to LifeWay and continued to lead it until 2020**, after which he departed; current Auxano leadership operates within LifeWay [^1][^2]
- **Employees**: Not publicly disclosed; small consulting team within LifeWay
- **Funding history**: Acquired by LifeWay (date undisclosed in sources reviewed; pre-2020 based on Mancini's tenure)
- **Current valuation / market cap**: Not separately disclosed (subsidiary of LifeWay)
- **Public/private status**: Subsidiary of LifeWay Christian Resources (Southern Baptist Convention publishing arm)

Auxano is a **church visioning and clarity consulting firm** founded by Will Mancini in 2004. Mancini sold it to LifeWay and led it until 2020, then departed. He founded Run Free in 2023 as a separate consulting shop. The current Auxano operates as a LifeWay-owned consulting brand. The original distinctive — Mancini's "vision clarity" methodology — left with him; the brand persists. [^1][^2][^3]

## 2. Category

**consultancy** — Auxano is a faith-aligned consulting firm focused on church visioning. **Not in Movemental's category**. Adjacent on the strategic/formation side, distant on the AI/technology side.

## 5. Structural advantages and disadvantages versus Movemental

**They have that Movemental does not:**
- LifeWay distribution into Southern Baptist Convention churches
- 20+ years of brand recognition in the church-consulting category
- Established methodology and book-driven funnel

**Movemental has that they do not:**
- AI-native everything — Auxano sells visioning, not technology
- Solutions tier and bespoke agent build
- Multi-tenant architecture
- Theological depth grounded in missiology (Hirsch) rather than vision clarity
- Path Bundle pricing reaching institutions

## 7. Recommended Movemental positioning response

**One-sentence differentiator**: *Auxano (LifeWay) sells church visioning consulting; Movemental does not compete with that — Movemental's four stages start where visioning ends and equip the church to operationalize the vision through AI infrastructure and staff formation.* Concede that Auxano-style visioning is real work and may be a prerequisite for some churches before Movemental's path makes sense.

In a sales conversation: position Auxano (or any visioning firm) as **upstream of Movemental** — the church that has done visioning work is more ready for Movemental's Safety Documentation and Skills Development stages because they have a clear "what" to build toward. Don't pitch against Auxano; pitch above it. If Will Mancini's name comes up specifically, note that he is no longer at Auxano (left 2020) and that his current shop (Run Free) is a separate organization.
