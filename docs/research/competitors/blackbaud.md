# Blackbaud

## 1. Current state

- **Headquarters**: Charleston, South Carolina, USA [^1][^2]
- **Founded**: 1981 [^2]
- **Leadership**: Public company; CEO Mike Gianoni led for many years; check current 10-K for exact 2026 leadership
- **Employees**: ~3,000+ globally (parent company)
- **Funding history**: Public on NASDAQ since 2004 (BLKB)
- **Current valuation / market cap**: Public; market cap historically in the $2.5B–$5B range (varies)
- **Public/private status**: Public (NASDAQ: BLKB)

Blackbaud is the longest-running and largest pure-play software company for the social-impact / nonprofit / education / faith sector. 2025 revenue annualized over $1.1B. The flagship products — Raiser's Edge NXT, Financial Edge NXT, Blackbaud CRM, eTapestry — span from small-nonprofit to major-institution tiers. Blackbaud has positioned its AI work under the **Intelligence for Good** brand, productizing AI inside Raiser's Edge NXT and Financial Edge NXT, with explicit posture: no training on customer data, customer-owned data. Blackbaud divested EVERFI in 2024, narrowing the company to the social-impact software core. [^1][^2][^3][^4]

## 2. Category

**nonprofit-crm** — Blackbaud is the institutional-tier nonprofit software incumbent. **Already in Movemental's category for the institutional segment** through productized AI inside the existing platforms.

## 3. Product offerings

| Offering | Description | Price | Customer segment | GTM motion |
|----------|-------------|-------|------------------|------------|
| Raiser's Edge NXT | Donor management with AI workflows | Custom — typically $5K–$25K+/yr [^5] | Mid-large nonprofits, schools, faith orgs | Field sales |
| Financial Edge NXT | Nonprofit accounting | Custom | Mid-large nonprofits | Field sales |
| Blackbaud CRM | Enterprise constituent CRM | Custom (high six figures) | Major institutions | Field sales |
| eTapestry | Entry-level CRM for small nonprofits | Tiered subscription | Small nonprofits | Inside sales |
| YourCause / Financial Edge / Education-specific | School/foundation/grant products | Custom | Sub-segments | Field sales |
| Intelligence for Good (AI) | Productized AI features inside flagship products | Bundled | All customers | Platform feature |

GTM motion: **Field sales + multi-year enterprise contracts + bbcon (annual user conference) + extensive partner ecosystem**. Blackbaud's sales cycles are 6–18 months for institutional deals. [^3][^4]

## 4. Overlap with Movemental's four stages

| Movemental stage | Competitor offering | Overlap level |
|------------------|---------------------|---------------|
| Safety Documentation ($2,500) | Public Intelligence for Good AI policy (no training, customer-owned data) — content, not paid artifact [^4] | adjacent |
| Sandbox Discovery ($15,000) | None — AI features are productized | none |
| Skills Development ($15k cohort + $5k/yr LMS) | bbU (Blackbaud University) is a paid learning platform; not a formation cohort but an LMS analog | analogous |
| Solutions Deployment (from $30,000) | Blackbaud Professional Services delivers implementation; partner ecosystem also delivers | analogous |

Blackbaud overlaps at **analogous** in two of four stages — Skills (bbU) and Solutions (Blackbaud Professional Services + partner ecosystem). This is the broadest stage-by-stage overlap of any single Tier 2 competitor. The differences are theological framing, missiological grounding, and faith-aligned ICP focus — not the existence of LMS or services. [^1][^4]

## 5. Structural advantages and disadvantages versus Movemental

**They have that Movemental does not:**
- $1.1B+ revenue and ~$3B+ market cap
- 40+ year operating history and largest installed base in the institutional nonprofit / faith / education segment
- bbU paid LMS and bbcon conference creating dedicated mindshare
- Professional Services capacity and large partner ecosystem
- Productized AI shipping in market under Intelligence for Good

**Movemental has that they do not:**
- Theological depth and missiological framing — Blackbaud serves all social-impact orgs generically
- AI-native architecture — Blackbaud's AI is layered into legacy products
- Single-vendor end-to-end delivery — at Blackbaud's scale, services are partner-delivered or via expensive Pro Services
- Movement-leader distribution
- Named refusals beyond a privacy/AI policy
- Pricing sized to mid-market church and small institutions ($2,500–$50K) — Blackbaud institutional deals start higher

## 7. Recommended Movemental positioning response

**One-sentence differentiator**: *Blackbaud is the institutional nonprofit software incumbent with productized AI and a paid LMS (bbU); Movemental is the theologically-grounded, missionally-framed formation partner that delivers a four-stage path Blackbaud's product-services split structurally cannot.* Concede that Blackbaud is the platform call for many large faith-aligned NGOs (Habitat affiliates, denominational service arms, large faith-based universities) and that Pro Services delivers competent implementation.

In a sales conversation: never contest the platform decision. If the prospect runs Blackbaud, route to **Safety Documentation + Skills Development as the entry points** — Movemental's policy artifact and staff-formation cohort are bbU-adjacent in *form* but missionally-framed in *content*. Solutions Deployment then integrates with Raiser's Edge NXT and Financial Edge NXT via API. Position Blackbaud as the institutional spine and Movemental as the missiological formation layer that spine has never had a partner for.
