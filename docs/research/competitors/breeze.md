# Breeze ChMS

## 1. Current state

- **Headquarters**: Grand Rapids, Michigan, USA [^1]
- **Founded**: 2013 by Jon VerLee [^1]
- **Leadership**: Operates as part of Tithe.ly portfolio post-acquisition; product brand maintained
- **Employees**: Estimated 20–50 standalone (basis: pre-acquisition product team size; now bundled into Tithe.ly's ~140 employees) [^2]
- **Funding history**: **Acquired by Tithe.ly on August 1, 2021** [^1]
- **Current valuation / market cap**: Not disclosed; rolled into Tithe.ly's reported financials
- **Public/private status**: Private; subsidiary of Tithe.ly

Breeze ChMS is a small-church-focused, transparent-pricing ChMS that has been part of Tithe.ly since August 2021. It serves more than 11,000 churches and is positioned as the simplest entry-level ChMS in the category at $50–$72/month flat. Functionally it overlaps with Tithe.ly's own ChMS product, which suggests Breeze is held as a brand for the existing customer base rather than actively differentiated. [^1][^3]

## 2. Category

**chms** — Breeze is a small-church ChMS, now a Tithe.ly subsidiary. **Not in Movemental's category today** and unlikely to enter — the brand is in steady-state inside Tithe.ly's portfolio, with no independent AI roadmap.
