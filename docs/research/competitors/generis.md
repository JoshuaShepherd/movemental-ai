# Generis

## 1. Current state

- **Headquarters**: Atlanta, Georgia, USA [^1]
- **Founded**: 1989 [^1]
- **Leadership**: Founder/principal team distributed; Generis describes its team as "practitioners with real-life experience working in churches, faith-based non-profit organizations, and Christian schools" — specific 2026 leadership names not consistently published in primary sources reviewed
- **Employees**: Estimated 30–60 strategists + admin (basis: typical mid-tier consulting firm size; not officially published)
- **Funding history**: Privately held; no reported outside funding
- **Current valuation / market cap**: Not disclosed
- **Public/private status**: Private; founder/operator-led

Generis is a 36-year-old church and faith-aligned-school **capital campaign and generosity consulting firm**. They have worked with 3,200+ churches and ministries since 1989. Their core service is multi-month capital campaign engagements, with a secondary line in ongoing generosity coaching and stewardship consulting. They are best fit for larger churches interested in generosity culture transformation beyond a single campaign event. [^1][^2][^3]

## 2. Category

**consultancy** — Faith-aligned consulting firm focused on generosity / capital campaigns. **Not in Movemental's category at all** — different problem space (giving culture and capital raises) versus AI infrastructure and formation.

## 7. Recommended Movemental positioning response

In a sales conversation: never compete with a Generis engagement. If a prospect mentions Generis, that signals an organization with budget and seriousness — both positive context for Movemental's path.
