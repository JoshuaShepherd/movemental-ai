# Slingshot Group

## 1. Current state

- **Headquarters**: Irvine, California, USA [^1][^2]
- **Founded**: 2007 [^1]
- **Leadership**: Stan Endicott (Co-founder); Monty Kelso (Co-founder); nationwide team of practitioners and consultants [^1]
- **Employees**: Estimated 30–60 across staff + practitioner network (basis: site descriptions; not officially published)
- **Funding history**: Privately held; no reported outside funding
- **Current valuation / market cap**: Not disclosed
- **Public/private status**: Private; founder/operator-led

Slingshot Group is a church and faith-aligned-nonprofit **staffing, search, and coaching firm** — not a software company. Their core revenue lines are executive search, specialized ministry-role search, and leadership coaching engagements. They serve churches and faith-based nonprofits across the US through a nationwide practitioner network. [^1][^3]

## 2. Category

**consultancy** — Slingshot is a faith-aligned consulting/staffing firm. **Not in Movemental's category** in any technological sense, but they sit *adjacent* on the formation/leadership-development side of the work.

## 3. Product offerings

| Offering | Description | Price | Customer segment | GTM motion |
|----------|-------------|-------|------------------|------------|
| Executive search | Senior leadership placement (lead pastors, EDs) | Standard search-firm percentage of first-year comp | Churches + faith nonprofits | Relationship + referral |
| Specialized ministry search | Worship, youth, ops, etc. | Per-engagement fee | Mid-large churches | Relationship |
| Leadership coaching | 1:1 + team coaching engagements | Custom | Pastors + executive teams | Relationship |
| Leadership transition / succession | Multi-month transition consulting | Custom | Churches in pastoral transition | Relationship |

GTM motion: **Pure relationship-based + denominational network referrals**. No SaaS, no AI products. [^1][^4]

## 5. Structural advantages and disadvantages versus Movemental

**They have that Movemental does not:**
- ~18-year operating history and trusted relationships across the evangelical church-staffing market
- Direct line into senior-leadership transitions (lead pastor searches in particular)
- Practitioner network with lived ministry experience

**Movemental has that they do not:**
- AI-native architecture and Solutions stage

## 7. Recommended Movemental positioning response

**One-sentence differentiator**: *Slingshot is a search and coaching firm for ministry leadership; Movemental is the AI-native formation and Solutions partner — these are complementary categories, not competitive ones.* The right move is **partnership, not displacement** — Slingshot places leaders into churches that would benefit from Movemental's four-stage path; Movemental's path equips the leaders Slingshot has placed.

In a sales conversation: do not pitch against Slingshot. If a prospect runs a Slingshot search, that is positive context — it signals an organization that takes leadership seriously. Movemental's pitch lands well *next to* Slingshot's work: Slingshot finds the leader, Movemental builds the staff capability and the agent infrastructure the leader will run on. Long-term, explore a partnership / referral relationship.
