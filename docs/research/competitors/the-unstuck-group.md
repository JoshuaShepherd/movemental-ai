# The Unstuck Group

## 1. Current state

- **Headquarters**: US-based (specific city not consistently published; consultants distributed) [^1]
- **Founded**: 2009 (founder Tony Morgan started 2008) [^2]
- **Leadership**: Tony Morgan (Founder & Lead Strategist); team of consultants and coaches [^2][^3]
- **Employees**: Estimated 15–30 staff + consultant network (basis: site descriptions; not officially published)
- **Funding history**: Privately held; no reported outside funding
- **Current valuation / market cap**: Not disclosed
- **Public/private status**: Private; founder-led

The Unstuck Group is a **church consulting and leadership-development firm** built around Tony Morgan's "Unstuck Process" methodology. They have served 800+ churches since 2009, anchored by a structured assessment-planning-coaching engagement model and supplemented by a Resource Hub of courses, leadership cohorts, and Morgan's book *The Unstuck Church* (HarperCollins, 2017). [^1][^2]

## 2. Category

**consultancy** — Faith-aligned consulting firm. **Not in Movemental's category technologically**, but more directly adjacent to Movemental's Skills Development stage than other Tier 3 firms because of the cohort and Resource Hub model.

## 3. Product offerings

| Offering | Description | Price | Customer segment | GTM motion |
|----------|-------------|-------|------------------|------------|
| The Unstuck Process | Multi-month assessment + strategic planning + ongoing coaching | Custom | Churches in plateau / health crisis | Relationship + book funnel |
| Leadership cohorts | Pastor cohort programs | Custom | Senior pastors | Relationship |
| Unstuck Resource Hub | Online courses and training materials | Subscription | Self-serve pastors | Content marketing |
| Books / podcast | Tony Morgan content | Retail / free | Pastors | Content marketing |

GTM motion: **Content-led (Morgan's brand + book + podcast) + relationship-based + cohort referrals**. [^1][^4]

| Movemental stage | Competitor offering | Overlap level |

The differences are content (church health methodology vs. AI capability building), not the *form* of the offering. [^1][^4]

## 5. Structural advantages and disadvantages versus Movemental

**They have that Movemental does not:**
- 16 years of operating history and Morgan's published-book brand
- 800+ church engagement track record
- Cohort and content-hub muscle already built
- Pastor-network credibility around church health

**Movemental has that they do not:**
- AI-native everything — Unstuck does no AI work
- Solutions tier and bespoke agent build
- Multi-tenant architecture and platform
- Theological depth in *missiology* (Hirsch) — Unstuck's content is church-health-focused, not missiologically-grounded in the same way
- Pricing path reaching institutions

## 7. Recommended Movemental positioning response

**One-sentence differentiator**: *The Unstuck Group teaches churches a specific church-health methodology through cohorts and Resource Hub content; Movemental builds AI capability and bespoke agents inside churches that already have a clarity of purpose — these can co-exist.* Concede that Morgan's methodology and content hub are credible and that for churches in plateau-and-decline mode, Unstuck's work may be the right next step.

In a sales conversation: position Unstuck as **a different shape of work, not a competing one**. Movemental's Skills Development cohort focuses on AI capability across staff functions; Unstuck's cohorts focus on church health and strategic planning. A church can do both. If a prospect says they're "doing the Unstuck Process," route them to Movemental's Safety Documentation and Solutions Deployment as the AI-native complement. Long-term, evaluate partnership.
