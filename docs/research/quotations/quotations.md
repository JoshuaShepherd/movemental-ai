# The Movemental Quote Library

> *A curated, verified collection of quotations for Movemental — for marketing pulls, hero rotators, section epigraphs, opening slides, social assets, book paratext, and "the quote of the day" randomizer.*

**No hallucinations.** Every quote here is verifiable — either extracted verbatim from Alan Hirsch's published books (via the local Hirsch corpus quote banks) or cross-checked through authoritative sources. Each entry carries its citation. Where attribution is commonly confused or disputed, a note appears inline.

**How this file is organized.**
Quotes are grouped by ten themes that map to Movemental's philosophy — movement multiplication, incarnational presence, platform ownership, technology as servant, risk/adventure, discipleship, network/communitas, imagination, simplicity, and kingdom. Many quotes sit comfortably in more than one theme; the themes field on each entry makes cross-indexing possible.

**Paired file:** [`external-voices-raw.md`](./external-voices-raw.md) holds the verified non-Hirsch harvest; [`quotes.json`](./quotes.json) is the machine-readable version for hero rotators and random pulls.

## 4. Technology as Servant of Mission

*Movemental uses technology — AI, analytics, SEO, owned publishing infrastructure — as a **means**, not an end. The quotes below frame technology as the carrier of the message, which itself shapes what the message can become. Expanded external voices (McLuhan, Postman, Illich, Ellul, Kelly, Lanier) live in [`external-voices-raw.md`](./external-voices-raw.md).*

> "If you can shape the various conversations that go on in the community, you can shape the culture. As we've said before, language shapes our consciousness."
> — **Alan Hirsch**, *On the Verge* — ch. 5 "Verge Vibe"
> *Themes: technology, language, culture*

> "Unless the paradigm at the heart of the culture is changed, there can be no lasting change. Change must come from deep inside the paradigm; anything less will simply be external and cosmetic."

## 11. Usage Notes

### Randomizer / rotator use

- The companion [`quotes.json`](./quotes.json) is the machine-readable index for a home-page hero rotator, a "quote of the day" call-out, a Stitch-level section epigraph, or a newsletter footer.
- Every entry has `id`, `text`, `author`, `source`, `themes[]`, and `length` (short/medium/long). Filter by `length: "short"` for hero rotations; by theme for pathway pages (e.g. pathway-for-nonprofits might pull `themes: ["kingdom", "incarnational", "simplicity"]`).
- Keep attribution visible. These quotes lose half their value (and all their credibility) when they float free of the author.

### Marketing pulls

- **Hero / manifesto page:** draw from *Movement & Multiplication*, *Kingdom & the World*, *Imagination*.
- **Platform / pricing page:** draw from *Platform, Ownership & Stewardship*, *Technology as Servant*.
- **Case studies / collaborator stories:** draw from *Network, Collaboration & Communitas*, *Incarnational*.
- **About / philosophy:** draw from *Risk, Adventure & Liminality*, *Discipleship*, *Simplicity*.

### Editorial rules

1. **Verbatim only.** Quotes are reproduced exactly as published. Do not paraphrase in production copy and pass it off as a quote.
2. **Always cite.** Book, chapter, and (where known) section. For non-Hirsch external quotes see [`external-voices-raw.md`](./external-voices-raw.md) for the verification trail.
3. **Pair wisely.** A scroll-stop quote in the hero should be paired with a primitive surface (blockquote with display-serif-pretender typography per DESIGN.md) — not run-on body copy.
4. **Length tiers.** Ultra-short (<20 words) for heroes/OG images; medium (20–60) for section eyebrows and pull quotes; long (60+) for chapter openers and essays.
5. **No mixing.** Don't stitch two separate Hirsch sentences into one "quote" — even from the same chapter. Each blockquote is a single continuous source passage.

### What lives where

- [`quotations.md`](./quotations.md) — this file. The curated thematic collection, Hirsch-primary, prose-readable.
- [`external-voices-raw.md`](./external-voices-raw.md) — verified non-Hirsch voices (movement authors, media/tech critics, literature). Populated by the research harvest.
- [`quotes.json`](./quotes.json) — machine-readable for rotators and randomizers.
