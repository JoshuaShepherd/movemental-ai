# Master Map

## Core Themes

1. **Agents and assistant systems**
   - Building and debugging Assistants API flows.
   - Practical implementation concerns (response format, Flask integration, deployment friction).
   - File: `01-themes/agents-and-assistants.md`

2. **Prompt engineering and custom GPTs**
   - Prompt design as a practical professional skill.
   - Custom GPT positioning, categorization, and SEO-aware descriptions.
   - File: `01-themes/prompt-engineering-and-custom-gpts.md`

3. **AI art and co-creation**
   - Questions of authorship and aesthetic criteria in AI-assisted visual art.
   - Prompt-based creative collaboration workflows.
   - File: `01-themes/ai-art-and-creative-collaboration.md`

4. **AI, faith, and social impact**
   - Ethical and theological concerns about who benefits from AI.
   - Missional/discipleship implications of AI use.
   - File: `01-themes/ai-and-faith-social-impact.md`

5. **AI as worldview and narrative**
   - Fascination with long-arc AI stories (Asimov, civilization loops, entropy).
   - AI framing beyond tools: meaning, destiny, and cyclical history.
   - Files: `01-themes/ai-and-faith-social-impact.md`, `02-book-material/signature-stories.md`
