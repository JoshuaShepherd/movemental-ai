The Architectural Elegance of Cellular Structures

Published Oct 24, 2023 • Dr. Aris Thorne

The fundamental architecture of life is built upon principles that engineers have only recently begun to fully appreciate. When examining the structural integrity of cellular membranes, one cannot help but notice the striking parallels to modern geodesic design.

"To understand the macro, we must first decipher the micro structural intent of nature."
