01 — Calling

You're called to missional ecclesiology and multiplication.

Not to run a single church or hold one post — but to help the church function as sent missionary people and to equip leaders for church planting and co-vocational ministry.

You think in terms of movement: reproduction, networks, and the long run. You're not selling a brand; you're forming practitioners.

Success for you is movement that outlasts you — co-vocational planters, missional congregations, and frameworks that are both theologically serious and practically usable.

02 — Audience

The people who respond to your work are movement-oriented.

They care about church planting, multiplication strategies, missional living, and the integration of marketplace and ministry. They don't just want better programs; they want the church to function as sent and reproducing.
