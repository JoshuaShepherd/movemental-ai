Theme 03

Missional Living

Empowering everyday people to live on mission through simple, repeatable rhythms

01

Mission isn't a program or an event—it's a lifestyle. Dave Ferguson's B.L.E.S.S. framework makes missional living accessible to everyone, not just the spiritual superheroes. B.L.E.S.S. stands for Begin with prayer, Listen, Eat, Serve, and Story. These five practices turn everyday rhythms into mission moments and ordinary people into missionaries.

The genius of B.L.E.S.S. is its simplicity and reproducibility. You don't need a theology degree or a missions budget. You just need to pay attention to the people already in your life—neighbors, coworkers, baristas, coaches. When you begin praying for them, listening to their stories, eating with them, serving them in practical ways, and sharing God's story at the right moments, you become a conduit for kingdom work right where you live.

Dave has seen this framework transform churches from weekend services into 24/7 mission movements. When every member embraces B.L.E.S.S., the church's impact multiplies exponentially. People who never thought of themselves as missionaries start seeing God work through their everyday interactions. The mission field isn't somewhere far away—it's right outside your door.
