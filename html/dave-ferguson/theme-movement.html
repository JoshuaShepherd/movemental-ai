Theme 02

Church Planting & Movement

Catalyzing exponential movements that multiply churches, leaders, and kingdom impact

01

Dave Ferguson has spent his entire ministry life starting and multiplying churches. As the founding and lead pastor of Community Christian Church and co-founder of NewThing Network, he's been part of launching hundreds of churches across multiple countries. But his passion isn't just about planting more churches—it's about catalyzing movements that reproduce at every level.

Through his work with the Exponential Conference and NewThing, Dave has championed a shift from addition to multiplication. He's helped thousands of church leaders move beyond the incremental growth mindset to embrace exponential thinking. This means measuring success not just by how many people attend your church, but by how many churches you start, how many leaders you multiply, and how many communities you reach.

The movement DNA Dave advocates for is built on five key practices: dreaming, committing, inviting, eating, and blessing. These aren't complex strategies that require massive budgets or specialized training. They're simple, reproducible behaviors that any church can embed into its culture. When these practices become rhythms, movements happen naturally.
