Theme 05

Church Strategy

Keeping score on what matters most and aligning your church around a clear mission

01

What you measure matters. Dave Ferguson has spent decades helping churches clarify their mission and keep score on the metrics that actually move them toward it. Too many churches measure the wrong things—counting seats and dollars while missing the deeper work of life transformation and kingdom multiplication. "Keeping Score" challenges leaders to define what winning looks like and then measure progress ruthlessly.

At the heart of Dave's approach to church strategy is mission clarity. Every church needs to discover and articulate its unique mission—the specific way God has called it to participate in the Great Commission. Once you know your mission, every decision becomes simpler. Does this program advance our mission? Does this hire align with our mission? Does this budget reflect our mission? Mission clarity creates organizational health and momentum.

But clarity without measurement is just wishful thinking. Dave teaches churches to identify the vital signs that indicate spiritual health and mission progress. These aren't vanity metrics like weekend attendance—they're leading indicators like baptisms, small group participation, percentage of people serving, leaders being developed, and churches being planted. When you keep score on what matters, you create a culture of continuous improvement and accountability.
