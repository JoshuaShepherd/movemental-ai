Theme 04

Spiritual Journey

Understanding the five thresholds spiritual seekers cross on their way back to God

01

Most people don't come to faith in a single moment—they cross a series of thresholds on their journey back to God. Dave Ferguson's groundbreaking research in "Finding Your Way Back to God" identifies five key awakenings that spiritual seekers experience: a curiosity about spiritual things, an openness to change, a readiness to seek God, a decision to follow Jesus, and a commitment to live as an apprentice of Christ.

Understanding these five thresholds changes everything about how we do evangelism and discipleship. Instead of treating everyone the same, we learn to meet people where they are and help them take the next step. Someone who is curious needs different help than someone who is ready to decide. When we understand the journey, we can guide people more effectively and avoid pushing them too fast or holding them back.

This framework has transformed how thousands of churches approach outreach and spiritual formation. It's not about getting people to "pray the prayer"—it's about walking with them through a process of transformation. And it recognizes that everyone is on a journey, including those of us who have been following Jesus for years. We're all finding our way back to God, threshold by threshold.
