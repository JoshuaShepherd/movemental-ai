Theme 01

Leadership Multiplication

From hero to hero maker: the five practices that multiply leaders at every level

01

The greatest leaders don't make the most followers. They make the most leaders. This counterintuitive idea sits at the heart of Dave Ferguson's work on leadership multiplication. After decades of leading and catalyzing movements, Dave has distilled the essential practices that separate leaders who hoard influence from those who multiply it.

The Hero Maker framework identifies five core practices: multiplying leaders, unleashing apostolic genius, creating ownership, empowering teams, and leading with generosity. These aren't theoretical concepts but battle-tested rhythms that Dave has used to plant churches, launch networks, and develop thousands of leaders around the world.

Whether you're a senior pastor, a team lead, or an emerging leader, the shift from being the hero to making heroes changes everything. It changes how you measure success, how you spend your time, and ultimately, how much kingdom impact you can have. Because your impact is no longer limited by what you can do personally—it's multiplied by everyone you invest in.
