Background & education

Dave holds a Bachelor of Arts in Bible and Communications from Lincoln Christian University (1986) and a Master of Arts in Educational Ministries, specializing in Church Planting, from Wheaton Graduate School (1991).

He lives in the Chicago area with his wife, Sue. They have three children: Amy, Joshua, and Caleb.

His writing — including Hero Maker, Exponential, B.L.E.S.S., Finding Your Way Back to God (co-authored with his brother Jon Ferguson), and others — has been published by Zondervan, Multnomah, and WaterBrook Multnomah.
