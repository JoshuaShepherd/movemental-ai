Who is Dave Ferguson?

Dave Ferguson

Dave Ferguson is a visionary leader, author, and church planter. He is the lead pastor of Community Christian Church, a multi-site church based in the Chicago area, and the president of the Exponential Conference — the largest church planting conference in the world.

Dave is the author of several books including Hero Maker, Keeping Score, The Big Four, Exponential, and Starting Over. His work focuses on leadership multiplication, movement building, and helping leaders escape the "Hero Trap" to become Hero Makers who develop other leaders.

Through Exponential, Community Christian Church, and his writing, Dave has helped catalyze movements of church planting and leadership development across the globe.
