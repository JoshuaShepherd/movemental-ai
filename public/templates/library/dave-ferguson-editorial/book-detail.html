Synopsis

What if the greatest thing a leader can do is not to lead more people but to develop more leaders? In Hero Maker, Dave Ferguson challenges the conventional definition of leadership success and offers a radically different vision: the hero maker.

Drawing on decades of experience building Community Christian Church and the Exponential network, Ferguson identifies five essential practices that transform leaders from heroes into hero makers — leaders who multiply leaders, who in turn multiply leaders, creating an exponential ripple effect that changes the world.

This is not a book about doing less. It is a book about doing what matters most: investing your life in others so deeply that your impact outlives you and multiplies beyond anything you could achieve alone.
