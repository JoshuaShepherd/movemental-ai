Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue through the end of your current billing period.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

We offer a 14-day free trial for the Premium plan so you can explore all features before committing. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as PayPal. Team plans can also be invoiced for annual billing.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or churches?

Yes, we offer special pricing for churches and nonprofit organizations. Please reach out through our contact page and we'll work with you to find the right plan.

What's included in the AI Lab?

The AI Lab provides an interactive AI-powered conversation experience trained on Dave Ferguson's books, teachings, and leadership principles. You can ask questions, explore concepts, and get personalized guidance for your leadership journey.
