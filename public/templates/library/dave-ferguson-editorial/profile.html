Recommendations

  1. Launch a structured YouTube strategy — Video content is the highest-leverage investment for authority building. Commit to weekly short-form content and monthly long-form teaching sessions.
  2. Implement structured data markup — Add Person, Organization, and Book schema to the website to improve search engine understanding and rich snippet visibility.
  3. Build a comprehensive content hub — Consolidate all content types (articles, podcasts, videos, book excerpts) into a single, searchable, thematically organized hub.
  4. Develop an AI-powered knowledge base — Create an interactive AI Lab trained on Dave's body of work to provide personalized guidance to leaders.
  5. Strengthen backlink profile — Pursue guest posts, podcast appearances, and co-authored content with other thought leaders to improve domain authority.
  6. Create evergreen course content — Develop self-paced courses that package Dave's frameworks into structured learning experiences.
  7. Establish a speaking page with social proof — Showcase testimonials, event photos, and a clear booking process to increase speaking engagement conversions.

Voice & Themes

Brand Voice Attributes

  • Approachable — Dave communicates complex leadership concepts in everyday language. His writing and speaking feel like a conversation with a trusted mentor.
  • Story-driven — Every principle is anchored in a real story, whether from Community Christian Church, Exponential, or his personal journey.
  • Empowering — The consistent message is about unlocking the potential in others. Dave's tone lifts people up and gives them permission to lead.
  • Practical — Ideas are always paired with actionable next steps. Dave avoids theory for theory's sake.
  • Visionary — There is a clear sense of calling and future-orientation in Dave's communication, always pointing toward what could be.
