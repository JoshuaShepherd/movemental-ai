Theme 04

Spiritual Journey

Mapping the path from skepticism to faith through five universal awakenings.

01

Overview

In Finding Your Way Back to God, Dave Ferguson maps out a spiritual journey that is both deeply personal and universally recognizable. The path from doubt to faith is not random — it follows a pattern of five awakenings that mark the turning points in every person's relationship with God.

This theme explores the landscape of spiritual formation, from the first stirring of divine discontent to the full surrender of a life given over to purpose. It offers language, frameworks, and stories that help leaders guide others through the most important journey they will ever take.

02

Key Concepts — The Five Awakenings

Awakening to Longing

"There's got to be more." The first stirring — a holy discontent that signals the beginning of the journey.

Awakening to Regret

"I wish I could start over." The honest reckoning with what has been lost and the desire for a fresh start.

Awakening to Help

"I can't do this on my own." The humility to admit need and the courage to reach out beyond yourself.

Awakening to Love

"God loves me deeply after all." The transforming encounter with grace — unconditional, unearned, overwhelming.

Awakening to Life

"Now I can live the life I was made for." The full integration of faith and purpose — the destination that becomes a new beginning.
