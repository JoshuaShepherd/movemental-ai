Theme 01

Leadership Multiplication

Moving from hero to hero maker — the art of multiplying leaders who multiply leaders.

01

Overview

At the heart of Dave Ferguson's work is a conviction that the greatest leaders are not heroes — they are hero makers. Leadership multiplication is the practice of investing in others so deeply that they, in turn, invest in others. It is a shift from addition to multiplication, from building a personal platform to building people.

This theme traces through decades of pastoral leadership at Community Christian Church, strategic work with Exponential, and a growing library of books that equip leaders to make the shift from leading to multiplying.

02

Key Concepts

The Five Practices of Hero Making

Multiplication, permission, discernment, investment, and the art of getting out of the way.

Level 5 Multiplication

A framework that measures an organization's movement from subtraction through addition and reproduction to true multiplication.

Boss vs. Leader

Understanding the critical difference between positional authority and relational influence in multiplying leadership capacity.

The Apprenticeship Model

A reproducible pathway — I do, you watch; I do, you help; you do, I help; you do, I watch — for developing leaders at every level.
