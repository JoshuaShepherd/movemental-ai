Theme 03

Missional Living

Everyday practices that bring the mission of Jesus into your neighborhood — the B.L.E.S.S. framework.

01

Overview

Missional living is the conviction that every follower of Jesus is sent on mission — not just pastors, not just overseas missionaries, but everyone. Dave Ferguson's B.L.E.S.S. framework distills this conviction into five simple, memorable, everyday practices that anyone can adopt.

B.L.E.S.S. stands for Begin with prayer, Listen, Eat, Serve, and Share your Story. These five rhythms transform ordinary neighbors into kingdom agents who carry the mission of Jesus into every corner of their daily lives — from the dinner table to the office to the sideline of a soccer game.

02

Key Concepts

B — Begin with Prayer

Praying for the people in your life who don't yet know Jesus — by name, every day.

L — Listen

Listening with the intent to understand, not to respond — creating space for real connection.

E — Eat

Sharing meals as an act of hospitality that mirrors the table fellowship of Jesus.

S — Serve

Meeting tangible needs in your neighborhood as a natural expression of the gospel.

S — Share Your Story

Telling your own story of encountering God — authentic, personal, and compelling.
