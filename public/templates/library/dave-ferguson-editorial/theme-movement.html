Theme 02

Church Planting & Movement

Catalyzing exponential growth through reproducing churches and movement-building strategies.

01

Overview

Dave Ferguson has spent over three decades on the front lines of church planting and movement building. As the founding pastor of Community Christian Church — one of the first multisite churches in America — and visionary architect behind the Exponential network, he has helped catalyze a global conversation about what it means to move from church growth to church multiplication.

This theme explores the principles, practices, and partnerships that turn a single local congregation into a reproducing movement. From the early experiments in Naperville to NewThing — a global network of reproducing churches — the story is one of relentless multiplication.

02

Key Concepts

Reproducing Churches

Moving beyond multisite to true reproduction — equipping churches to plant churches that plant churches.

The Exponential Framework

A five-level scorecard — from subtraction to multiplication — that helps organizations assess their movement potential.

NewThing Network

A relational network of reproducing churches spanning multiple continents, cultures, and denominations.

On the Verge

The idea that every church is on the verge of becoming a movement — and the practical steps to get there.
