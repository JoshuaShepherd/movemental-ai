Church Strategy

Practical frameworks for measuring what matters, simplifying the message, and leading with clarity.

01

Overview

Strategy without mission is bureaucracy. Mission without strategy is chaos. Dave Ferguson's work on church strategy sits at the intersection — providing practical, reproducible frameworks that keep organizations focused on what matters most: transformed lives and multiplied impact.

From Keeping Score to The Big Idea, these resources help church leaders simplify their communication, measure the right things, and build organizational systems that fuel — rather than hinder — movement. The goal is never complexity for its own sake, but clarity that empowers action.

02

Key Concepts

Keeping Score

A framework for measuring the metrics that actually matter — not just attendance and giving, but life-change and multiplication.

The Big Idea

Simplifying church communication around a single, powerful idea each week — creating clarity and alignment across every ministry.

Alignment Over Activity

The discipline of saying no to good things so you can say yes to the best things — reducing program clutter to increase mission focus.

Reproducible Systems

Designing organizational DNA that can be replicated — so growth does not require the original leader's constant presence.
