The Hero Maker Framework: A Visual Guide

Dave Ferguson · Leadership · 24 min

Video Player

About This Video

In this presentation, Dave Ferguson walks through the complete Hero Maker framework — the leadership paradigm shift that has helped thousands of leaders move from addition to multiplication. Recorded at the Exponential Conference, this session covers the five essential practices of a Hero Maker and provides practical next steps for implementing the framework in your organization.

Whether you lead a church, a nonprofit, a business, or a team of any size, the Hero Maker framework offers a proven pathway to multiplying your impact by investing in others. Dave shares real stories from Community Christian Church and the broader Exponential network to illustrate each principle.

Key Topics Covered

  • The Hero Trap and why it limits your impact
  • The five essential practices of a Hero Maker
  • How to shift from addition thinking to multiplication thinking
  • Permission Giving as a leadership superpower
  • Real-world examples from churches and organizations
