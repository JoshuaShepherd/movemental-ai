mDNA Assessment

Discover which elements of Missional DNA are strongest in your life and context — and which need attention. The six elements (Jesus Is Lord, Disciple Making, Missional-Incarnational, APEST, Organic Systems, and Liminality & Communitas) form the genetic code of apostolic movements. This short assessment gives you a snapshot and a starting point for formation.

Takes about 15 minutes. Answer honestly for yourself or your community.

Rate each statement

From 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 5 (Strongly Agree). There are no right answers — only your current reality.
