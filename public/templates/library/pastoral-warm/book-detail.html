Reframation

Alan Hirsch

Formation Missional Church

The church stands at a pivotal crossroads. In a rapidly changing world, old frameworks no longer serve the missional calling that lies at the heart of the gospel. Reframation is a prophetic invitation to reframe our understanding of church, leadership, and discipleship for a new era of movement.

Drawing on decades of research into apostolic movements and the DNA of the early church, Alan Hirsch challenges us to recover the theological imagination that once turned the world upside down. This is not about tweaking existing structures but about a fundamental reorientation toward the missional heart of God.

Through compelling stories, sharp theological insight, and practical frameworks, Reframation equips leaders, pastors, and everyday disciples to move beyond institutional maintenance and into the wild adventure of participating in God's mission in the world.
