About Alan

Alan Hirsch is a missional theologian, author, and thought leader. He has written widely on apostolic movement, discipleship, and the renewal of the church. His work includes The Forgotten Ways, 5Q, and Reframation, and he co-founded the 5Q Collective and Movement Leaders Collective. This platform exists to translate his work—into conversation (AL), courses, books, and your language.
