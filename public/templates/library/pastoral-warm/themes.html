Key frameworks from Alan’s work

Concepts that run through his writing, teaching, and formation resources.

mDNA

The six-element genetic code of apostolic movements: Jesus Is Lord, disciple-making, missional-incarnational impulse, APEST culture, organic systems, and communitas. Central to The Forgotten Ways.

 APEST

Apostle, Prophet, Evangelist, Shepherd, Teacher — the fivefold ministry Christ gave for the maturity and mission of his body. Explored in 5Q and The Permanent Revolution.

 Communitas

The deep bond that forms when people share a liminal experience — risk, mission, or threshold. Not comfort-first community but the togetherness that emerges from shared adventure.

 Apostolic Genius

The emergent phenomenon when all six elements of mDNA are active. The “silver imagination” that has driven every significant Jesus movement in history.

 Disciple making

The church’s primary commission: forming people who look like Jesus. Frameworks and practices from Untamed and everyday missional living.

 Christ at the center

The confession “Jesus Is Lord” as the heart of mDNA — not a slogan but a worldview that restructures life, mission, and community. Central to ReJesus and reframation.
