RATIFICATION · ACTION 01

Signing the AI Organizational Statement

As Executive Director, your signature on the Statement is the first action of ratification, committing Northridge Community Center to the ethical principles outlined below.

WHAT YOU'RE SIGNING

AI Organizational Statement of Principles

We, the undersigned, recognizing the profound impact of Artificial Intelligence on society and our operations, hereby commit to the following principles:

I. Transparency and Accountability: We commit to maintaining clear transparency regarding our use of AI systems. All decisions made or influenced by AI that significantly affect individuals or communities must be explainable and subject to human oversight. We accept full accountability for the outcomes of these systems.

II. Fairness and Equity: We pledge to actively mitigate bias in our AI implementations. We will continuously monitor our systems to ensure they do not discriminate against protected groups or exacerbate existing societal inequalities. Our AI tools will be designed and deployed to serve all constituents fairly.

III. Privacy and Security: We uphold the fundamental right to privacy. Any data collected, processed, or stored by our AI systems will be handled with the highest standards of security and in strict compliance with relevant data protection regulations. We will minimize data collection to what is strictly necessary for the intended purpose.

IV. Beneficence and Non-Maleficence: Our AI initiatives will prioritize the well-being of humanity and the environment. We will rigorously assess potential risks and harms prior to deployment, ensuring that the benefits of our technology significantly outweigh any negative consequences.

V. Continuous Monitoring and Adaptation: We recognize that AI is rapidly evolving. We commit to ongoing education, regular audits of our systems, and the willingness to adapt or discontinue AI practices that no longer align with these principles or emerging ethical standards.

YOUR SIGNATURE
