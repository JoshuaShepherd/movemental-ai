"Voice preservation works differently when your voice is still forming."

Noticed that providing older memos confused the AI because our institutional voice has shifted post-merger. We had to create a synthesized 'ideal state' document first to anchor it.

JB
James Brennan

"Process documentation is harder than it looks."

Tried feeding raw transcripts directly to the recipe. It missed the implicit steps. You still need a human to map the skeleton before the AI can flesh out the details effectively.

SK
Sarah Kim

"The Style-Checker pays for itself in the first hour."

We ran our entire Q3 communications plan through it. It caught inconsistencies in tone that our manual review completely glossed over due to fatigue.
