AI Ethics Review Invitation

You've been invited to review.

Northridge Community Center is exploring how AI might be used in our work to amplify our impact while honoring our commitments to equity and transparency. As a trusted community partner, your perspective is crucial to ensuring this technology serves our mission, rather than detracts from it.

Below are seven use cases our cohort has explored over the past three months. We ask that you review these proposed workflows and offer your unvarnished feedback. Concerns are as valuable as endorsements. We need to know where we might misstep.

Linda Ozawa, Executive Director

Development

Grant Narrative Drafting

Using a large language model to draft initial narratives for recurring foundation grants, synthesizing previous successful applications and current programmatic data. The goal is to reduce administrative burden on our writing team by 40%, allowing them to focus on personalized storytelling rather than boilerplate structuring.
