EMERGING — STRONG CONSENSUS

Voice is everything.

The cohort strongly aligns on the necessity of preserving human nuance. As the baseline of generated content rises, the premium on distinct, idiosyncratic human voice increases exponentially.

"If we sound like the machine, we are competing with the machine. We must sound inextricably like ourselves."

EMERGING — PARTIAL CONSENSUS

AI raises the floor; humans set the ceiling.

General agreement that while AI establishes a new minimum viable standard for structural and analytical work, breakthrough insights remain firmly human territory.

"The middle is hollowed out. We must either operate at the absolute foundational level of execution, or the absolute pinnacle of strategic abstraction."
