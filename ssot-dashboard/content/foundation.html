Why Movemental.ai Exists

Movemental.ai exists to solve a specific problem: movement leaders lose 85–90% of their revenue to traditional publishers and digital platforms. The platform helps movement leaders own their digital publishing platform, keep 90% of revenue (instead of 10%), and amplify their impact through AI-powered content creation and network effects.

Movemental builds complete digital publishing platforms for movement leaders—content, commerce, community, analytics—for $1,000 instead of the $50K–$150K industry standard. The site is built on: structure before copy, meaning before UI.
