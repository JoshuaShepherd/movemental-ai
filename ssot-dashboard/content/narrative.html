Narrative & Story

The story we tell and how we talk about pricing and invitation. Use for talks, sales, keynotes.

The Story We’re Telling

Where we started: “I’ve spent years watching movemental leaders get trapped in digital sharecropping. They pour their hearts into platforms that own their audience, control their content, and capture 85% of their economic value. Meanwhile, true platform ownership means $50,000–$150,000 and 6–12 months—accessible to megachurches but not to the leaders advancing God’s kingdom on the ground. That felt prophetically wrong to me.”

What we’re building: “Movemental is an invitation-only collective of movemental leaders pioneering platform ownership. We’re creating infrastructure for movements to multiply—not through rented space, but through owned, connected, AI-integrated publishing platforms that serve the leader’s calling and collaborative kingdom impact. This isn’t just about websites. It’s about changing how movements multiply.”

Why now: “Technology has caught up to the vision. AI can handle complexity that used to require six-figure budgets. We build impressive platforms in weeks, not months. The movemental community is ready—tired of sharecropping, hungry for authentic connection, prepared to invest in tools that align with their values.”

How We Talk About Pricing

Revolutionary economics: “We charge $1,000 to build your platform, then 10% of what you earn through it. We succeed when you succeed. Compare that to Substack—you keep 15%, they keep 85%—or traditional development: $75,000 upfront and hope it works.”

Scaling story: Founding members at $1,000; next cohort $2,000; later $3,000–$4,000. Network effects make each spot more valuable; early members grandfathered at their price.

Value framing: If your platform earns an extra $20K/year, you pay $2K revenue share—you keep $18K. 900% return on $1K in year one. Most members exceed that within six months.

Invitation & Vision

1,000 member cap: We’re capping at 1,000 to build infrastructure, not scale infinitely. At 1,000, the network is the definitive movemental leader ecosystem. At 100: tight-knit collective, collaboration, cross-promotion, constant conference. At 1,000: movement infrastructure—find practitioners, collaborate, content seen by hundreds of aligned audiences.
