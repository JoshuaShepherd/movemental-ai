Principles & Non-Negotiables

Guardrails for all content and decisions. We do not contradict these.

Four Non-Negotiables

1. Formation > Growth. Spiritual formation, character, relationship over metrics and virality. Success = transformation, not traffic. Prevents: vanity metrics, growth-at-all-costs, measuring by numbers alone.

2. Humans > Hacks. Technology serves human flourishing. AI amplifies, never replaces; human voice primary. Prevents: automating away judgment, replacing creativity with AI, delegating theological discernment to algorithms.

3. Scenius > Genius. Collaborative genius over isolated achievement. Mutual elevation, network effects. Prevents: empire-building, scarcity thinking, protecting IP at expense of movement.

4. Technology Properly Ordered. Tech serves mission and human flourishing. Enhances creativity and community, advances movements rather than extracting. Prevents: tech as end in itself, building because we can not because we should.

Ranked Values (Top 10)

#1 AI as amplification, not replacement. #2 Voice preservation as priority. #3 Scenius as the credibility solution. #4 Transparency and disclosure. #5 Theological integrity non-negotiable. #6 Human agency and control. #7 AI as both problem and solution. #8 Ethical AI usage. #9 Network verification over individual metrics. #10 Quality assurance and validation (theological accuracy, voice consistency).
