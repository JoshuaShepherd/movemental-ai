Site Order & Sitemap

The correct order of understanding and the complete public sitemap.

The Correct Sequence

The site should be understood in this order. The homepage is not the starting point—it assumes prior understanding.

  1. Fit Check → "Am I the right person for this?"
  2. Why Movemental (Longform) → "What problem does this solve and why does it matter?"
  3. Onboarding Path Overview → "What happens if I'm a fit?"
  4. Team/Credibility → "Who are these people and why should I trust them?"
  5. AI Book (Knowledge Spine) → "What is the foundational knowledge?"
  6. Learning Hub → "What can I learn here?"
  7. Pricing/Access → "What does it cost and what are the limits?"
  8. Homepage → "Where do I go from here?"

Primary vs Supporting Pages

Primary (foundational): Fit Check, Why Movemental, Onboarding Path. Supporting: Team, AI Book, Learning Hub, Pricing, Homepage. Navigation should guide visitors through this sequence.
