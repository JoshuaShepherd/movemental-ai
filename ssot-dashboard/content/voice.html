Platform Voice & Tone

How Movemental sounds in platform content. Leader content may follow leader-specific style guides (e.g. Alan Hirsch).

Movemental Sounds Like

Formation > growth → We don’t hype vanity metrics or traffic; we talk about transformation and impact.
Humans > hacks → We don’t promise “AI does it all”; we promise amplification and voice preservation.
Scenius > genius → We emphasize collaboration, network, collective credibility, not lone thought-leader branding.
Technology properly ordered → We describe tech as serving mission and movement, not as the product.

Conversational, clear, descriptive. No jargon (synergy, leverage, ecosystem as filler). Lead with what we do and who it’s for; why follows. One-liner and value props are the anchor.

We Avoid

Generic marketing fluff; algorithm-first or growth-at-all-costs language; implying AI replaces human insight or theological depth; individual empire-building over network; overused abstractions (solution, platform as buzzword).

Leader-Specific Voice

Content attributed to or generated for a specific leader (e.g. Alan Hirsch) follows that leader’s voice spec. See _docs/themes-content/voice/ (e.g. ALAN_HIRSCH_VOICE_FIDELITY_SPECIFICATION.md, VOICE_STYLE_GUIDE in content-chain). Platform marketing and product copy use the platform voice above.
