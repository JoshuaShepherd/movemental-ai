The arc

How the weeks run

Three stages move you from orientation through deep practice to integration — each week building on the last.

1

Orient

Weeks 1–2 establish shared vocabulary, surface assumptions, and set the trajectory for the cohort.

2

Deep work

Weeks 3–6 are the engine — guided readings, field experiments, peer dialogue, and facilitator check-ins.

3

Integrate

Weeks 7–8 synthesize learning into a personal action plan and a peer accountability structure.
