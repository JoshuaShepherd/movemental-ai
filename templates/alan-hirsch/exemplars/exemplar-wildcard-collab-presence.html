Collaboration Presence

A compact multiplayer-adjacent strip showing who is currently viewing a shared artifact. Four avatar circles stack with a negative margin overlap, an overflow pill shows additional viewers, and a ghost "Follow" button invites the user to track edits.

SM JT PK DR +2
3 people viewing

Shared artifact canvas. In production this would render the document, slide deck, or workspace that collaborators are viewing together.

This is a static pattern. No WebSocket connections, Liveblocks SDK, or network calls are involved. The avatars use CSS initials on var(--elevated) circles with var(--muted-foreground) text, meeting WCAG AA contrast requirements. The online indicator dot uses var(--primary) at 9px — small enough that blue remains an accent rather than a dominant element. The avatar stack overlaps with -8px margin-left and a 2px solid var(--card) border to maintain visual separation.

On narrow viewports (below 480px), the presence bar stacks vertically: avatars sit above the label and follow button, preserving readability without horizontal scrolling.
